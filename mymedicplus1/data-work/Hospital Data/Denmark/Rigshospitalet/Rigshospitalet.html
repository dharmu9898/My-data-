Description:
Rigshospitalet has high research activity. This means that we are constantly developing new and improved treatment methods. We are a university hospital and are involved in many international research and development projects. At the same time, the hospital is a popular choice for thousands of students when deciding where to train in the health area. Our goal is to be the preferred choice for all patients with a need for highly specialised services. We are sure your treatment at Rigshospitalet will be successful.

Highlights
We believe that care, treatment, research and development are all important factors in a safe and successful hospital visit. Our goal is to provide you with personal and professional treatment. When admitted to the hospital or when visiting one of the hospital's out-patient clinics, a contact person is assigned to each patient, and this person will be responsible for the entire course of treatment. We consider it our responsibility to make sure that your time in hospital is as well-organised as possible - also if you are being treated in several different departments.
