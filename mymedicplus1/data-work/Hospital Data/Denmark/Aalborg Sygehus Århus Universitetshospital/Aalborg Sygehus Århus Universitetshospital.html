Description:

Aalborg Hospital is the largest hospital in the North Denmark Region. It is also northern Jutland’s largest employer, with approximately 5,000 employees.

The hospital consists of Section South and North in Aalborg and Dronninglund Hospital. Surgical Department A in Hobro, the Eye, Nose and Throat Department and the Ophthalmology Department in Hjørring, the clinic-chemical functions in Hobro and Farsø and the dialysis section in Hjøring are also part of Aalborg Hospital.
