A dental services provider offering a range of treatments and procedures including root canal, fillings, crown and bridge work, and implants.

Körmendy Dent offers a range of dental services covering tartar removal, fillings, root canal treatments, crown and bridge work, prostheses, gnathological treatments, dental jewels, implants, laser therapies and other dental surgical operations and procedures. Körmendy Dent is accredited by the Semmelweis University in Budapest.

Körmendy Dent is situated in the city of Sopron, an area near a known tourist attraction – the Ferto Lake. Sopron is also conveniently located near the border bounding Hungary and Austria. The location is also relatively close from Vienna and Bratislava.
