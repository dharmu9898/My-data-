Cosmetic Dentistry Abroad - BHMS specialises strictly in treating foreign patients, more specifically patients from the UK and Ireland. It is very important for clients who are looking for dental treatment abroad to ask questions before go overseas for treatment and the following questions should be asked:

How long have you been treating foreign patients?
Our cosmetic dentistry is a long established surgery and have been treating UK patients since 2006, so you are in professional hands, with people who understand your needs and know how to offer the very best service available. Our dentists in Budapest are registered with the General Dental Council, the organisation which regulates dental professionals in the UK.

How experienced are your dentists in Budapest?
Our dentists have many, many years of combined experience and regularly attend courses to ensure that they learn the newest techniques and are continually improving themselves. Both oral surgeons , Dr. Batorfi and Dr. Halasz are extremely professional and highly skilled do nothing other than implant surgery and bone grafting procedures, hence having vast experience in this field of expertise. It must be noted that many new dentistries appearing on the internet do not have the vast experience that is offered at BHMS.

Please contact your UK rep immediately. There may be several solutions to a problem, however, if you need to see a dentist, you can either return to Budapest where all treatment will be carried our free of charge or if you prefer to see a dentist in London, then a one-off consultation fee of £40 is charged.

I have come from a position where I had teeth knocked out as a teenager through sport, then gone through the following 15 years with crowns and them a plate when they failed. I found this to be thoroughly unpleasant and demoralising-there was not a day, for whatever number of years it was, that I did not hate wearing my false teeth! If you are reading this you probably know exactly what I mean-I really, really detested it!

I had read more and more about implants and had moved dentist, from my original NHS dentist to a private dentist in the UK. My reason behind this was that I knew one of the dentists there was branching out to specialise in implants. I was very impressed by the difference in private V's NHS in the UK (but was later to be amazed at how much better private was in Budapest!!) I had visited the dentist in the UK who specialised in implants and was flabbergasted when I was quoted £2000 for the ONE implant, as well as another bill for £2000 that was essential (According to him) for a bone graft into the jaw as well-so I was looking at £4000 for the one tooth to be replaced.

Shocked by this I was encouraged by a BBC One show report on dental tourism to look into dentistry abroad. I googled a few and contatced three outlining my situation. One did not bother to reply even after I contacted them twice, but two did. Another contatced me with a girl whose English was limited, and the third, British Hungarian Medical Service (BHMS) contatced me via Agnes Tuba, their UK representative. Her english was perfect and she was always exceptionally helpful throughout-and indeed was always contactable by phone or email-this was a great source of reassurance.
I eventually ploughed through reviews and tripadvisor reports etc and gathered up the guts to commit to treatment......(remember I was going to get one implant-though had space for four)
I made the arrangements for my first visit-which was very daunting-I couldn't locate Budapest on a map or tell you anything about it!
The practice kept in touch during the days before departure and the safety of someone to collect you at the airport is a godsend! I had my initial consultation with Dr. Balázs Gyovai who was the dentist I then saw most of during my visits. I had previously requested that Dr Batorfi do the implants as he has the best reputation from everything I had read. On consultation with Dr Gyovai we discussed the opportunities in my mouth and I committed to getting all four gaps filled!!!!!........and the real dealbreaker was the fact that the implants were coming in around £900 each..............and no bone graft needed............for FOUR implants!! (April 2008 prices)
It took three trips to complete the treatments, but every one was completed to a very high standard-the fitting of the implants was the most frightening part, but I am delighted to report-as someone who has had a host of dental treatments, it really was nothing too awful.......afterwards it isn't very pleasant-like getting a tooth pulled, some swelling and mild discomfort, and the insertion of a temporary plate- but on reflection it is entirely worth it!
Three months later I returned to be fitted for the teeth to attach to the implants, and then a week later for the final fitting. Dr Tunde Toth completed the fitting process, again with Dr Gyovai always available and providing continuity across the service. It has taken me a while to get used to having teeth again-without any doubt my four implants are now the strongest teeth in my mouth!! They are slightly bigger on the inside of the mouth than your natural teeth-due to the construction on the inside, but less than a year later I wouldn't notice it at all.
I would strongly encourage anyone who is reading this and wavering over the thought of it to take the plunge-there has not been a moment where I have regretted it-perhaps my only regret is that I did not get more done!!
The prospect of travelling to Budapest is slightly daunting, English is not very prominent, but the clinic-and in particular Bianca and Katalin at the desks are extremely helpful and friendly and will set you at ease. Budapest is a very busy city, but the clinic sits at the outskirts of the city in a very pleasant residential area-it is just a run of the mill house converted into a great clinic. The clinic will always collect you and leave you back to you accommodation. I had booked my own hotels, but was very impressed by reports of other patients in the waiting area who had used the clinics recommmended apartments.
I am just about to book my visit for April/May next year for a check up and have raved to anyone who has asked me about the service provided and especially the outcome!!!"
