"I had treatment just in time”.

My name is Andrew, I am 47 years old and I have always had problem teeth. I was not blessed with the whitest teeth and I was very prone to cavities as a child. These were filled with amalgam which I had carried with me all my life – it had become unsightly and damaged and I was conscious of it when I laughed and smiled.

Even more significantly my gums were causing me problems – they were bleeding a lot when I brushed my teeth and some of my closest friends had been brave enough to tell me that my breath was not always too sweet.

I had had two teeth extracted and the gaps had been simply left. I had never received any advice on what the options were following the extractions.

I knew I had neglected my teeth and I was not a big fan of visiting the dentist, I needed to do something – I take care of myself in other ways – regular exercise, healthy diet etc.

I was lucky enough to meet Dr Horvath and to learn about his dental practice and dental standards in Hungary. He examined me and without hesitation I accepted his offer of treatment - I was mortified to learn that my gums and teeth were in such bad shape.

I had extensive treatment with him and his team over a five day period. All the staff were very kind and helpful and they went to extreme lengths to avoid causing me any pain.

I cannot praise them highly enough – most importantly my gums no longer bleed and my teeth are clean and bright with no unsightly amalgam. My personal desire was not the ultra-bright “Hollywood” smile but a healthy and clean set of teeth and gums.

My gum problem was so severe that two teeth had to be removed as a result of periodontitis (erosion of the jaw bone caused by deep infection in the pocket between the gum and the tooth) – I am returning to Hungary later this year for implant surgery which will complete my treatment.

I have saved thousands of pounds but, of equal importance, I was treated with respect in an unhurried yet professional manner. The work I had done would have involved numerous visits in the UK, countless interruptions to my work schedule and time off.

I have traveled extensively and lived overseas for much of my life – this was my best dental experience by far.
