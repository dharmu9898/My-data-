About Us and Our Clinic
Oswald Andra-Dent’s surgeries have welcomed patients from all over Europe since 1990 and are continuously modernised to meet current standards and practices of dentistry.

In bright and friendly surgeries, modern equipment such as computerized and panoramic x-ray machines and state-of-theart dentist’s chairs help create a relaxing atmosphere wherepatients are not anxious. During a thorough preliminary consultation with one of our English-speaking dentists, the patient receives and discusses all the information about the available treatment options and the costs that would be involved.

Collaborating with 25 qualified dental technicians, we use top quality materials: high-carat-gold, remanium and ceramic. We create shatter-proof prosthetics that are worn with total comfort and ease.

Following their scholastic education, all the dentists in this surgery have absolved a 5-year degree course at the Hungarian University and then completed their training by assisting a dentist for 3 years. All our dentists hold a doctorate.

A member of our staff is a jaw surgeon, who is responsible for dental implantology and any other operations on the jaw such as building up the bone. Having successfully undertaken more than 1000 implants, he is considered to be at the forefront of his field in Hungary. In addition to the excellent and friendly dental treatment Oswald Andra-Dent practices provide using the English language, they also ensure that your leisure time in Hungary becomes an unforgettable experience.

Agi, our tourist guide, spares no effort to ensure the well-being of our guests whilst in Hungary. She organizes visits to Budapest, to monasteries and museums as well as beautiful walks in the countryside and boat trips in the natural and romantic Raba region. You can enjoy our fountain of youth, the iodine-rich thermal spa, either together with other group members or on your own. Or you may wish to book a foot or a body massage, or indulge in treatment using a variety of mud packs.
