Description:

Access-Smile are based in Loughborough in the East Midlands but run their own state-of-the-art dental clinic in Budapest, Hungary, which is only 2 hours away and easily accessible by low-cost flights.

Cosmetic dentistry is changing people's lives for the better, giving them not only a healthy mouth and a bright smile, but perhaps more significantly, a renewed confidence. Our dentists are deeply proud of their own particular expertise and the entire team - receptionists, nurses and dentists - speak English, making communication simple and easy.

With our knowledge of the Hungarian market we can offer customers first class dental treatment at a fraction of the local cost and help you with booking flights and accommodation and personal adviser while you were in Budapest.

We're dedicated to providing outstanding dental treatment in a relaxed and caring atmosphere. And, with all our treatments backed by our full 'no quibble' guarantee, everyone is assured of a relaxed experience with us.
