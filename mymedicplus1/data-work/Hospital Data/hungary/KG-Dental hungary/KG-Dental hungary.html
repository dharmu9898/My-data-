Description:

A trio of dental clinics operating in different parts of Hungary, offering dental and holiday packages complete from dental services to travel and lodging

KG Dental is a group of dental clinics operating in three branches in different parts of Hungary. KG Dental specializes in providing dental treatments that go with holiday packages they offer as well. The packages include everything the patient needs for dental services and treatments, as well as travel and accommodation concerns. KG can as well arrange for leisure activities for the patient has he/she required.

Dental treatments offered by KG Dental are available for both local and foreign patients. The clinics’ foreign clients mostly come from countries in Western Europe and the United States.
KG Dental clinics can be located in Budapest, the country’s capital; Hévíz, a town boasting a thermal lake; and in Mosonmagyaróvár, a place where dental tourism has been flourishing for quite awhile now. All clinics of KG employ the modern technologies in the field of dentistry, from equipment to materials. All clinics also have their own laboratories

Healthy teeth are available for everybody

KG Dental Ltd that operates in Hungary provides dental treatments of high level for foreign and Hungarian patients at reasonable prices. Foreign visitors mostly come from Western Europe and America. The company’s secret lies in the professional commitment of our colleagues, and the high level of their qualification guarantees that our patients will get the best quality service. This philosophy makes it possible that our company has been staying at our patients’ disposal trustworthily for 17 years. Our clinics are situated in 3 regions of Hungary that are preferred by tourists the most:

  • In Budapest, the capital
  • In Hévíz, a town with a thermal lake near Lake Balaton
  • In Mosonmagyaróvár, near the border of Austria

Guaranteed the best care and quality services

KG Dental offers and guarantees the best care and quality services based on strict regulations and constant inspection to ensure all requests are fulfilled and to provide visitors valuable and pleasant time during their medical treatment and holiday.

Dental LaboratoryWe receive our patients in our high-tech clinics which have laboratories on site furnished with the best and the most modern dental equipment.

The tight but flexible working relationship between dentist and dental technician makes possible the fast procedure of dentures using quality-controlled materials and producing excellent quality work.
Due to the very modern laboratory technology applied in our practices we are able to make all kinds of dentures including the most up-to-date zirconium-oxide crown.
All the materials and equipment used by our laboratories are purchased from german and swiss companies.

Guarantee

KG Dental Ltd. does not forget its patients after the end of their treatment and their departure. Our quality assurance covers a full range of guarantee provides our patients with security at control repeated treatments.

We give guarantee of 2 to 5 years for all prostheses made by us depend on their types. The precondition of the guarantee is the careful oral hygiene and the annual control in the course of that we have the possibility to revise the changes and anomalies in the oral cavity.

Naturally, the guarantee does not cover the problems occurring because of changes in the oral cavity: e.g. loosening row of teeth because of paradentitis and atrophy, treatment of teeth inflamed under the prosthesis, periodical change of precy and ot-cap inlays.

In the event of failure in the course of inserting implants we return the cost of the implantation.

A beautiful smile makes you self-confident

As a first step, we prepare a tailor-made treatment plan and a quotation for our patients in which we pay attention to the requests of our patient. This service is free of charge, and does not impose any obligation. We start the treatment only after the patient has accepted and understood our offer.

During the treatment you can experience the calm and welcoming atmosphere, positive feeling and care. It is very important for us not to cause pain and protect the patient’s body from the least harm.

We only work with Swiss and German materials and instruments of high quality which satisfy the strictest EU standards. The equipment of our surgeries belongs to the most up-to-date. The handling room is suitable for carrying out sterile operations. The patients are looked after by eight highly qualified doctors, whose qualification covers the whole spectrum of dental treatments. We are well versed in every field from the simplest filling to the most complicated implants, from dental surgery to orthodontics.

Our clinics have the lead in the field of implant treatments

Our speciality is to place in implants and fix instable prostheses with implants. We hold vocational trainings for dentists. We take part in related research and development of dental implants. Our latest project is the implant with zirconium oxide base which belongs to the forefront of the world and was developed with the Swiss METOXIT company together.

We have been cooperating successfully with the firm Sanatmetal for years in developing and selling implants with titanium base.
