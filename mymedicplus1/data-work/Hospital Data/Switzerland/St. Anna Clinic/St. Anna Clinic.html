Description:

St Anne Clinic operates under the philosophy of providing professional, personalised, comprehensive, cost-effective care to all patients while maintaining and promoting their health and well-being. This facility which is certified by the Department of Health to provide day surgery and outpatient services offers a friendly, personal yet efficient modern service within a welcoming and re-assuring environment.
