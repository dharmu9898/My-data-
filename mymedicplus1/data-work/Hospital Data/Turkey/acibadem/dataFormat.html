Acibadem Healthcare Group  

Description:

Turkey's leading healthcare institution, Acıbadem, operates with over 6.500 employees in 21 different location through a network of 6 general hospitals, medical centers, outpatient clinics, 1 ophthalmology center and laboratories. Besides its partnership with one of the most important hospitals of Istanbul International Hospital and International Etiler Outpatient Clinic, Acıbadem expans its "healthcare chain" continuously outside of Istanbul with Adana,Kayseri,Eskişehir and Bodrum Hospitals and in Istanbul with Maslak and Beşiktaş (Fulya) Hospitals.

Public Offering
Acıbadem made a public offering of its shares in 2000 and with its proficiency, turned to new investments for the increase of its capacity. Today Acıbadem is the only healthcare institution whose shares are traded on the Istanbul Stock Exchange (IMKB).

New Investments
Growing rapidly, 3 new center will be added to the Group in the very near future.
·  Acıbadem Beşiktaş (Fulya) Hospital
·  Acıbadem Bodrum Hospital
·  Acıbadem Eskişehir Hospital


Healthcare Personnel
Acıbadem offer services to the public in all medical branches with the most advanced facilities of modern medical technology and a healthcare army of over 6.500 personnel, of which 1001 are physicians.

Advanced Technology and Information Systems Network
While providing leadership in the field of new knowledge and procedures of medical science, and following the latest developments of medical technology at close range, Acıbadem maintains an effective communications network between the ''Hospital Information Communication System'' in which all the medical records of patients are stored and Acıbadem Hospitals. Within or between the hospitals, Acibadem employs an advanced Visual Transfer System.
