Description:

For a better solution of healthcare problems experienced both in our country and worldwide, the mindset of foundation is of great importance.

Only with such mindset, it would be possible to eliminate an incomplete and lacking service and a commercial way of thinking depending on the limited government allowance.

Established on October 30, 2000 under the light of these facts, Hizmet Hospital is a healthcare institution established under "serving comes first" motto of the Turkish Kidney Foundation, upholding human services above anything.

Offering the best possible service to Turkish people with its emergency, outpatient clinics and laboratories is the core principle of Hizmet Hospital together with its employees.

Besides its advanced transplantation unit, Hizmet Hospital proves out to be a unique hospital with the cutting-edge modern equipment in all other medical departments and doctors experienced in their own fields.

Besides the ability of any kind of emergency response with its clinics and outpatient clinics, diagnostic laboratories, imaging center, intensive care unit, nuclear medical center, operating rooms, reanimation (intensive care) unit, dedicated floor for obstetric department and delivery room, baby friendly hospital environment and inpatient treatment services, Hizmet Hospital has been following all recent inventions and technologies of the 21st century and provides world-class healthcare services with its expert team.
