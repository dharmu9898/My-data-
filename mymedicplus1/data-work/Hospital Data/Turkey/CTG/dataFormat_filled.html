Description:

CTG HEALTHCARE is one of the most well-known Medical Groups in Turkey, whether you require a breast enlargement, face lift, liposuction or cosmetic dentistry . We offer high quality, affordable Medical Services in Turkey and have served a large quantity of patients from both Turkey and abroad. Certified plastic surgeons & dentists with very good reputation, as well as the experienced staff are all fluent in English. All procedures are performed in the technologically advanced operating theaters of our Hospital and Dental Clinic located in Izmir, Turkey.

Accommodation and travel services are provided by CTG HEALTHCARE's own travel department, group A travel agency which is specialized on Medical Tourism. CTG HEALTCARE travel department can provide you flights to Izmir and accommodation , a personal welcome at Izmir International Airport and transfer to your hotel, Full 24-hour support from our dedicated Medical Tour Experts, private transfer to and from your treatment, a complete package of relaxing recuperation options from spa treatments to city tours including nurse support if necessary.

In the beautiful city of Izmir, it is possible to enjoy both historical sites such as Ephesus, Pergamon, and nice beaches such as Çesme, Kusadasi. Maybe a nice ferry tour on Izmir bay, a nice yatch tour for a few days on the Aegean sea or visiting some big shopping malls might be an unforgettable experience for you. Besides, the hospital and the clinic are situated in a very central neighborhood of the city, which makes it practical to explore the beautiful city of Izmir by yourself.

With our experience of treating hundreds of patients, we make the process of travelling for overseas surgery simple and easy taking the stress out of booking and being there when it matters most.
