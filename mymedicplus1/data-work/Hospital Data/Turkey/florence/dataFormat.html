Group Florence Nightingale and Istanbul Bilim University Cooperation
Istanbul Bilim University, which was founded by the Group Florence Nightingale to train its own infrastructure and personnel, incorporates the  following departments:

  • Faculty of  Medicine
  • Faculty of Arts and Sciences - Psychology Department
  • Institute of Health Sciences
  • Clinical Research Center
  • School of Nursing
  • School of Health Care Technicians
    (Anaesthesia, Biochemistry, Nutrition and Dietetics and etc.)

Students and physicians studying  at Istanbul Bilim University and students abroad benefit from international physician-nurse training programs.

Florence Nightingale Hospitals serve as the teaching hospital for Bilim University, Medical Faculty, where a major portion of our physicians teach and lecture as faculty members.

Testimonials:

Letter from Cambridge after visiting and participating to Liver Transplant Program at Florence Nightingale Hospital.

UNIVERSITY OF CAMBRIDGE
Department of Surgery
27 June 2008
 
The care we saw at all levels was exceptional we were impressed by the radiological assessment, care of patient selection and the standards of anesthesia and surgery. We observed a wide variety of useful techniques and aspects of care in particular relation to the living liver transplantation and anesthesia.
 
A major strength of the unit is the closely-knit nature of the team and the way they work together. The close working relationship between the donor and recipient surgical teams is obviously essential to the process but we were also very impressed by the excellent relationship with the other team members in all aspects of care.

This was a very valuable visit for us allowing us to gain enormously from the substantial experience of an outstanding unit demonstrating high standards excellent outcomes and a quite remarkable volume at work. We look forward to further collaborations as we establish our own live donor program here in Cambridge.

 

To Group Florence Nightingale Hospital in Turkey
 
I was referred to your hospital with intestinal problems. Before I had 4 surgeries and unfortunately could not gain my health. Through the chief advisor to government Dr. Shaip Muja's kind interest I was referred to your hospital. With your help, I came to Istanbul by an ambulance plane. I was really hopeless.
 
When I arrived to Istanbul to your Sisli Florence Nightingale Hospital at almost midnight, I was fully escorted and my room and a team of doctors was ready to examine me.
 
That night they gave me medicine and prepared me for a surgery dated for the next morning. On 15th December I was operated under the leadership of Prof. Dr. Yildiray Yuzer and his surgical team for 4 and a half hours. After that I was under control of Prof. Dr. Osman Bayindir and his team at ICU. I was expected to stay at ICU for 5 days, but, fortunately I was sent to the 3rd floor to my private room after stayin in ICU for only one day. On third day after the surgery I started to eat and walk and every passing day I started to feel better. On the 17th day after surgery I felt more better.
 
I stayed at hospital for 20 days and I noticed that everyday I was progressing in a very healthy and rapid way.
I personally want to thank the president of the hospital Prof. Dr. Cemsid Demiroglu for giving me the chance to be operated in Florence Nightingale Hospital. I would like to thank my head surgeon Prof. Dr.Yildiray Yuzer, surgeon Dr. Murat Dayangac, head of anesthesy team and ICU Prof. Dr. Osman Bayindir, all my nurses and all teams that were interested in my illness. I also would like to thank Medical Directory and International Patient Department.
 
Now I am in Ipek-Kosovo, I am thankful for the miracle that became true at Florence Nightingale Hospital.
