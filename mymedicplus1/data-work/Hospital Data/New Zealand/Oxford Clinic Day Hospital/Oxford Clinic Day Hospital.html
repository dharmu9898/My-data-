Description:

Oxford Clinic was established in 1996 by specialist surgeons committed to quality healthcare creating a centre of excellence in healthcare provision.

Oxford Clinic is a medical and surgical centre of excellence where some of the best gynaecological and gastro-intestinal specialists in Australasia work as a team to provide consultation, diagnosis and treatment, as well as minimally invasive surgery where appropriate.

Our specialist fields include digestive tract (stomach, bowel and colon) as well as bladder, reproductive organs and pelvic floor.

In 2004 a joint venture was established with Southern Cross Hospitals to further develop the hospital business, combining the experience and expertise of both parties to offer a wide range of innovative procedures.

Our surgical focus is on minimally invasive surgery, or key-hole surgery and our specialists have remained at the forefront in their particular areas of expertise of this highly specialised technique. Their ongoing involvement in research and teaching has also ensured that they remain at the leading edge in their field.

Oxford Clinic is a centre of excellence with 3 complementary businesses:

  • Oxford Clinic Hospital – a short stay surgical facility where procedures are performed for the diagnosis and treatment of gastrointestinal, colorectal and gynaecological conditions with a focus on endoscopic surgery.
  • Oxford Clinic Women's Health – consultants specialising in reproductive, fetal medicine and gynaecological services.
  • Oxford Clinic Intus – consultants specialising in the diagnosis and treatment of gastrointestinal and colorectal disorders
