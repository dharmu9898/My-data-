Description:

Gillies Hospital is a private surgical hospital that is a joint venture between Surgical Group Ltd (a group of specialist surgeons) and Southern Cross Hospitals Ltd. There has been a private hospital on this site, 160 Gillies Ave Epsom, since 1943.

The hospital has been designed for a wide range of specialist surgery with a focus on short stay procedures.
