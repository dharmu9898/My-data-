Description:
Wellington Regional Medical Center is located in Wellington, Florida. It is a 143-bed, acute care hospital, owned by a subsidiary of Universal Health Services, Inc., a highly respected, healthcare management organization. Wellington Regional is proud to have provided fine healthcare services to Palm Beach County since 1986. As a community hospital, accredited by The Joint Commission, Wellington Regional prides itself on its commitment to meet the changing needs of patients, physicians, payors, staff and the community at large.
