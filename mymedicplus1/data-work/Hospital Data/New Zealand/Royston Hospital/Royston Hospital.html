Description:

Set in attractive grounds and close to local amenities, Royston Hospital in Hastings is a member of the Wakefield Health Group and has had a history of providing healthcare to the residents of Hawkes Bay since 1921.

Supported by the very latest technology, equipment and facilities Royston Hospital provides the most comprehensive range of private surgical facilities in the Hawke’s Bay area. The treatment and care you receive will be of the highest standard and our staff will do their best to help you return to good health as quickly and as comfortably as possible.

At Royston, we are committed to providing high quality care having regard for your individual requirements. You will experience particular attention from our staff aimed to ensure you enjoy a superior level of care, comfort and convenience.

Our nursing staff are experienced health professionals committed to providing care for patients and their families in a compassionate, personal manner. They appreciate that a stay in hospital is often a major event in their patients' lives. That is why they make a special effort to get to know the people under their care and attend personally to individual patient requirements.

We also offer a warm welcome to all visitors and invite those considering private medical and surgical treatment to meet with staff and view the hospital facilities.

The Royston Centre, adjoining the hospital, provides specialist consulting rooms as well as physiotherapy and radiology facilities (including private MRI and CT).

This is why Royston is the choice for so many Hawke's Bay people when it comes to specialist surgical treatment or medical care.

Highlights

In January 2006, Royston Hospital merged with Wakefield Health Limited and it is now a member of the Wakefield Health group of hospitals (including Wakefield and Bowen Hospitals in Wellington).

Royston continues to advance and serve the people of Hawke's Bay with specialist services, state-of-the-art facilities and with staff who are always striving to achieve the highest possible level of patient care.
