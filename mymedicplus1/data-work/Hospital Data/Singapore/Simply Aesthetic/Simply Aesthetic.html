Description:

Simply Aesthetic combines Medical Aesthetic treatments with modern technology to provide minimally-invasive solutions to your aesthetic needs.The team consists of Dr Christine Cheng, Aesthetic Therapists Mery and Joey, together with Clinic Assistants Dyna and Charizma.

Rejuvenation:

To sustain or restore a naturally youthful look.

We aim to use synergistic treatments to gently stimulate the skin into a healthy, glowing, more youthful state. From enhancing cellular metabolism to improving cellular nutrition, our holistic rejuvenation treatments complement and enhance the usual therapeutic measures that stimulate collagen production alone.

Contouring:

Balance face and body shape by simple sculpting treatments.

Our comprehensive slimming and body contouring programs give our clients a choice of treatments for fat elimination, both non-invasive and surgical.We are the only clinic in Singapore to offer 5th Generation Water-Jet PLUS Liposuction, the Korean modification of the German WAL technique with enhanced results, minus the prolonged downtime associated with other forms of liposuction.
