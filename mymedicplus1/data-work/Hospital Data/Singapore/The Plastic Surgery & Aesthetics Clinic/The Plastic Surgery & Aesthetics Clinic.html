Description:

Plastic surgery can be broadly divided into aesthetic and reconstructive surgery. Reconstructive surgery addresses deformities due to trauma, birth defects and illnesses. Aesthetic or cosmetic surgery shapes and moulds the face and body to improve appearance.

At The Plastic Surgery & Aesthetic Clinic, we offer cosmetic plastic surgery of the face and body, and non-surgical facial rejuvenation & body sculpturing. We also offer reconstructive plastic surgery.

The Clinic is located within the Pacific Healthcare Specialist Centre on the 19th floor of the Paragon on Orchard Road. We are equipped with our own laser rooms and procedure rooms for minor surgery. Surgery under general anesthesia can be performed at our surgical facility within the same building, and we have access to the facilities and wards of all the major private hospitals, including the conveniently located Mt Elizabeth and Gleneagles hospitals.

We believe that each patient has different goals and ideals, and our aim is to work with you to achieve yours. Our philosophy is to provide personalized and individualized care for our patients.
