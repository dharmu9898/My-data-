About Us


Angels Abroad is aware of fears and doubts people feel when making such kind of trip; and that is why we intend to offer you the necessary information to make you feel more secure.

We are active members of the Medical Tourism Association.

What we will do for you
We will help you to plan the whole medical trip, giving you information about doctors, medical treatments, clinic facilities, hotels, Guatemala, and so forth. When you arrive to the country, we will provide you a concierge, transport, a cell phone to contact doctor, your concierge and family and friends. We will take you to each appointment and we will be alert to your needs.

If you decide to take a tour we will contact trustful tour operators.

Just 4 steps to improve your health!

1. Sign up. Send us your contact information and we will contact you soon. Fill out a medical intake form and send us your medical exams.
2. Meet the doctor. You can choose the doctor of your preference on our website. We will send the doctor all your information and arrange a phone call so you can ask him about doubts and concerns.
3. Get a quote. If you feel comfortable with the doctor, then we will send to you a list of our hotels and tourism packages. When you make a decision, we will be ready to send you the more approximate price.
4. Come to Guatemala!!
