Description:

Clínica Santa María is a highly specialized center for the diagnosis and treatment of couples with fertility problems. It came about as a result of a need in the region for a center specialized in human reproductive techniques.

Clinica Santa Maria,It was created in 1998 by its current director Dr. Juan Francisco Solís Bercián. Since then, the miracle of life has become a reality for many couples with difficulties conceiving.

Situated where the city’s main roads converge, in one of the most exceptional locations in Guatemala City.

Edificio Multimédica is located in Vista Hermosa, a very nice city area, where under one roof there are more than 200 physicians, specialized in all branches of medicine, a hospital and diagnostic centers.

Clìnica Santa María has its own facilities and highly specialized equipment to provide the latest treatments in human reproduction, with international quality standards
