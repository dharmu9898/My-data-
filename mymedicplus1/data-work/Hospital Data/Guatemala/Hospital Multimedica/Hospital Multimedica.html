Description:

The Hospital Multimédica is the most modern outpatient surgery center of attention and innovation in Guatemala; it is situated in the prestigious complex Multimédica, which has the most outstanding group of doctors in medicine and health. This hospital is equipped with the most advanced medical technology, a highly functional organization and a design that exceeds the international standards for this type of installations.

All these facilities were created so that the patient can feel supported professionally, and also receive a quality attention. The Hospital Multimédica, besides being a surgery center of excellence has the support of Tecniscan and the Multimédica Laboratory to provide good attention to the patient in services of diagnosis as magnetic resonance, helical computerized tomography, X rays, ultrasound with doppler, mammography of high resolution, cardiology and computerized clinical laboratory.

OUR VISION

Hospital Multimédica will be distinguished for its leadership in ambulatory medical procedures and excellent service. It will be recognized for its highly qualified personnel, its quality, innovation and technology in health services.

OUR MISSION

To offer a secure, efficient, responsible, cordial and human service to our patients, offering the best service and treatment in a safe environment. Our compromise is to our patients and our services are oriented in satisfying their needs.
