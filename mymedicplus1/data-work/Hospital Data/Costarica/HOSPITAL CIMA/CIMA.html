Description:

Consorcio Hospital Internacional, S.A de C.V. (CHI) was incorporated in 1992 with the vision of establishing, in Mexico as well as in Latin America, a premier private medical services provider. Consorcio Internacional Hospital is the Mexican affiliate of International Hospital Corporation, company headquartered in Dallas, Texas. This consortium invests in, operates and markets hospitals, who work under the name of Centro Internacional de Medicina (CIMA). CHI also operated hospitals in Brazil under the VITA name.
CIMA’s main objective is to have specialized hospitals. All CIMA hospitals include departments like emergency, surgery, internal medicine, diagnostic areas, pediatrics and ob-gyn; they also offer intensive care for adults, children including newborns, and high-complexity specialties such as cardiology and neurology. Some centers also offer preventative health care, physical rehabilitation premises and medical practice office buildings.
CIMA hospitals offer their services at:

• CIMA Hermosillo
• CIMA Chihuahua
• CIMA San José de Costa Rica
• CIMA Santa Engracia
There will soon be another CIMA in Puebla. CIH provides the highest quality in private medical care to Latin and South America. So, now and in the future, CIMA and VITA will continue to represent the pinnacle of private medical care.

Medical Travel describes a rapidly growing practice of traveling to another country to obtain medical services.
Since the exponential growth of travel for healthcare, our hospitals have found the need to aid International Patients by being directly involved with the patient’s personal needs before, during and after they receive care in the CIMA and VITA hospitals.
Although there are many healthcare providers trying to capitalize on this growing trend, most only provide local hospital services. Few can provide personalized service packages that include travel, hotel accommodations, medical care and continuity of care.
Medical Value Travel (MVT) describes the unique service of providing international patients a world class resource for quality safe medical services at accredited private acute care hospitals managed by U.S. based company for a single, affordable price. International Hospital Corporation has recently created a Medical Value Travel Department in all of their Latin American Hospitals. The MVT Department will provide international patient services focusing on patient-centric services.

International Hospital Corporation is a Texas company that owns and operates high-quality healthcare facilities throughout Latin America. With a U.S. based management team and assets in Mexico, Costa Rica and Brazil, IHC is uniquely positioned as the sole Pan-American leader in hospital based healthcare.
IHC manages eleven acute-care private hospitals: eight operating hospitals and hree hospitals under development. Each hospital is modeled after state-of-the-art facilities in the United States, providing a full range of basic and sophisticated surgical, emergency and diagnostic services.
