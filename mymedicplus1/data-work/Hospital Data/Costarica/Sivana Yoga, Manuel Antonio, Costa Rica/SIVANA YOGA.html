Description:

Silvia became a certified Yoga instructor at the Pentalfa Academy in Sevilla, Spain. She practices and instructs the contemporary Hot Yoga style of Bikram Choudhury.  Her main inspiration for Ashtanga Yoga came with her teacher training with Larry Schultz from "It's Yoga, California" in San Jose, Costa Rica.

Silvia brings joy, enthusiasm and love for yoga to her teaching, and her gentle guidance and knowledge is a great experience.  Her classes have an atmosphere of challenge without competition or judgment and attends to each student individually, which helps everyone feel the pose more deeply, making it more joyful. She continues to attend workshops and training to grow in experience and knowledge.

Balance Inside Out

In her yoga retreats, Silvia will adjust her teaching to accommodate all students needs and will make the practice as gentle or difficult as they like. Having lived in Costa Rica for almost 12 years, Silvia will be able to advise students on all aspects of their stay in Costa Rica

 After traveling, it's time to start the transition into your tropical, laid-back, stress-free retreat week. To get you started, you will receive a wellness massage and scrub. It's a perfect way begin your week.

 Yoga Classes

In the mornings we start at 7 AM with a 2-hour class, explaining and discussing the yoga poses and practice.  For regular yoga practitioners, it is an opportunity to work on the practice intensively. For beginners, this is an opportunity to learn from the experience of the other participants and they will get as much assistance as they need.

 

After class we will get together for breakfast.  An excellent start for your health and wellness to prepare to head out to your adventures. This will be a time for planning your day and sharing experiences. The day will be free for all the fun things available in the Manuel Antonio area.  We will be happy to arrange any of these activities for you. Private classes can be arranged also. See the Adventures page to see all the exciting options to choose from.

 Sunset Yoga Sessions

At sunset, we will join together for a smooth Vinyasa Yoga session while enjoying a tropical sunset. Sessions are built around Vinyasa sequences, with a focus on precise alignment and BREATH.  Go with the flow.  You will walk out light, free and energized.
