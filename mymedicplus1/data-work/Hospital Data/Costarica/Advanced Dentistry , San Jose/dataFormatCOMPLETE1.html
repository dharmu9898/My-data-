Doctors:

Dr. Eloy Mora

Dr. Mora graduated as a Dental Surgeon at the Latin-American University of Science and Technology (ULACIT) in San Jose, Costa Rica. He received his training in prosthodontics from the University of Chile where he graduated with honors and then furthered his training in Cosmetics and Restorative Dentistry at University of Minnesota.

Dr. Mora is a professor at various academic institutions in Costa Rica and has lectured in Costa Rica and other countries as well.

Dr. Mora is an active member of the Costa Rican Academy of Prosthodontics, and the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry.

 

 

Dra. Mónica Naranjo

 Dr. Naranjo is a highly qualified Dental Surgeon who specializes in Peridontics, and her main academic and private practice focuses on treatment of periodontal diseases, scaling and root planing, bone grafts, gum surgery as flaps, periodontal plastic surgery, gingivectomies in adults and children among other procedures.

Dr. Naranjo is a theory professor and clinical instructor at undergraduate, graduate and intership levels of the prestigious School of Dentistry at the University of Costa Rica and the University of the Americas where she is Professor Member of the Thesis Jury for the Undergraduate Level.

 

Dr. Christopher Lang

Dr. Lang graduated as a Dental Surgeon at the Veritas University in San Jose, Costa Rica. He received his training in endodontics from the Autonomous University of San Luis Potosí, México where he graduated and then worked as a assistant clinical instructor.

Dr. Lang has been a professor at various academic institutions in Costa Rica and is an active member of the Mexican Council of Endodoncy.

Dr. Lang´s main academic and private dentistry practice focuses on root canal therapy and root canal surgery using state of the art surgical tools and techniques, which allows him to extend his services to overseas patients seeking affordable, top-quality restorative services with the best results.
