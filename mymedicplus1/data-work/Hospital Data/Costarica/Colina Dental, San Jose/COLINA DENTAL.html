List of Surgeries/Treatments:

Prosthetics

·         Dental Crowns

Replaces the tooth structure that has been lost by cavities, fractures or old fillings giving back the original strength of the natural tooth. They can be made of porcelain fused to metal or just porcelain to enhance the aesthetics

·         Porcelain Veneers

Use to improve the aesthetics of colored, mis aligned, chipped, short or narrow teeth. Made of the finest porcelain, is a two session treatment that will improve your look with a minimal tooth loss.

 

·         Removable Dentures

A procedure made to replace some or all of your lost teeth to bring back the function of your mouth and the general appereance of your face. This treatment can be mixed with dental implants to improve the retention of the prostheses.

 

·         Resin Fillings

Non metalic fillings to restore teeth with cavities. needing conservative drilling and reaching the highest expectations of an aesthetic restoration.

·         Dental Bridge

Made to replace one or more teeth, not removable, made of porcelain fused to metal or just porcelain.

 

·         Bleaching

In less than two hours you can have your teeth bleached with a high quality in office product with no damage to your teeth.

Dental Implants

A dental implant is an artificial tooth root that an implantologist places into your jaw to hold a replacement tooth or bridge… more

Endodontics (Root canal)

Is a dental procedure that removes the vital part of the tooth that is damaged by an extense cavity or a trauma. This treatment depends on the amount of canals that the tooth has, usually one in the anterior teeth, two in the bicuspids and three or four in the molars.

Periodontics

Specialty of dentistry that takes care of your gum and bone, to give your teeth the correct support and health.

Orthodontics

A procedure done to correct mis aligned teeth to provide aesthetics and adequate function to your mouth.

Pediatric Dentistry

To prevent future problems in your kid’s mouth, the early treatment and good habit build up should be the rule. Our specialist will solve all your doubts about your child oral health
