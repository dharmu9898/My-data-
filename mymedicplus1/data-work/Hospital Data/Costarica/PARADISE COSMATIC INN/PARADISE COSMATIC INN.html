  • Rashi Rosenstock, M.D.

Dr. Rashi Rosenstock earned his medical doctorate at the Medical Sciences University of Central America with full honors, obtaining a Summa Cum Laude degree and the academic crown.He then served his residency in general surgery at the Calderon Guardia Hospital in Costa Rica, where he was admitted as the number one contestant nationally.

Dr. Rosenstock is an accomplished aesthetic surgeon for all areas of the body including facelift and eyelid techniques, breast surgery, arm lift, abdominoplasty, and contouring of all areas of the body with liposuction or lifting surgery.
