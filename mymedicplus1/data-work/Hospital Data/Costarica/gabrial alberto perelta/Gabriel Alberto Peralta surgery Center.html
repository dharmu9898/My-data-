Doctors:

Dr. Gabriel A. Peralta M, M.D.

Dr. Gabriel A. Peralta’s interest in plastic surgery begins early in his medical career, while he was doing his third year.

He has assisted several of most renowned plastic surgeons of his natural Santa Fe de Bogotá, Colombia since 1987.

In 1991, presented the exams of the Educational Commission for Foreign Medical Graduates (ECFMG) - American Board of Medicine and the Federal Licensing Examination - Pennsylvania (FLEX) approving them successfully.

Between 1992 and 1993 he works as assistant instructor of Anatomy in the Medical and Dental Schools at the Universidad El Bosque (formerly, Escuela Colombiana de Medicina).

Moved to San Jose Costa Rica where he did five years training in Plastic and Reconstructive surgery at the University of Costa Rica between 1994 and 1998.

In 1999 he travels to Guadalajara, Mexico for a one-year fellowship in Aesthetic Surgery with Dr. Jose Guerrero-Santos and Dr. Rafael Vergara Calleros.

In 2001 completed the certification to join the Physicians and Surgeons College of Costa Rica.

 

Magda Liz Peralta Mantilla, DDS

The sense of aesthetics and taste for Surgical Sciences came together since an early age, under the influence of her parents, prestigious Colombian dentists focusing their professional practice in Integral Advanced Dentistry.

In 1992, received her Dentistry’s Diploma at the Pontificia Universidad Javeriana in Santa Fe de Bogotá, Colombia.

During 1993, she received training at the San Juan de Dios Hospital in Bogotá on Aesthetic Surgery procedures. At the same time, did her private practice in Cosmetic destistry.

In 1995, began specialization studies at the P.U.J in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, receiving her Diploma in 1998 after an intensive residency at the Medicine and Dentistry Faculties.

In 1999, she directed her efforts to treatment of dentofacial anomalies at the Police Central Hospital in Bogotá, Colombia, under the supervision of Dr. Maria Costanza Pineda (distinguished Maxilofacial surgeon).

Later on, in 2001 she moves to San José, Costa Rica where she completes her training in Oral Implantology at the U.L.A.C.I.T ( Universidad Latinoamericana de Ciencia y Tecnologia ).

Dr. Magda Peralta has fulfilled the requirements of law as a Dentist and Maxillofacial surgeon of the Costa Rican College of Dental Surgeons, of which she is member since 2003.
