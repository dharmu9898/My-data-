Description:

Hospital Punta Pacifica is a newly constructed hospital located in the heart of one of the most prominent areas of Panama City. The hospital is the only digitally connected hospital in the geographic region, and offers the highest quality of medical services. The staff at Hospital Punta Pacifica is highly trained in all areas. Many staff physicians working at the complex have been either trained in the United States or Europe and have international certifications.

Hospital Punta Pacifica was constructed in March 2006 and is equipped with state-of-the-art, high-quality machines. The digital facility provides patients with advantages in a number of different areas. Videoconferencing equipment allows our physicians to confer remotely with other physicians abroad over sharp and clear images of patient tests, X-Rays, CT scans, and MRIs. Mobile laptops are made available to nurses and doctors, making possible bedside recordings of patients’ vital signs, symptoms, and medication requirements.

Our History and Mission

Hospital Punta Pacifica was born from the hopes and dreams of a group of Panamanian physicians. These specialists, in association with notable businessmen, wanted to provide Panama, Latin America and the world with a world-class digital hospital possessing the highest standards of quality, care, security and technology. Hospital Punta Pacifica has a state-of-the-art facilities and systems supported by one of the best hospitals in the world, Johns Hopkins Medicine International.

Highlights

Hospital Punta Pacifica, Affiliated with Johns Hopkins Medicine International, is a thoroughly modern, state-of-the-art hospital. The hospital is designed to the standards of Johns Hopkins Medicine International and includes:

  • 52 private rooms and 12 suites. These rooms are specifically designed to provide patients comfort and after care treatment in a relaxed atmosphere.
  • 8 private rooms for intensive care and critical care patients.
  • 3 beds for semi-intensive care patients
  • 2 hemodialysis treatment units
  • 1 isolation room
  • 8 cradles for neonatal critical therapy
  • Neonatology ward for up to 20 newborns
  • 4 intelligent operating rooms
  • 2 childbirth operating rooms
  • 3 labor and delivery rooms
  • Recovery room
