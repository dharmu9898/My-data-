Description:
Clinica Hospital San Fernando is the dream-come true of a group of doctors-businessmen who joined resources to fulfill a deeply need of the community then: to have a private medical enterprise delivering excellent health care. On April 19th, 1949, such idea came to be the Clinica San Fernando.

Since day one, Clinica Hospital San Fernando has maintained itself as the number one Medical Center in the Republic of Panama, since to reach such goal we have established our vision and our mission.
