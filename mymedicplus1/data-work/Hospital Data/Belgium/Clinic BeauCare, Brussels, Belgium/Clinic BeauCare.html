Description:

Clinic BeauCare is a private clinic for plastic and aesthetic surgery, located in the rural Machelen, near Brussels. We offer a broad spectrum of the most progressive plastic and aesthetic surgery at honest all inclusive prices, for women and for men.Our personal and warm approach is central and our top surgeons provide you with aesthetic plastic surgery of superior quality.

Facilities:

  • We only work with highly qualified plastic surgeons.Doctor Plovier is one of the most well known Belgian plastic surgeons. He has a lot of surgical experience and is asked frequently as a guest speaker for medical symposia.
  • All our surgeons are registered in the UK at the General Medical Council and are member of the Belgian society for Plastic, Reconstructive and Aesthetic surgery.
  • We have nearly 25 years of experience in cosmetic surgery. Our surgeons use the latest techniques. Due to very innovative equipment and a high level of expertise we can offer a treatment which gives a minimum of scaring and revolutionary results.
  • All our staff is English speaking.A personal & warm accompaniment is our trade mark!
  • We only use all-inclusive prices without additional charges as well as a free re-intervention after possible medical problems (i.e. bleeding…).Our prices are very competitive and are often less then half the prices in the UK!Belgium is famous for its exceptional standards of medical treatments at affordable prices.
  • Our clinic offers life time guarantee on the used implants (breasts & buttock implants).Our prices are net prices. This means you will not be confronted with any extra charges for anaesthesia, lipopanty or special support bra.
  • 5 years guarantee on capsular contraction.1 year guarantee for a free re-intervention whenever problems after a surgery.
  • Easy and cheap transportation to Belgium using the Eurostar shuttle train or coming by plane. Our clinic is located only 10 minutes away from the international airport and 15 minutes from the Eurostar train station.
