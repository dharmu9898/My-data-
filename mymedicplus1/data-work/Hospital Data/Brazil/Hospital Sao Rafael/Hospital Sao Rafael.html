Description:

The HSR, with more than 20 years and an enterprising vision, keeps its focus as a surgical hospital, focused on the accomplishment of elective procedures with scheduled hour of short permanence.

The Complex was especially projected to offer an attendance of quality and security, allied to a fastidiousness and comfort environment always in growth and evolution.

Attention to the human side, personalized attendance and proximity with our customers had always been recognized as our characteristics.

History:

  • 1988 - Inauguration of the HMSR;
  • 1990 - Opening of the HSR for external surgeons and anesthetists;
  • 1997 - Inauguration of CC ambulatory, for surgeries without internment, carried through in the security of a hospital environment;
  • 2002 - Determination of the focus in elective surgeries and short permanence.
  • 2004 - Opening of beds for intensive monitoring and specialized medical duty 24 hours;
  • 2005 - Acquisition of last generation’s equipment and implantation of the Fast Sterilization system;
  • 2006 - Beginning of 2ª Phase of the magnifying of the Hospital São Rafael.
