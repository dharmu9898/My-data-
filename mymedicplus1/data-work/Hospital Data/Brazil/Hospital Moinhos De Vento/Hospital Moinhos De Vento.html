Description:

Hospital Moinhos de Vento was founded in 1927, which means 81 years of tradition, quality and excellence in processes and services.

Our Institution is nationally and internationally recognized. Awards such as the “Troféu Ouro,” granted by the Program of Productivity Quality and the international accreditation Joint Commission International are manifestations that confirm the efficiency and efficacy of our service.

Last January we were once again mentioned as one of the most admired hospital institutions in Brazil by the magazine Saúde Análise 2009 – The Most Admired in Medicine. In addition to highlighting important physicians that are part of our clinical staff, that publication also stated that our organization is recognized in 24 specialties.

José Adroaldo Oppermann
President of the Administrative Board of Associação Hospitalar Moinhos de Vento.

Highlights
