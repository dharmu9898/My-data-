Description:

The Sírio-Libanês Hospital is famous for its pioneering practices and excellent service. Its actions always involve the human aspect in its patient relationships. After all, what makes the Sírio-Libanês Hospital such a special institution is the feeling that people have of being treated with care and respect after visiting the Hospital for an examination, surgical procedure or medical treatment.

Occupying an area of 80,000 m2, and with 4,500 collaborators - including staff, nursing and medical teams - the Sírio-Libanês Hospital has the capacity to carry out up to 50 surgical procedures per day, around 2,000 types of diagnostic examinations and has beds for 300 people.

A pioneer in incorporating new technology, the Sírio-Libanês Hospital specializes in over 40 areas and is a worldwide reference point in its areas of competence, because it gives priority to everybody's most important asset: their life.

MISSION

The Ladies’ Beneficent Society – Hospital Sírio-Libanês - is a Brazilian Philanthropic Institution focused on integrated actions in the areas of social work, health, education and research.

VISION

To be internationally recognized for its leadership and pioneering spirit in health care and in generating knowledge, with social and environmental responsibility, and self-sustainability, attracting and retaining medical talent and collaborators.
