Description:

A. C. Camargo Cancer Hospital, an institution sponsored by the Brazilian Foundation Fundação Antônio Prudente, is one of the main Latin America Centers for diagnosis, treatment, education and research on cancer. Established in the beginning of the 1950’s, is a result of the persistence of visionaries who joined forces to manage the first Brazilian hospital devoted to oncologic treatment.

If today the fight against cancer is still a complex process, in those years it was almost impossible. However, the current reality is that we are conquering cancer. Our therapeutic success indices are similar to those of the best world cancer treatment centers. This is due to the excellence and specialization of our team, the incessant development of research and the accuracy in diagnosis and treatment.ing Home).

A. C. Camargo Cancer Hospital today

To become the main center of cancer diagnosis, treatment, teaching and research in Latin America, the Cancer Hospital of São Paulo congregated in these 53 years of existence what there exist of more qualified and advanced in relation to the illness: it created the cancer school of the country; it brought back to Brazil famous doctors and researchers; it developed techniques that today are a reference in the whole world; it has implemented the most advanced techniques for diagnosis and treatment of cancer. In addition, it formed a multidisciplinary team with more than 250 specialists, masters and Ph.D.s of international reputation.
