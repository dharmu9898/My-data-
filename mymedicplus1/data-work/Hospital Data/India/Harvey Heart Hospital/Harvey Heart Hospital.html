Description:

Harvey Heart Hospital is a clinical care facility housing 25-30 beds, including 12 critical care beds, solely dedicated to Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery and ancillary services with out-patient areas and clinics. It has the potential and option in increase the bed strength to 50-60 beds in the immediate future. The Hospital provides clinical care for patients with heart disorders and includes state of the art diagnostic, therapeutic, rehabilitative and preventive facilities. Harvey Heart Hospital is located at a modern custom built hospital facility in the heart of Chennai, the Vasanthi Medical Centre.

List of Surgeries/Treatments:

Cardiology

The services offered includes

  • Outpatient services for diagnostic workup
  • Echocardiography
  • Computerised Stress Test Laboratory
  • Cardiac catheterisation (Adult and pediatric)
  • Interventional Cardiology (PTCA, Balloon valvolomies, EPS)
  • Coronary Care Services for acute cardiological problems
  • Inpatient services for cardiological treatment

Cardiac Surgery

The cardiac surgical services offered includes the full spectrum of operations for both congenital and acquired heart disorderes involving both adult and pediatric patients. This would include:

Congenital Heart disorder

  • Palliative operations
  • Corrective surgery

Aquired Heart disorder

  • Myocardial Revascularisation Surgery
  • Valve surgery (Replacements/Repairs/Homografts/Autografts)
  • Thoracic Organ Transplanttation (Heart/ Heart and Lung /Lung)
  • Arrhythmia surgery
  • Thoracic aortic and large vessel surgery
  • Vascular Surgery
  • Pulmonary and oesophageal surgery
