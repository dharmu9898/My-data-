Description:

Apollo Hospitals Bangalore is the latest of Apollo’s offerings and is located at one of the most vibrant and dynamic cities in India - Bangalore.

Apollo Hospitals Bangalore is the newest addition to the ever-growing network of Apollo Hospitals all over the world. The hospital is equipped with some of the most advanced facilities in the world and some of the most eminent names in several medical specialties and superspecialities. It has been awarded the gold-standard certification from the Joint Commission International (JCI) USA .

The doctors at Apollo Hospitals Bangalore have been chosen after an extensive process where their commitment and expertise is assessed in equal measure. Most of them have studied, trained or worked abroad at eminent institutions. The hospital has over 100 consultants available round the clock.

Apollo Hospitals Bangalore is committed to invest in the latest medical technology on par with the latest worldwide developments to ensure that every one of our patients benefit from the latest knowledge.

Highlights

Surgical Infrastructure

Attention to surgical infrastructure goes far beyond the obvious. A completely sterile environment during and after surgery is ensured with the help of the latest technologies. From airflow systems to surgical equipment, world standards in surgical protocol are followed to eliminate even minimal chances of infection. Navigation systems with complete orthopaedic and neuro modules and on-time cardiac output monitoring system are also available.

Cath Lab

Cath Lab with 3D CA technology that minimizes the quantity of contrast dye used in a single injection, is available for the first time in India.

Dialysis Unit

10 State-of-the-art stations with both hi-flux & low-flux transfusion compatibility, advanced round-the-clock renal service for all age groups and one of the best Reverse Osmosis water treatment plants for ultra pure water are some of the unique features in the Dialysis unit.
