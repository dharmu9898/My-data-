We have great pride and pleasure to introduce ourselves as one of the most reputed 150 bedded Multi-Specialty and Super-specialty Hospitals in Northern India, equipped with Ultra modern and state-of-the-art facilities for Comprehensive Care – right from OPD Consultations and routine health check-ups to Intensive Care and Specialized & Super-Specialized Surgeries. About Us

This hospital was started 30 years back in Jallandhar in the year 1976 by an experienced team of a physician and a surgeon, Dr. B.S. Chopra M.D. and Dr. S.K. Sharma M.S. as 20 bedded hospital. With their dedicated hard work in the medical field the hospital has grown to have a number of specialties and culminated in a first private cancer hospital to become fully functional in north India with all Facilities for oncology and cancer surgery. The total bed strength has reached 153 beds out of which 65 beds are only for cancer patients.
Aims and Missions

While having diversified interests, the group has always upheld its social commitments and have invested extensively in charitable causes of medical and patients care at rates affordable to all the section of the society .

The motto of the hospital is :
“Comprehensive, compassionate, dedicated and efficient patient care with latest health care techniques”

Location

Patel hospital is ideally located in the heart of the city in Civil Lines area of Jallandhar spread over in approx 100000 Sq ft. It is about 10 minutes distance form the Bus Stand and Railway station and about 100 meters off the main G.T. Road. With a bed capacity of 153 beds out of which 65 beds are for cancer patients, the hospital has 5 dedicated operation theatres, 8 bedded post operative recovery, 13 bedded Intensive Care Unit (ICU) , 8 bedded Trauma Unit, 32 deluxe private rooms, 18 private rooms, 16 semi deluxe beds, 16 ward beds with AC and 32 beds in general wards among others.
