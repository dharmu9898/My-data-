Community Opthamology

Our community OUT REACH CAMP PROGRAMME aims at providing quality eye care for all, irrespective of their capacity to pay. Regular diagnostic (check - up) camps are organised in collaboration with several service organizations, far and near. From these the patients who need surgery are brought to our base hospital, CALCUTTA LIONS NETRA NIKETAN. The operation including IOL, stay and food are all free. Check-up of school children is a regular feature.
