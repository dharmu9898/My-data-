Description:

One of Eastern India 's leading Eye Hospitals, Priyamvada Birla Aravind Eye Hospital offers the best in eye care treatment, with multispeciality approach.

Priyamvada Birla Aravind Eye Hospital is amongst the premier ophthalmic hospitals in Kolkata and one of the best in India with emphasis placed on empowering eye care by combining the latest technology available and the best human element in health care.

The pioneering endeavor of the hospital was to make available the latest advances in ophthalmology at a very competitive cost.The hospital is fully equipped to deal with a wide spectrum of eye disorders, making it a center for TOTAL AND COMPLETE EYE CARE.

Aravind Eye Hospitals, collaboration centre of World Health Organisation for prevention and control of blindness, had established ARAVIND EYE CARE SYSTEM. It is not only the greatest service provider but also a model to replicate. Aravind has tried this out in 160 projects out of which 120 are in India and 40 abroad.

EYE CARE FACILITIES available at all centres are Cataract & General Ophthalmology, Retina Vitreous, Cornea, Paediatric Ophthalmology, Strabismus & Contact Lens, Glaucoma, Uvea, Orbit & Oculoplasty, Neuro-Ophthalmology and Vision Rehabilitation Services
