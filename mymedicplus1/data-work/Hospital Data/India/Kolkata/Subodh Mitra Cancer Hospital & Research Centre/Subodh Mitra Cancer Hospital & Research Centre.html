Description:

Subodh Mitra Cancer Hospital & Research Centre, provides world class cancer care at an affordable cost. It is a self sufficient cancer care hospital which offers a coordinated multi-disciplined approach that helps patients to feel comfortable with their diagnosis, treatment plan and surgical procedures.

The organization works to ensure the well-being and comfort of patients suffering from cancer through engaging round the clock available highly qualified specialists and using technologically most developed equipments. We develop customized treatment strategies that are appropriate for each patient and consult when necessary with leading specialists. Its a state of art cancer treatment centre under one roof.

Cancer is a deadly disease and the most expensive one. So the poor cancer patients are deprived of proper treatment. Subodh Mitra Cancer Hospital has been trying its utmost to provide minimum treatment and to find out a treatment protocol for the poor cancer patients

Highlights

Mission:

  • To deliver safe and up-to-date world-class cancer care for the people at a very low cost.
  • To deliver excellent care in a non-profit making environment.
  • To increase and develop the expertise of doctors, nursing staff, technicians and other health care workers at the hospital through training, good management and (where appropriate) through technological and methodological interaction and exchange with specialists.
  • To raise the state of knowledge about cancer among health practitioners and the public at large in eastern India.
  • To promote and develop clinical research activities in the field of cancer.
  • To introduce exacting standards of evaluation and quality control to ensure optimal health care at the hospital.
  • To become a beacon of excellence and trust for cancer patients and their families.
