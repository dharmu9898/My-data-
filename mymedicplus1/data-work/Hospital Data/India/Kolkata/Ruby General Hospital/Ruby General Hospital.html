Description:

Dr. Kamal K. Dutta is the founder Chairman of Ruby General Hospital. This is the first Non-Resident Indian doctor's hospital in Eastern India. It was made following the hospital model in United States. Most of the instruments and technology was imported from United States.

The Hon'ble Chief Minister Shri Jyoti Basu inaugurated Ruby General Hospital on 25th April 1995. Mother Teresa opened the Cardiac Unit on 28th October 1995.

The motto of Ruby General Hospital is to give honest, caring, compassionate and economical care to the people of Eastern India. The consultants working at Ruby are the best with national repute. The quality of medical care is always monitored so that the patients are assured that medically necessary treatment is only being given to them. There is also a constant inflow of doctors from United States who bring the latest advances of treatment to the patients at Ruby Hospital.

Organization
There are many hospitals in Kolkata. However, if one is looking for international standard care, the choice is severely limited. In many cases the essential infrastructure is either not available or out of date. "

Ruby Hospital seeks to breach the gap by providing quality care. Though a general hospital, it provides the finest facilities for critical and emergency care available in the city, as well as some of the best surgical skills. It not only offers essential medical support, such as round-the-clock doctors and working equipment, but goes well beyond. It is one hospital that takes extraordinary care to focus on small details that can make the vital difference between life and death. "

Ruby today is widely recognised as one of the finest medical institutions in Eastern India. As an ISO 9001:2000 hospital it assures patients consistent quality care. As a humanitarian hospital, it opens its doors for all.
