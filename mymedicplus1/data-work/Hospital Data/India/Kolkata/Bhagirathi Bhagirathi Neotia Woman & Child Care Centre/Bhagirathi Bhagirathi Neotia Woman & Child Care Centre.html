Description:

BNWCCC is a super speciality 83 bedded hospital for woman and child healthcare in eastern India. The hospital was set up in February 2002 by the renowned Neotia family, who have diversified interests in real estate and hospitality.

BNWCCC takes pride in its specialists, experienced nurses and occupational therapists who are committed in providing comprehensive care to all patients and making them feel at home away from home.
