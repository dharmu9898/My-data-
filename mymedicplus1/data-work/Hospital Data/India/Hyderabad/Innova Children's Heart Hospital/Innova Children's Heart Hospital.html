Description:

The story of the Institute reads like a refreshing treatise in the sphere of modern medicine. Its chapter includes development of new procedures and also welcomes success in new form of treatment. This Hospital is given utmost importance for asepsis.

The operation theatres are well maintained, with most sophisticated 0.3µ, hepafilters, with laminar flow air tent to reduce intra - operative infection. The ICU’s are also equally well maintained with 5µ hepafilters that facilitate many air exchanges. Utmost care and importance are given for hand washing. All the important areas are provided with washbasins for an easy hand washing. The critical areas are supplied with reverse osmosis processed water with UV sterilization.

The Hospital is situated in a prime location in Hyderabad. It has a capacity of 100 beds, 3 Operation rooms, three state- of-the-art echo-cardiograph machines and one cardiac catheterization lab. It also has 50-bedded specially designed intensive care units for post-cardiac surgery, surgical step-down, pre-operative stabilization and Neo-natal ICU.
