Description:

Dr. Mohan's Diabetes Specialities Centre., was inaugurated on September 1, 1991 by Late Prof. M. Viswanathan, Director, Diabetes Research Centre, Madras & father of Dr. V. Mohan.

The centre was started with a two fold purpose:

  • To provide comprehensive and Total Diabetic Care i.e. not only routine treatment of diabetes but also of its various complications.
  • To provide this modern and efficient diabetes service right in the heart of Chennai so that the facilities could be made available to a larger section of the population.
  • The response to the centre has been phenomenal both from the patients as well as from the medical profession.

Highlights

Our Vision

  • To provide truly World class health care to diabetic patients at nominal charges.
  • To provide free service to the poor and needy diabetic patients.
  • To carry out research on diabetes and related complications so as to improve the treatment of diabetic patients and also to determine strategies to prevent diabetes and its complications.
  • To train doctors, paramedical personnel, scientists and students on various aspects of diabetes health care and research.
