Description:

KALYANI DENTAL HOSPITALS is a perfect dental care centre for the whole family. Our objective is to offer you the best possible dental care so that you can maintain a radiant SMILE and keep smiling through life and beyond.

KALYANI DENTAL HOSPITALS offers a complete range of services from braces to bridges and from smile maintenance to smile makeovers.

Easy Appointments
We work by appointments so that YOU don't need to waste your time waiting and we have the time to treat you well.

Established in 1998 by Dr T Sampath Kumar, Kalyani Dental Hospitals is a specialised and well-equipped Dentistry and Implant Centre that offers professional Super-speciality dental service of excellent quality and at cost-affordable rates. It has grown into one of the leading dental hospitals in the city with six branches and has been rendering client-friendly service with a touch of success.

Kalyani Dental Hospitals possess State of the art building has a spacious reception hall with a large sitting capacity that is host to well equipped doctors chambers with fully automatic dental chairs and state of the art dental equipments. A separate X-Ray room is made so as to avoid radiation exposure to other patients and a central music system is in operation to generate a soothing atmosphere in the hospital.
