Description:

Established in 1994 by Mr KR Gangadharan, a specialist in hospital administration with decades of experience and expertise in a comprehensive range of management activities. With its headquarters at Hyderabad, it extends support services in Chennai, Vijayawada and Bangalore as well.

A Multi-specialty hospital with a rich collection of medical facilities of the best variety, it offers affordable medical and non-medical services to clients.

Our Philosophy

We do not believe in the philosophy of healthcare that merely treats patients and declaring them medically fit. We believe in the overall well being of patients, which includes their social, emotional, Psychological and physical well being. Also, we believe that it is people.

We will respond to the specific needs for attention and care of patients that go far beyond medical intervention at the hospital. A complete range of services & activities is offered to comprehensively meet the patients needs.

Highlights

Total Patient Management

Magnificent 100-bed, state-of-the-art hospital is aesthetically designed & spacious with patient-friendly interiors. With a wide range of diagnostic and treatment infrastructure that offers services at cost-effective rates.

Modern Operation Theatres, equipped with state-of-the-art apparatus such as a 'C-Arm', imported shadow less lights, tables, anaesthetic equipment, surgical diathermy and operating microscopes.

  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU) with ventilator support, to handle acute/emergency cases and to treat patients with cancer, diabetes, hypertension, chronic liver problems, heart/cerebro-vascular disorders, pneumonia and other respiratory diseases
  • In-patient rooms to cater to various customer preferences: super-deluxe rooms, deluxe rooms, sharing rooms and general wards, TV sets and telephones are installed in all rooms and wards for the comfort of the patients and their caregivers
  • Fully computerised patient care procedures and billing systems
