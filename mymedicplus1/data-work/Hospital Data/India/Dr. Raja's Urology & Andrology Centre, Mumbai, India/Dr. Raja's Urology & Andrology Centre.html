Dr Dilip Raja :

M.B.B.S., M.S., M.N.A.M.S., F.I.C.S., D.N.B.E. (UROLOGY)

Dr. Dilip Raja is a consulting Urologist and Andrologist for more than twenty years at Mumbai, India. He is a Gold Medalist and recipient of many scholarships during his under and post-graduate training. He has been trained extensively in his speciality at various renowned centers in the world like…for stone disease in Germany, Microsurgery in Belgium, Endo-urology, Urinary Malignancies, Impotence and Urinary Incontinence at Mayo Clinic, USA and for Infertility at Australia.

He has done pioneering work in the field of kidney stone diseases. He was the first in India to use Lithotripsy (ESWL) machine. He has large experience in treating stone patients with all the modalities like PCNL , URS and Open surgery as a complete management of stone disease.

He is also one of the first few urologist and Andrologist to start newer technologies like Electro-vaporization of prostate, Penile Implants, Microsurgery in Urology etc in India. Endo-Urology, Stones, Prostate, Impotence are his all time favorites.

Dr. Raja has a large number of national and international publications to his credit. He has delivered many Guest lectures, conducted surgical workshops and has been instrumental in setting up Kidney Units at national and international levels.

At present he is a senior consultant Urologist and Andrologist at Lilavati Hospital, Sir Hurkisondas Hospital, Wockhardt Hospital, Breach Candy Hospital and Saifee Hospital at Mumbai, India. He has his Urology & Andrology Centres at Bandra in Mumbai, India.

He is a member of Mumbai Urology Society, Urology Society of India,National Academy of Medical Sciences, American Urology Association, Endo-Urology Association, USA, Socie`te` Internationale d`Urologie and almost all the prestigious international urology and andrology societies in the world.
