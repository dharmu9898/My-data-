Description:

Hospital for Orthopaedics, Sports Medicine, Arthritis, Accident-Trauma, Hosmat Arthritis & Joint Replacement Center (HAJRC) ,Hosmat Institute of Neurosciences (HINS) and Hosmat Institute of Spine Problems (HISP) is a super major speciality hospital of 350 beds in Bangalore, India, ideally located in central Bangalore about 0.5 kilometers from Brigade Road or MG Road. It is the first comprehensive hospital in India specializing in Orthopaedics, Sports medicine, Arthritis, Accident-Trauma and Neurosciences, and is undergoing an expansion phase to 500 beds, making it the largest speciality hospital of its kind in Asia.

A modern and spacious building has been designed and constructed for Orthopaedic and Neuro patients, the latest state of the art equipment in all disciplines of Orthopaedics, Neuro surgery, Neurology, Accident - Trauma and Sports Medicine have been installed. Highly qualified and experienced medical consultants are the mainstay of the hospital.

In the past ten years, HOSMAT has gained a reputation of being one of the best hospitals in orthopaedics and its subspecialities,joint replacement, arthroscopy, children's ortho, hand, spine, arthritis, Ilizarov, sports medicine and reconstructive surgery, Neurosurgery and Neurology making it the first Orthopaedic and Joint Replacement Hospital to get ISO9002 - TUV Certified.

The various surgeries and facilities are: General Orthopaedics Surgeries,Medical and surgical treatment of arthritis, Joint replacements of the knee, hip, shoulder and elbow, Fibreoptic arthroscopy of various joints, treatment of fractures, non-union and malunion of fractures, ligament injuries, reconstructive surgeries, specialized Ilizarov surgery (limb lengthening procedure), Correction of deformities, Spinal surgeries (disc problems, spondylitis, pinched nerves, spine fractures, deformities, scholiosis and tumours), Paediatric Orthopaedics and Industrial injuries.

The Hosmat Institute of Neuro Sciences (HINS) provides comprehensive neurosurgical as well as neurological care which includes:

1. Microsurgery
2. Skull base
3. Trauma work
4. Nerve injuries
5. Surgery for brain tumors
6. migrane
7. muscular discorder
8. Parkinson's disease
9. multiple sclerosis
10. vegetablur problems
11. Guillian Barre (GB) syndrome, etc.

The Hosmat Institute of Spine Problems (HISP) provides comprehensive care which includes:

1. spinal cord tumours
2. slipped discs
3. paraplegia
4. hemi-plegia
5. quadraplegia

An eminent panel of doctors deal with polytrauma cases on a daily basis.

History

The concept of a Major Orthopaedics, Accident Trauma and Neurocentre was inititated by Dr. Thomas A Chandy the Medical Director and Chief of Orthopaedics. He did his MBBS at St. John's Medical College, Bangalore and moved to the US in 1976 for specialization in orthopaedics.

Dr. Thomas Chandy
M.B.B.S., F.A.A.O.S., D.A.B.O.S., F.A.C.S., F.I.C.S., (U.S.A.)
Director and Chief of Orthopaedics &
Joint Replacement Center

After completing a 4 year Post Graduation Resident training in 1980 in Orthopaedics at the Jewish Hospital and Medical Centre in New York , he was on the faculty at the same hospital for two years.

Dr. Chandy trained for a year (1982-1983) at the University of Oklahoma in Arthroscopy, Sports Medicine and knee surgery.He was appointed as the Chief of Orthopaedics at the Claremore Regional Medical Centre, in 1983 and worked there till 1993. At this time, the idea evolved to start a world class Orthopaedic, Neuro and Accident hospital on an international standard. He is a U.S. Board Certified Orhopaedic Surgeon.

He performed his first Joint Replacement at the Jewish Medical Center in 1978 and has successfully performed 2000 Joint Replacement during his 18 years in the US and 13 years at HOSMAT and in addition Numerous other Orthopedic operations such as Arthroscopic surgeries of :

1. Knee and Shoulder.
2. Arthroscopic Cruciate Ligament Reconstructions.
3. Paediatric Orthopaedics and Trauma.
