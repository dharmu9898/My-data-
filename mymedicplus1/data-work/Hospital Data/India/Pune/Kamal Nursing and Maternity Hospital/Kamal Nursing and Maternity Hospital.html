Description:

This hospital is run successfully by a husband wife team of Dr. Raj and Dr. Vaishali.

The hospital was started in 1999 with the basic objective to give surgical and maternity care to people staying in areas like Wanawadi and Kondhawa, to give emergency care to all patients of any kind and guide them to a faster recovery and to become a basic surgical and maternity center in the area.

Staff nurses are fully trained to conduct the normal delivery, and to assist surgical procedures.The hospital is active in doing charity work through its Kamal Medical Foundation.The hospital hopes to be one of the most trusted hospital in Wanawadi and Kondhawa areas for surgical and maternity matters.
