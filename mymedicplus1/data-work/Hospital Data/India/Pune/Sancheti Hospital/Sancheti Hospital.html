Description:

Pune, the heart of education & culture, is one of the foremost leading cities for health care facilities in India. The rapid industrial progress and overall growth of Pune city has resulted in the health related problems more so due to vehicular accidents and other Orthopaedic problems.

Dr. K.H.Sancheti, the founder of Sancheti Institute, had the foresight to develop an Orthopaedic Hospital of such a magnitude as far back as in 1972. Sancheti Institute, a 150 bedded purely Orthopaedic hospital with a built up area of more than 1 lac. Sq. ft. one of the largest Orthopaedic Hospitals in South East Asia. It is a highly specialized hospital rendering services in all specialties of Orthopaedics like Joint Replacement, Traumatology, Spinal Surgery, Pediatrics Orthopaedic, llizarov Techniques, Arthroscopy and Sports Injuries, Hand and Plastic Surgery.

It has 12 Orthopaedic consultants attached to the hospital, 16 in house residents and 18 Panel Consultants of allied specialties.
