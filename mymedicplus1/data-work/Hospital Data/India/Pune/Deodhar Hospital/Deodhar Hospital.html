Description:

Deodhar Hospital is located about 10 minutes from Thane Railway Station and Eastern Express Highway. It has 2 blood banks within a 2 minute walking distance.

Salient Features:

  • Spaciously built with an area of 6000 square feet with 20 beds
  • An outpatient department over 1000 square feet 5 consulting rooms.
  • In-house pathology lab .
  • An invertor back for the whole hospital with
  • Separate invertor for the operation theatre,
  • Seminar Hall for patient lecture programs,
  • 24 hours security with CCTV surveillance.
  • The operation theatre is well equipped with a Boyles machine, pulse oximeter, defibrillator and baby warmer.
  • The labour room is well equipped with a fetal monitor and baby warmer.
  • The hospital has a team of postgraduate doctors attending the hospital for all the 24 hours.
  • All deliveries are conducted by gynaecologists and attended by the paediatrician.
  • There is separate staff for the OPD with separate assistant doctors.
  • The Nursing staff is experienced , competent , caring and ever smiling The Housekeeping staff is polite with a touch of homeliness.
