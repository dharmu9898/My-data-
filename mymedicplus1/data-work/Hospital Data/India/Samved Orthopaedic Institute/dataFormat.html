About Samved Orthopaedic Institute
Samved Orthpaedic Hospital is a part of Samved Hospital, recognized in India and all over the world. It has world-class, well equipped, and sophisticated facilities. Our well trained specialists, surgeons, and medical team to treat the most complicated cases.

Samved Orthpaedic Hospital an ISO 9001 - 2000 certified hospital is the first Hospital in the western region of the country with, Brainlab, Vector Vision - Germany for Total Knee Replacement Surgery, Hip Replacement Surgery, Spinal Surgery & Polytrauma.

Samved Hospital, inaugurated on 27th September 1998, is a group venture of renowned and experienced Doctors. In a short time, Samved has built up a good reputation as a surgical and medical service provider. The hospital currently has four operation theaters with quality service offered in Orthopaedics, Urology, General Surgery and various Endoscopic surgeries.

Facilities Provided by Samved

Samved Hospital has excellent equipments like Laminar flow ultraclean theater for joint replacement surgery, orthopaedic equipments for spinal surgery, lithotripter, laparoscopy surgery equipments, endoscopic surgery equipments of kidney stones, prostate etc. The hospital has excellent radiology services like MRI, spiral CT scan, Doppler, sonography, X-ray etc.

Location

Samved Orthopaedic Hospital an ultra modern, well equipped & sophisticated hospital, catering to all orthopaedic ailments is located in the prime, posh and clean locality of Ahmedabad situated between stadium circle and commerce six roads. It is easily accessible from the State transport Depot, Railway Station, Domestic and International Airports.

Samved Orthopaedic Hospital set in 1998 today is the one of its kind not only in Ahmedabad but in Entire State of Gujarat too. With its wide range of comprehensive care in the field of orthopaedic. Brainchild of Dr. Bharat J Patel and his dedicated team Samved offers a personalised care, a commitment to perfection with the most advanced instruments like the BRAIN Lab’s VECTOR VISION.

An institute equipped with

- A Laminar Airflow Operation Theater equipped with surgical staff using space suits – filtered air is released into the operation theater adding to the sterile environment. Since bacterial can enter the open wound at the time of the surgery in a regular operating room, we operate in a laminar flow operating room in which special filters provide clean air, free of most bacterial. In addition, the surgeon and assistants wear a sterile space suit. The suit encloses the entire head and body, and includes a sterile face mask

- Fully functional surgical intensive care unit
- 24 hour pathology department
- Highly trained physician round the clock to tackle any medical emergency
- Physiotherapy department
- Paramedical staff trained to look after joint replacement patients

Infrastructure

Samved Orthopaedic Hospital has specialised teams for all surgeries of Orthopaedic, Joint Replacement Unit. Our orthopaedic unit caters primarily hip and Knee replacements, Shoulder and Elbow joint replacements and secondarily Arthritic & Arthroscopic Surgeries. These surgeries of late become very popular, result oriented and rewarding.

It is very important to have an infection-free environment. Complications develop due to infection that occurs during surgery and post surgery. At Samved, all the surgeries are done in specially designed operation theatre known as ‘ULTRA CLEAN AIR CLASS 100’ environment with the basic aim to minimise infection.

Samved has a setup of ‘Clean Air Technology‘, which is approved by well known national consultants. The operation theatre with world class sophisticated and modern equipments makes it comparable to the best in India, for the time in the country.

Samved has introduced the latest procedure in Joint Replacement with the help of image guided system called BRAINLAB, made in Germany, which supercedes all advantages of other systems. There is practically no reaming as is relevant and is 100% accurate along with a very reduced rate of complications like less operating timeless blood loss minimum hospital stay and the life of the Joint is increased.

Samved Orthopaedic Institute has a 1.0 Tesla open magnet MRI, Spiral CT Scan team consisting of renowned radiologists. Samved Hospital has a 42 beds, 15-crore hospital set up in a record time of 8 months.

What makes Samved Hospital different, and in fact puts it at an elevation, besides being the only one of its kind in whole of Gujarat and the State of Rajasthan, is not only has it been fully promoted by doctors, but is also being managed by them.

There are four operation theaters for:

  • Polytrauma
  • Joint replacement – Laminar Flow Theatre (ultra clean class 100)
  • Emergency operation theatre
  • An endoscopic theatre.

We also have a polytrauma care unit with ambulance services and respirators (CCU – critical care unit) around the clock.

With stupendous efforts of Dr. Bharat Patel, Image Guided Surgery machine (IGS), the first of its kind in whole India, was introduced. It could simply be described as a “Great Revolution” to this hospital.

What makes Samved Hospital unique is that an accident victim admitted here, does not have to undergo further trauma of being shunted for tests to other venues, as Dr. Bharat Patel and his team have ensured that the same is available here, and that includes a chemist within the hospital premises.

Dr. Bharat Patel has been so professional in his approach that in this hospital he has ensured the services of two other orthopaedic surgeons besides himself, for he wants to make the Hospital an Institution; not a personal fiefdom.

Dr. Patel has also added an auditorium to the Samved Hospital, for he firmly believes in deliberations, exchange of views and training as an ongoing process and a constant education.

We have facilities such as: Physiotherapy, ambulance, pharmacy, ICU, diagnostic services, cafeteria, radio diagnosism, and nuclear medicine.
