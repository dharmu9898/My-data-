Description:

Lokmanya Medical Foundations sister concern, Lokmanya Medical Research Centre is a non profit organisation and a Societal Transformer. Lokmanya places special emphasis on Health promotion, prevention, protection, partnership and providing Health care services in pursuance of the objective of the trust and health for all strategy of government of India. While providing excellent health care facilities through Lokmanya Hospital Chinchwad and Nigdi we never lost sight of our wider social responsibility by continuing various public health oriented projects and research in community healthLokmanya Medical Research Centre is functioning in the Pimpri-Chinchwad Industrial Belt since 1974 rendering valuable Health Care Service to the surrounding population which consist mainly of:

a.) the resident of the townships
b.) the industrial workers from the surrounding factories and
c.) the sons of the soil from adjacent rural area.

As the health personnel of the foundation began to think more and more on these lines and more links between Profession and diseases became apparent with a definite link pattern emerging. It also became apparent that something must be done to reduce the incidents of both occupational accidents and diseases and earnest efforts were started in that direction, tackling these problems at the level of foundations existing existing hospitals for almost 4 years revealed that Occupational Health being a full time task, a separate body would be necessary if the were to take contemplated shape.

With this background, birth was given to LOKMANYA MEDICAL RESEARCH CENTRE, on 28th February 1985 to concentrate on the aspect of occupational Health & Research.

In the meantime Lokmanya Hospitals at Chinchwad and Nigdi served as the service organs of the L.M.R.C, were thoroughly upgraded to keep with the pace with the latest technology.
Memorandum of Association was signed with the Swedish Collaborators for medical care and a contract was entered into with ICHC a leading Hyderabad based consultancy regarding management aspect.

LMRC also offers certain medical courses for MBBS doctors.

Thus with its service organs setting their standards to high excellence, L.M.R.C is poised to take a leap forward towards achievements of its Aims and Objectives
Mission Statement

Our mission is to make health for all a reality by promoting, preventing and providing health care, by conducting basic, applied and extra mural research in health care, by advocating for the rights of all people at all stages of their lives, and by creating an environment conductive to wholesome living.

  • Our Aims
  • Promote health in its holistic sense including its economic, social and spiritual dimensions.
  • Promotion of positive health, prevention of diseases, early diagnosis-treatment and follow up of disease and provision of healthful environment.
  • Identifying present and futures trends in health, new and emerging disease patterns and seeking joint actions with like minded organisations to meet those needs
  • Foster partnership between NGOs, Government agencies, International Agencies and other public and private organisations for undertaking action-oriented studies.
  • Provide and create a platform to share information and experiences and enhance cooperation in health education and research.
  • Our Objectives
  • To undertake Scientific Research in the area of Bio-Medical Sciences.
  • To establish, provide, promote, maintain and conduct or assist research laboratories and institutions engaged in advancement of scientific research pertaining to Occupational Health, Industrial Safety, Environmental Health and Research in indigenous Medicinal Plants.
  • To provide research facilities for carrying out basic and applied research, to conduct experiments and to undertake and carry on research in concerned systems and disciplines.
  • To promote, develop and improve scientific exchange of knowledge as well as technical co-operation between research institution with similar interests and objectives.
  • To give advice on, publish and/or otherwise promote utilisation of research results in the fields.
  • To make available to other techniques and materials developed as a result of research.
  • Our Credentials
  • Recognised as a Scientific & Industrial Research Organisation by Department of Science and Technology, Government of India.
  • Recognition as Research Centre for Indian System of Medicine & Homoeopathy by AYUSH , Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
  • Recognised by the National Safety Council Mumbai, as NAC (National Awareness and Preparedness for emergencies at Local Level) Sub center
  • Recognised by the DG, FASLI – Central Labour Institute to conduct Associated Fellowship in Industrial Health Course for Doctors
  • Recognised, as extension of BJ Medical College Teaching programme by University of Pune & Government of Maharashtra for M.D. (Med.) & M.S. (Gen. Surgery) students
  • National Board of Examination Accredited Institute for DNB in Orthopeadics, Family Medicine, General Surgery and Radiology
  • Conducts 50 various Diploma Course in Nursing Education and Medical & Paramedical faculty.
