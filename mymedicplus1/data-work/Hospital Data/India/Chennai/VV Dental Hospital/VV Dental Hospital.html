Description:

V.V.Dental Hospital was established by Dr.N.V. Arumugam, M.D.S. who is a specialist in Dental, Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery with over 20 years of experience in Dental practice. He is also a Senior Consultant at Apollo Hospital.

V.V.Dental Hospital is situated in Annanagar, Chennai. It is a multi - speciality Dental Hospital which treats all kinds of dental diseases. V.V.Dental Hospital runs regular outpatient clinics on all six days (except Sunday) of the week between 9.00 am to 12.30 pm & 5.30 to 9.00 pm.

The panel of doctors other than Dr. N. V. Arumugam are Dr. Ranjani Arumugam, Dr. Janardhan (Orthodontist), Dr. Yamini (Dental Surgeon).

V.V. Dental Hospital offers efficient, state-of-the-art dental services to people, equipped with modern dental chairs, the latest sophisticated instruments for diagnostic precision and for provision of the best dental aid to all patients.

List of Surgeries/Treatments:

Aesthetic Dentistry

  • Smile Alignment
  • Bleaching, Bonding, Laminates, Veneers.
  • The latest in metal free ceramic crowns.

Root Canal Therapy

  • Using Anthogyr and Protaper system and RVG (Radio Visiography). The advantages of RVG is instantaneous viewingof X ray films and greatly reduced radiation exposure to the patients and operaters alike.
  • Implants : Centre Pulse - Zimmer Root Form & Oraltronix Bicortical screw implants are used for replacement of single teeth as well as implant retained full dentures.
  • Full dentures using the BPS System (Biofunctional Prosthetic System) which is the most advanced denture construction system in the world.

Preventive Dentistry

  • Fluriode Application, Pit and Fissure Sealants
  • Patient Education with Cam Scope Intra Oral Camera

Bridges

  • PFM (Porcelain Fused to Metal)
  • Metal free Ceramics
  • Cast Metal

  • Orthodontist to correct irregular teeth. Ceramic/Polycarbonate brackets for Aesthetic Orthodontic Applications.
  • Orthognathic Surgery - Surgical Correction for severely malaligned teeth and jaws.
