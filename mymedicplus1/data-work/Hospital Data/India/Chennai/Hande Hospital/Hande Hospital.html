Description:

Hande hospital was started in 1984 by Dr.H.V.Hande, former Health Minister, Govt of Tamilnadu.Dr.Hande has been ( and continues to be ) in practice for the past 52 years. He is not only a family physician but also a family friend for thousands of families and has seen almost three generations grow under his care. Hande hospital has recently been expanded into a 50 bed hospital. It is being run by

Dr. H.Krishna Hande MS.,FRCS.,MCH (Plastic) FMMC. Who is a General,Plastic, Laparoscopic and Cosmetic Surgeon. He is ably assisted by his wife Dr.( Mrs ) Indira Hande MBBS., DA who looks after the administration of the hospital. HANDE HOSPITAL has been empaneled by various Companies and Health Insurance providers.
