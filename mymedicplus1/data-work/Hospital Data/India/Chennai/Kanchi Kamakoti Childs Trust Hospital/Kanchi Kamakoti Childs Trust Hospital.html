Description:

To care for this most valuable resource, a spirited journey began in the year 1979 towards caring for the generation of tomorrow. It was a proud moment of realization of a dream for the nation - the creation of CHILDS Trust Hospital. With a vision to bring sunshine in an ailing child's life, the hospital continues its relentless endeavour in bringing international healthcare standards to India combined with great warmth & hospitality.

Today, KKCTH stands as a beacon of excellence in the field of pediatric healthcare, alleviating the pain and suffering of the children. KKCTH stands tall as an adobe of excellance where children are nursed and cared for by a highly dedicated team of professionals. The happy smiles of children and parents are the greatest reward for the band of committed and persevering staff of the hospital.
