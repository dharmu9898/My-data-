Description:

B.R.S Hospital started in July 1992 as a multi speciality center, offering the best possible facilities and services to the patients at an affordable cost. B.R.S Hospital is more than 15 years old and is growing from strength to strength.

The Hospital is situated in the heart of the Chennai city close to Gemini flyover, located in quite ambient atmosphere. This makes it easily accessible from any part of the city in the event of emergency.

The hospital occupies 35,000 sq.ft. of area, which has been carefully alloted to the medical and surgical departments. The hospital is managed by a set of dedicated doctors, who are present round the clock. The staff are highly trained to act promptly during emergency situations.
