Description:

The Guest Hospital has been functioning since 1980 and has carved a niche in the field of Urology, Nephrology and Kidney Transplant Services for the last three decades. We have a group of well qualified highly committed band of professionals to head all our specialty sections.

We have excellent surgical teams in the specialties of Gastroenterology, Plastic and Cosmetic Surgery, General Surgery, Orthopedics, Ophthalmology, Oncology, Pediatric Surgery Obstetrics & Gynecology, Vascular surgery.

Highlights

Our operation theatre complex of three major operation theatres and one minor operation theatre is well equipped to handle efficiently all major surgeries. Our minimally invasive centre provides the latest treatment using laparoscopy and lasers to ensure prompt recovery and minimal postoperative discomfort. Our most modern nine bedded intensive care unit recently completely renovated in 2005 provides the best possible care for the critically ill patients.

Our aim since inception is to provide state of the art quality healthcare at an affordable cost so as to bring the benefits of modern medicine within the reach of everyone. In today’s world of ever increasing cost of healthcare our aim is more relevant today than ever in the past.
