Description:

Sri Jayadeva Institute of Cardiology is a Government owned Autonomous Institute and is offering super specialty treatment to Cardiac Patients and also conducting postgraduate courses in DM/MCh. It has got the State of the Art equipments in the form of 4 Cathlabs, 4 Operation Theaters, Non Invasive Laboratories and 24 hours ICU facilities with 540 beds.

  • Our Vision: redefinine the way cardiovascular care is delivered
  • Our Mission: Improve clinical outcomes for cardiac patients through a physician driven, patient focused approach
  • Our Values: people, partnership, quality, integrity
