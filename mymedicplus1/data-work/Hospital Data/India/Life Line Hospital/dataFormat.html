Life Line Hospital is one of the best superspeciality hospital which is situated in the Dehradun valley- the capital of the Uttaranchal state of India.
The Life Line Hospital provides patients care in field of plastic and cosmetic surgery urology, gynecology, obstetrics, orthopedics and pathology apart from general surgery and medicine. The Life Line Hospital is dedicated to the highest standards of patient care and surgical excellence within a safe and friendly environment and the highest professional standards, our patient care is personalized and confidential. The hospital maintains exceptional quality of care and is staffed by a well-trained and experienced health care team.

The Life Line Hospital has an enviable reputation as one of the leading centers for Plastic Surgery . The Hospital is serving 100's of peoples from india and other countries every year, and we are proud to say that we are expertly equipped to exceed their expectations.
Life Line Hospital's highly experienced doctors are specialized in taking care of Cosmetic Surgery, Reconstructive Surgery, Surgical Procedure to Increase Height(Limb Lengthening), Urinary Stone Disease (Endourology & Lithotripsy), Prostate Disorders, Infertility, Gynecology (Uterine & ovarian Disorders), Joint Replacement (Hip, Knee, Shoulder Joint), Resurfacing (Arthroplasty), Spinal Surgery, Disc Replacement, Spinal Fusion, Decompression for spinal stenosis, Endo Urology, Orthopedics and Pathology Investigations.

Dr Mohit Goyal ( Chief Cosmetic Surgeon )

Dr Mohit Goyal is the chief Cosmetic Surgeon of The Life Line Hospital and is very experienced and specializes in Reconstructive surgery.

He was born and raised in Dehradun and joined MBBS course in 1989 . Dr Mohit Goyal completed M. S General Surgery and Plastic Surgery MCH course from King George Medical College Lucknow in 1999 and got Awarded with Gold Medal in studies.

Dr Mohit Goyal joined Plastic Surgery Department as senior resident surgeon in Safdarjang Hospital, New Delhi for a period of one year, This training provided him with knowledge in various aspects of Cosmetic and Reconstructive Surgery.

Since after then Dr Mohit Goyal practicing and serving peoples of India and Aboard as chief Cosmetic and Reconstructive Surgeon in Life Line Hospital, Dehradun.

Dr Sanjay Goyal ( Chief Surgeon Urology)

Dr Sanjay Goyal passed class 12th in 1980 and joined MBBS course in LLRM Medical College Meerut. He completed his graduation in 1986 and joined masters course in surgery in the same year.

After finishing his post graduation in surgery in 1989 he worked as a senior resident in urology, general surgery and pediatric urology unit at UCMS and GTB Hospital for a period of two years .
In the year 1992 he joined MCh course in urology in PGIMER Chandigarh. After Completed the course of MCH Urology he joined the Moolchand Hospital New Delhi and Kailash Hospital & Research Center Noida as a senior Urologist and in 1995 till 2002.

In the year 2002 Dr Sanjay Goyal started urology department in CMI Hospital Dehradun.In the year 2005 Dr Sanjay Goyal started Life Line Hospital and been practicing the endioscopic urology, Infertility and sexual sexual dysfunction treatment.

Dr Sanjay Goyal is one of the few qualified urologist in the Uttaranchal and he has been awarded with the highest degree of urology in the year 1994 at PGIMER Chandigarh.
