Description:
Apollo Gleneagles Hospital, Kolkata, a landmark in the healthcare map of Eastern India can be defined as the realization of a dream – providing healthcare of international level within the reach of every individual which is the mission of Dr Prathap C Reddy, Founder & Executive Chairman, Apollo Hospitals Group.

It combines the expertise of two premium healthcare providers, which have carved a niche of their own.

Apollo Hospitals – India’s first Corporate Hospital is the largest private hospital group in Asia. With 44 hospitals spread in India and abroad, Apollo Hospitals today is synonymous to healthcare of international quality. The group also has significant presence in the field of Pharmacy, Neighbourhood Clinics, healthcare education including nursing colleges, virtual university, hospital management courses, IT outsourcing, Telemedicine, Insurance and so on.

ParkwayHealth is a wholly owned subsidiary of Singapore-listed Parkway Holdings Limited. It is Asia's largest healthcare organization with an extensive network of hospitals and integrated healthcare facilities in the region
The Parkway Group manages hospitals under the brand names Gleneagles and Mt. Elizabeth and accounts for 70% of the private healthcare market in Singapore. It owns East Shore, Gleneagles and Mount Elizabeth Hospitals in Singapore and a network of seven hospitals in the region.
The group has its operations in Singapore, Malaysia, Brunei, China and India.

The Apollo Gleneagles pool of talent has earned international acclaim and the increasing number of patients traveling from abroad for availing treatment is an adequate proof.

This 405-bedded multispecialty tertiary care hospital provides the harmonious teamwork of best expertise, latest technology, state-of-the-art equipments, dedicated care and warm hospitality which facilitates rapid recovery. The meticulously planned ambience is designed to create a health space conducive to the healing process. Thus it is of little wonder that this is the most preferred healthcare destination of East and beyond.

Doctors

Doctors form the life-blood of any healthcare organization and here Apollo Gleneagles Hospitals, Kolkata scores with élan. Almost all Consultants attached to this hospital has spent many years abroad, in some of the best medical institutions of the world like Glasgow, Edinburgh, US Universities and so on. When they made up their mind to settle down in India and serve the country, they chose Apollo Gleneagles Hospitals, Kolkata for its world class settings, latest technologies, comfortable working ambience and the continuous flow of challenging cases from all over the region.

Nurses

Nursing care plays an equally important role in the recovery of patients. Since its inception, Apollo Group has put adequate stress on Education as is evident in its mission statement. Keeping true to its commitment, the group introduced the Apollo Hospitals Educational Trust. The Apollo College of Nursing are managed by this Trust. It eqips the girls and boys with nursing expertise of international standard and ensure a steady stream of trained nurses in the nation-wide network of hospitals of Apollo. A systematic training on all aspects of nursing science, health administration, ethics and legal aspects of medical cases ensure that the girls receive a truly international training.

Add on to this the urge to commitment to provide the tender loving care to every patient, every time….

Equipments

Medical technology is perhaps the one of the fastest evolving fields of technology. At Apollo Gleneagles Hospitals, the latest gadget and equipment is the norm. But that is not all. Correct installation and utilization of these sophisticated equipments is essential for the accuracy of therapy. Regular training sessions, refresher courses and workshops are organized for doctors, nurses and paramedics who operate these equipments on day-to-day basis. The Biomedical department works round the clock to provide the necessary support to ensure that the thousands of sophisticated continue to function smoothly and accurately, taking care of patients’ lives in the best possible way.
