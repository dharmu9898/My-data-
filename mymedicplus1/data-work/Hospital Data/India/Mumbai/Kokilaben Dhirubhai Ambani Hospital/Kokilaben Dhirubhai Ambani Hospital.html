Description:

The Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital & Medical Research Institute, which has become operational in the first week of 2009, is one of the country's premier healthcare initiatives in the private sector. Located at Four Bungalows, Andheri - West in the bustling heart of India's financial metropolis Mumbai, the sprawling facility will support specialization across the realm of clinical medicine.

In concept and design, the new venture has been inspired by the values and vision of our legendary founder, Late Shri Dhirubhai Ambani, who believed that India 's private sector had a critical part to play in redrawing the healthcare map of the country.

Shri Dhirubhai Ambani believed - "A hospital is not a place where money and profit is important. Every single life saved or a patient cured would give me more satisfaction than crores of rupees of profit". (28 Dec 1932 - forever).

Highlights

Healthcare, for Shri Dhirubhai, was not just about expensive diagnostics or the latest in medical research and technology. It was as much about the human dimension, the values and processes that determine the character of a medical institution, and the quality of care and services it offers.
In keeping with his idea, Kokilaben Hospital will pay special attention to the qualitative dimension of healthcare delivery, even as it pursues the goal of leadership in knowledge and excellence in the choice of personnel and technology.

Vision Statement

We aim to be a global healthcare institution that combines the best in medical treatment with strong ethical principles and a culture of care and compassion.
