Description:

Laksh-Deep Hospital was established on the 16th of April 1989, to cater to the growing needs of the population of Vashi and other surrounding nodes of NaviMumbai.The main feature of this hospital is All Facilites Under ONE Roof.

We started as a small nursing home and have blossomed into a multi-disciplinary hospital with facilities for all routine and advanced medical needs. We are today one of the premier hospitals of NaviMumbai, catering to all strata of society. We have a bed-strength of 35+ and a consultant strength of 45+, involving all branches of medicine and superspecialities like Cardiology, Neuro-surgery, Plastic & Cosmetic surgery, Pediatric surgery, Cosmetic Dentistry, etc.

Highlights

Our multi-diagnostic centre comprises well-equipped Pathology laboratory with blood-gas analyzer, cell counter, etc, x-ray, and Sonography with colour doppler.

We have an in-house 24 hrs chemist.State-of-the-art ICCU is equipped with ventilators, central monitoring, etc. and is managed by Cardiologists.Urology centre has Lithotriptor, Uroflometry, endoscopic surgery.

Our Aesthetic Centre has facilities for improvement in facial aesthetics, and will involve Cosmetic Dentistry, Plastic Surgery, Skin, Endocrinology, Physiotherapy, etc.Our consultants reside in NaviMumbai itself, thus being easily available in times of emergency.
