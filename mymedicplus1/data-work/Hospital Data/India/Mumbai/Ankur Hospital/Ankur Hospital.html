Description:

ANKUR (syn: "bud") was established on 4th May 1975. It was the only hospital in Kandivli run by an Obstetrician & Gynecologist. It was from those days that it has been a pioneer hospital for healthcare for women.

It has always been the motto of "ANKUR" to practice the Best of Medicine prevalent in that period. It has always been a leading hospital with firsts in many areas. In Brief "ANKUR" is a bud of knowledge which will never stop growing.

It was the first hospital in this part of Mumbai to be performing Laparoscopy, utilize Cardio-tocography (Fetal Monitor) and offering Pre-Natal Diagnostic Techniques like CVS, Amniocentesis, etc. "ANKUR" always believed in team work, which tradition also continues today.
