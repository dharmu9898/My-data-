Description:

The Hospital takes honour of performing first Heart surgery and Heart Inasive procedures(Angiography/ Plasty/ Stenting) in Navi Mumbai.The Hospital has recently installed Spiral CT Scanner and MRI

Taking the above facts into consideration, with a noble ambition, Dr. S.R. Tarlekar and Dr. (Mrs.) Anita. S. Tarlekar put in untiring efforts and conceptualized the existence of Shushrusha Heart Care Centre and Speciality Hospital, a super-speciality cardiac care hospital in Navi Mumbai which caters to the medical needs of the residents of Navi Mumbai, Raigad, Thane and adjoining districts and provides them a healthy life.

Highlights

The major Cardiac theatre is designed to carry out all cardiac and neuro-surgeries. It has the most advanced SARN’S Heart Lung machine, Honolux operating light source, Fabius 2 Anaesthesia machine, electrically driven ventilator and Electronic vaporizers, space lab multi-para monitors with invasive pressure and electric operating table. The second major operation theatre for all surgeries is equipped with two Boyles machines with vaporizers, pulse oxymeters, multipara monitors. All operation theatres are supported by central sterilising department with ETO Sterilizer, Steam Sterilizer, Flash Sterilizer etc.

This 10 bedded unit stands as a strong back-up for the Cardiac - Operative cases as well as acute complications in various facilities. Equipped with the state-of-art Multipara monitors, defibri llator, ventilators etc. This also has the expertise of medical & paramedical staff to take care of all critically ill patients.

Pathology Lab:

The hospital is also equipped with a well-established pathology unit which carries out all routine and advanced pathological investigations and prompt report generation which provides a strong support to the services of the hospital and makes the procedure of diagnosis and treatment accurate and faster. Dialysis Unit:

This unit is set up as a strong back-up force, which content three modern dialysis machines, for the patients who suffer renal failure due to any ailments. With good expertise of paramedical staff, this unit is ready to cater to such patients round-the-clock.
