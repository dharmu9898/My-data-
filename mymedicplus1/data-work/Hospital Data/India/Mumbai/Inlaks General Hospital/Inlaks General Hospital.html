Description:

Inlaks General Hospital at Chembur is a medical Institution with a difference. A medical setup, which aims at high class, dedicated management of human malady. A Multi-speciality hospital with extensive and highly technical medical relief methodology extends help to the poorest of poor and richest of rich - both equally with personalized care, attention and loyalty.

A six-story structure providing facilities of advanced medical techniques and management of dire emergencies with exceptional speed, accuracy and devotion. Very valuable lives are thus taken out of crisis and saved. All departments of value to the population at large are working round the clock and operation theatres are incessantly functioning to provide ready surgical intervention at the shortest notice.

This hospital does not believe in delay in medical relief.Doctors of repute are available at all times, day and night and dedicate their physical professional and intellectual resources to alleviate human sufferings to the best of their capabilities and expertise.

Laproscopic surgery for a variety of complicated surgical problems has now been developed in this hospital and a total thrust has been given in this direction.Artificial Kidney Dialysis is a very strong point and Hallmark of this institution wherein the poor sufferer of renal damage is taken care of, in the utmost sympathetic and economical manner.
