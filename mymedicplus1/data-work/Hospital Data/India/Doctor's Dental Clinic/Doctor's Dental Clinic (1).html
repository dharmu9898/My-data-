Doctor's Dental Clinic is an advanced dental clinic, located in Thiruvananthapuram (formerly known as Trivandrum), Kerala, India, Dental Clinic in Trivandrum, Keralawith all state-of-the-art ultra modern facilities in Dentistry. We provide specialized service in cosmetic dentistry and dental implants. Foreigners, Non Resident Indians (NRIs) frequently visit our clinic for dental treatment. Through our quality service, dedication, and hard work, we have earned high reputation among our patients. Visit our dental clinic and feel the difference

Doctor's Dental Clinic is highly acclaimed for its state-of-the-art equipments, the dental chairs at our clinic are highly advanced, automatic and are with multi positional facility for the comfort of patient. X-ray machine facility within the room helps in avoiding patient inconvenience. The clinic equipped with intraoral digital camera with two simultaneous view ports help patients to get a clear picture of the the treatment being done.

Using the latest innovations and technologies of dentistry, aided with ultra modern equipments and state-of-the-art dental chairs our clinic shows that we value results by exceeding the expectations of the people we serve, as well as the expectations we set for ourselves.

Digital x-ray system maximizes the diagnostic value of all intraoral radiography techniques and produces exceptionally clear images with outstanding sharpness and maximum detail. This system senses the onset of the x-ray exposure and automatically acquires and downloads the image data to a computer for the purposes of real-time display and review.

Intra-oral digital camera computer system gives you a live picture of what's inside your mouth and the photographs of what your smile looks like before and after the treatment. Features: High Resolution 760,000 Pixel SONY COLOR CCD Sensor. Adjustable Lens & Auto Focus, 2 cm Up To Infinity. Composite Video Output To Monitor, Split Image & Freeze. Four Pure White High Intensity Cold LED Illumination. Image Freeze Button On Hand Piece. Split Image, Freeze & Capture Compatibility"

Dental x-rays are one of the tools that we have which help us detect problems while they are still small before they are even visible to the naked eye. X-ray machine facility within the room, is just one more example of our clinic's commitment to bringing the best of dentistry to our patients.
