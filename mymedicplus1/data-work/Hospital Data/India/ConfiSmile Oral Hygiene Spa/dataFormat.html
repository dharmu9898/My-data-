Cosmetic Dentistry

ConfiSmile Teeth Whitening

May be you always wanted a beautiful white smile, or your have yellowed your teeth over time, or you are not happy with staining that result from tobacco or drinking coffee, tea or cola. What ever your reason for wanting white teeth, you are not alone. Here we have a solution to give you a pearly white to boost your confidence and self-esteem that comes from a great smile. We simply reverse years of everyday staining and yellowing by whitening in just 30 MINUTES.
A change of just 2 or 3 shades can make a noticeable difference in almost anyone's smile. The goal should be to achieve your individual natural Whiteness by still looking natural in a safe manner.
Laser Teeth Whitening
Your smile says a lot about you. The staining on your teeth due to various reasons as tobacco, drinking tea, coffee etc. is making you self-conscious. Then laser teeth whitening is a safe and easy way to lightning teeth and restoring their natural whiteness by accelerating the gel put on your teeth by the LED (light emitting diode) light. A change of 2-3 shades can make an appreciable difference in almost anyone's life.
Tooth Jewellery
For the royalty in you, ConfiSmile offers elegant tooth jewels. Your richest possession will be on permanent display. Our ConfiSmile Doctors take pride in sparking your smile with Swarovski crystal on your tooth. It is truly an amazing and joyful moment in styling your smile at ConfiSmile.
Esthetic Restoration
Esthetic restorations are natural looking & permanent they are done in a single visit. This type of restoration is more convenient & healthy as it uses ceramic materials to restore teeth to their natural strength, function & beauty and they are chemically bond to the teeth.
Veneers
Veneers are an advanced treatment option in the field of cosmetic dentistry. It is the one of the best way of achieving a perfect smile. Dental veneers are ultra thin shells of ceramic or composite material which are bonded to the front of teeth in order to cover up discolouration, cracks, chipped and broken teeth [Diastema] and also to change the shape and size of your teeth.
Depigmentation of Gums
Is a procedure used in cosmetic dentistry to remove block spots or patches on the gums caused by excessive melanin. A clinical assessment is carried out by our experts to customize the treatment and give best possible results by surgical, chemical or laser oblation techniques.
Tooth contouring & Reshaping
Is a painless and effective method to correct minor imperfection as small chips, irregular tooth shapes & length and smoothing out bulges. It is usually done to improve appearance by creating more harmony or balance in the look of the smile.
Re-Contouring of Gums
When there is lot of gum tissues around upper teeth it is called 'gummy smile' which makes teeth appear short & unattractive. This cosmetic procedure helps correct gummy smile by sculpting & shaping excessive & uneven gum tissues creating a natural & even smile line.
Dental Implant
It is a miracle of replacement of missing teeth with titanium implants. This complex technique requires the most sophisticated diagnosis of treatment planning which is provided by our experts. The final result has an amazing, natural tooth like appearance restoring both form of function.
Orthodontic Treatment (Braces)
Is the most efficient & effective ways of correcting Malaligned teeth. Our in house specialists are masters in their skills and produce visible results b/w 12-24 months depending on the severity of the cases. This treatment gives you a beautiful smile.

Basic Oral Treatment

Teeth Scaling
A dental cleaning every six months helps to keep your teeth & gums healthy & reduce your risk of heart disease & stroke. Cleaning is one of the essential components of good oral Hygiene performed to smoothen the tooth surfaces by removing plaque and tartar that have built on your teeth over time.
Teeth Polishing
Polishing not only brings shine but it is a preventive procedure considered to be the first line of defence against dental problems. It delays the progression of gums disease. It gives glistening appearance to your teeth.
Restoration (Cavity Filling)
It is also called filling. It is the repair of damaged or decayed tooth & restoring it back to its normal shape, appearance and function with a choice of filling materials.
Root Canal Treatment
The goal of root canal treatment is to improve the health of your damaged tooth. During the treatment the infected pulp is removed from root canals through the opening in the crown and filled later on. It takes more than a single visit for the procedure to complete.
Crowns
Crowns strengthen and protect the weak tooth structures and improve the appearance of your teeth. They can be made from different materials E.g. Metals, porcelain fused to metal & ceramic.
Bridges
Bridges are false teeth designed to 'bridge' the gap creating by one or more missing teeth. A bridge is made up of two crowns for teeth on either side of gap and false teeth i.e. gap between them.
Complete Denture
If you have lost all your natural teeth, whether from periodontal disease, tooth decay or injury. Complete denture can replace your missing teeth and of course your smile. They are cost effective and removable and are made from various types of materials.
Partial Denture
They are for patients who have some missing teeth on a particular arch. They are cost effective & can be removed & reinserted by the patient.
Extraction
The main goal of modern dentistry is prevention of tooth. All possible means should be taken to preserve & maintain teeth but sometimes it is necessary to remove a tooth e.g. very severe decay, advanced periodontal disease, orthodontic correction, fractured teeth, and impacted 3rd molars. Our spa has experts who remove the tooth painlessly.
Post & Core with Root Canal Treatment
A post & core is a metal component of an extensive tooth repair. It is usually recommended where there is inadequate support for a crown after root canal treatment. It acts as an anchor for the placement of a crown.

Night Guard
A night guard is a plastic device that covers both upper and lower teeth. It is usually worn by patients who have the habit of tooth grinding. A night guard helps prevent TMJ & Clenching disorders.

Infection Control
We at the Confismile Oral Hygiene Spas follow all the disinfection & sterilization procedures needed for infection control. The steps are followed for each and every patient. It helps prevent transmission of infectious diseases like Hepatitis, HIV Virus Etc. We meet & exceed all standards for infection control in Oral Hygiene.
We use disposables like needles, syringes, glasses, tissue, gloves, patient drapes, facemask, tips etc to be thrown after single use
At our ConfiSmile Spas the following steps are followed to ensure a strict sterilization protocol

Cleaning Disinfection Autoclaving
All non-disposable instruments are scrubbed thoroughly to remove any blood or debris then washed with sterile water. Then instruments are kept in disinfections solution e.g. (korsolex) for min of 30 minutes. Then washed with sterile water & dried with sterile gauge These sterile instruments are then packed in sterlization pouches & vacuum sealed &kept for autoclaving.
Our sterilization equipment are regularly checked to insure its effectiveness in killing pathogenic organisms
Hazardous material clearly labeled.
Hand piece are also sterilized after use.
All surfaces are disinfected prior to each patient's use.
If you have any question about our infection control do not hesitate to ask. The professionals at our spa would be glad to clear your doubts.
