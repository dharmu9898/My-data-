Dr. R.N. KalraWe take care, with the hands of compassion. Medicine is the divine hand of the almighty, and we endeavour to redeem this benefaction. Kalra Hospital began its journey into the zone of health care, on August 19, 1990. Kalra Hospital Sri Ram Cardio-Thoracic & Neurosciences Centre is committed to providing high-quality comprehensive specialist services, including tertiary services, focused on meeting the health care needs of the local population. Our institute is also making relentless endeavours, in order to discover new horizons in the zone of quality health care.

Extensive programmes in medical education and research support our patient care, offering virtually every medical expertise, treatment and diagnostic tool. Our mission is to provide the highest quality, compassionate care at a reasonable cost through a physician-led team of diverse people, working together in clinical practice, education and research, in a unified multi-campus system.

The dynamic environment at our institute unifies the energy and enthusiasm of people, working together for the best interest of patients. The hallmark of this team environment is competence, mutual respect and professionalism, creating an atmosphere conducive to constant learning and innovation. As one of the most respected medical centres in the region, Kalra Hospital & SRCNC has set a standard of excellence for the collaborative approach to patient care that has thrived for more than a century. This high standard lives on today, through team members who directly and indirectly contribute to patient care. Our panel of doctors hold themselves accountable, to this timeless standard, upon which diverse ideas, opinions, perspectives, values, and most importantly, individuals thrive.

Kalra Hospital & SRCNC is ISO-9001 certified, 106 bedded multi-specialty institute. The hospital has the most sophisticated Trauma Section (Emergency Support System), with drive-in casualty. It's highly efficient Intensive Care Units (Adult/Paediatric), the Heart Command, and the three Operation Theatres are fully equipped with the latest engineered technology. It also has a technically superior Cardiac O.T. & a spacious labour room

List of Surgeries/Treatments:

Cardio-Thoracic Surgery:

Including Adult Cardiac Surgery, Minimally Invasive Cardiac Surgery, Endoscopic Vein Harvest, Adult Thoracic Surgery, Video Assisted Thoracic Surgery, and Paediatric Cardiac & Thoracic Surgery.

General Surgery:

Surgeries include abdominal aneurysm repair, abdominal aortic bypass, appendicectomy, carotid endarterectomy (stroke prevention), artery bypass of the extremities, bowel resections, prostatectomy, gall bladder procedures; and treatment of benign and malignant breast disease, just to a name a few.One of the most frequently practiced specialities is orthopaedic medicine. From arthroscopic procedures to total joint replacement to spinal surgery, the hospital’s orthopaedic surgeons provide the latest methods available to care for injuries, and diseases affecting the musculoskeletal system that includes bones, joints, and the muscles that move them.

Nephrology:

Including minimally invasive and non-invasive surgery, as well as shock wave and laser lithotripsy.

Orthopaedics:

Services we offerOur orthopaedic surgery team offers a wide range of surgical and nonsurgical treatment options for bone and joint diseases, and injuries.Joint reconstruction and replacement

Joint reconstruction and replacement of the hip and knee; our experts have experience using many different types of advanced prosthetic devices for joint replacement.Corrective surgery of the foot, ankle, wrist, shoulder, elbow, and hand.Care for children and adolescents

Paediatric orthopaedics, including specializations in sports medicine, scoliosis, spinal deformities, and other conditions for children and adolescents.Therapeutic servicesWe also offer physical medicine and rehabilitation services, including physical therapy, and occupational therapy to help patients restore motion and strength.Sports medicineWe can treat many sports-related injuries using minimally invasive techniques.
