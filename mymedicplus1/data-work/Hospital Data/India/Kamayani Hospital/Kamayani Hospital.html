Description:

Established by a renowned group of Agra providing medical facilities since 1991.The ONLY centrally air-condition hospital in Agra with 24 hour's power supply backup. Situated on 4 lane National Highway No. 2 on an easy approach, thereby providing fast access in case of emergencies. Under control of honest, ethical & dedicated management . Existing covered area more than 65,000 sq. feet. In house medical / chemist shop, canteen, STD, PCO facility under control of hospital management.

Kamayani Hospital was started due to foresight and vision of Dr. Munishwar Gupta, Founder of Kamayani Patients Care (India) Ltd. He realize the need for a hospital Cancer and decided to start one in Agra.

And on 2 March , 2000 the day when his dream com true. The 100 bedded hospital called Kamayani Hospital was inaugurated by Shri Rajendra Singh (Rajju Bhaiya).

In 3 years, the hospital has grown from strength to strength and today with 100 beds, it is a major centre for quality medical care in this region.

Our Cancer therapy programme, which has developed over past 3 years, is based on total cancer therapy which not only include extensive investigative procedure, treatment through surgery, chemotherapy and radiotherapy but has vital element of counselling. Kamayani Hospital which was set up in 2 March , 2000 , counsels the cancer patients and their relatives by giving mental strength to face the disease.

All types of Brain and Tumour Surgery

These surgeries include excising, or cutting out, brain tumors and removing ruptured discs in the spine, an operation known as a laminectomy. The use of specialized imaging equipment, such as computed tomography (CT) and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), permits to identify the exact location of some tumors, making surgery to remove these growths more precise and less harmful to surrounding healthy tissue.
