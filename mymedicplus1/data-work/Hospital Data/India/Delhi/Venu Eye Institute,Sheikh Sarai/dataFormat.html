Description:

A unique comprehensive three tier quality eye care delivery network comprising of a state-of-art Referral and Teaching Eye Hospital in Delhi, 5 Satellite Hospitals, 25 Primary Clinics and 15 Community Based Programmes covering 9 States in North India, serving over 3,00,000 patients and rehabilitating 300-500 persons every year. A one-of-its kind Eye Bank processing, coordination and distribution center helping restore sight of corneally blind through corneal transplant.

Quality eye eare at the doorstep of the patient irrespective of his ability to pay. A dream seen 25 years ago, today is a symbol of excellence in eye care in India. That’s Venu Eye Institute & Research Centre with it’s over 200 dedicated and qualified family serving the eye care needs of the nation.

Thank you Dr. R. K. Seth for igniting the passion to bring the joy of sight for all.

Highlights

Mission

  • To provide modern preventive, curative and rehabilitative eye care services to people who have no access to healthcare facilities.
  • To take eye care services to the doorstep of the patient, rather than wait for the patient to come to the hospital.
  • To provide these services to one and all, regardless of caste, colour or creed, whether they can pay, pay a little or not pay at all.
  • To provide the services with humility and care, always mindful of the dignity of the human spirit.
  • To create an environment of professional growth for the serving team.
