Description:

Delhi Heart & Lung Institute is a ‘Centre of Excellence’ specializing in Cardio-Pulmonary services situated at the T-Junction of Panchkuian Road and Mandir Marg. Hospital adheres to the European standard as it is certified by ISO 9001:2000 by Intertek which is European company and is under the process of getting NABL (National Accreditation Board of Laboratory) and NABH (National Accreditation Board of Hospital). It is 100 bedded centrally air-conditioned hospital which offers state-of-the-art facilities for consultation, investigation, early diagnosis and management in the fields of Interventional Cardiology, Cardiac Rhythm disorders, Paediatric Cardiology, Adult and Paediatric Cardio-Thoracic Surgery, Pulmonology, Pulmonary Surgery and Sleep Disorders.

The institute’s cutting-edge core competencies are well sustained by an efficient collaboration with support services like Emergency Medicine, Biochemistry, Clinical Pathology, Blood Transfusion, Telemedicine and Facilities Management adhering to world-class modern standards, making the institute comparable with any other setup of its kind in the world.

Delhi Heart & Lung Institute offers a comprehensive Executive Health Check Program which includes an array of heart and lung diagnostics as well as laboratory investigations.

Highlights

The Delhi Heart and Lung Institute offers the most comprehensive array of services to patients and families touched by Heart and Lung Diseases .

A diagnosis of latherosclerosis, coronary, rheumatic, congenital, myocarditis, angina, arrhythmia or other Heart and Lung Diseases brings many daily challenges. The Society is committed to providing support and guidance to patients and the health professionals who care for them.

The institute has fully equipped modern operation theatres which form the Backbone of the centre. Advanced and innovative techniques are peformed regularly keeping pace with the latest developments.

The institute’s cardiac surgery unit brings together the skill-sets of highly competent surggeons with state of - the art equipmentand truly exceptional post-operative care.
