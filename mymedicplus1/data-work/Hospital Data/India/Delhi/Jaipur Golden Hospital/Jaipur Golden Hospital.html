Description:

The Hospitals philosophy is `To provide World Class Quality Health Care Services to all sections of the society' with emphasis on philanthropy. To fulfil this objective the hospital has highly qualified & dedicated medical professionals, paramedics and managerial support staff who regularly enhance their skills to the most contemporary world standards.

The Hospital conducts special training sessions for all level of staff, an ongoing exercise, for enrichment of working knowledge and thus resulting in increasing effectiveness.

Jaipur Golden Hospital attaches a lot of importance in Continuing Medical Education programs. Workshops and Seminars are regularly conducted; many of these are attended by doctors from different parts of India and abroad.

Medical Expertise

With over 100 hospital based Consultants; which is a unique feature of the hospital, there is always an experienced specialist available to initiate treatment withoutdelay. The various specialities covered are Cardiology, Cardiothoracic Surgery, Neurology, Neurosurgery, Orthopaedics, Oncology, Ophthalmology, Rheumatology, Endocrinology, ENT,Gastroenterology, Paediatrics, Paediatric Surgery, Paediatric Neurology, Urology, Nephrology, Dermatology, Dentistry, Plastic Surgery, Gynaecology, Pulmonology, Psychiatry, General Medicine & General Surgery.

Highlights

Cutting Edge Technology In Diagnostics

The diagnostic facilities offered by the hospital are most comprehensive which include Laboratory services, Imaging, Cardiology, Neurology and Pulmonology.

Jaipur Golden Hospital has fully automated Laboratory Medicine Department. The Laboratory services are available round the clock. The services include biochemistry, haematology, microbiology, serology, histopathology, transfusion medicine and RIA. The Laboratory offers over 500 different types of tests some of which are exclusive. The Laboratory also offers an emergency / Stat menu of tests with a very short turn around time. Stringent quality assurance exercises in the department ensures reliable and high quality test results.

Imaging is a key function of the diagnostic facility at the hospital. The department offers a gamut of services ranging from General Radiography, Ultra Sonography, Bone Densitometry to high end services like Spiral CT Scanning, and Digital Subtraction Angiography (DSA) along with interventional procedures.

Technology Upgrade

The hospital keeps upgrading its technology by acquiring new state-of-the-art diagnostic & therapeutic equipment. To enhance the specialized services in invasive cardiology, the hospital has equipped itself with GE – Innova 2000 cath lab machine for better visibility of blood vessels, in the procedures carried out the Radial lounge, the first of its kind in India, is a unique concept for carrying out canonry Angiography and Angioplasty procedure. In support of the services at the hospital a new state-of-the-art AKD (Advanced kidney dialyses) department has been set up to provide best of care for dialyses patients.

Surgery

The Hospital’s main thrust areas in surgery are minimal access surgery, Neurosurgery, Cardiothoracic surgery and Orthopaedic surgery. Operation theatres (O.T) are dedicated for these surgeries. Operation theatre is ultramodern and complete with modular units and attached recovery room, seminar air flow, total environment control and an alert team of trained medical and nursing staff. An Acute Post Operative Pain Service managed by the Anaesthetists is also available.
