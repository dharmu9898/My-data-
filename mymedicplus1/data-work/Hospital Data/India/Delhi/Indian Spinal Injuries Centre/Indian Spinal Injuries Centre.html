Description:

The Indian Spinal Injuries Centre has been set up in collaboration with the Government of Italy, through San Raffaele Hospital, Milan, along with support from the Government of India. Today it is a model institution comparable to the best in the world. A 153-bed hospital, it has 30 beds reserved for poor patients, many of whom cannot afford to pay. No one is turned away due to lack of funds and there is no discrimination in the quality of medical care. With the latest state-of-the-art diagnostics and surgical equipment and a highly qualified team of doctors, surgeons, physiotherapists, who have all been trained at the world's best institutions, ISIC has become a major referral centre. Its patients come not only from across India, but from all over the world.

It is also probably the only hospital in the world designed by a patient. Major Ahluwalia was able to use his own experience to provide the architects unique insights into the problems faced by patients and help to find optimum solutions. It is the only hospital in India that is totally barrier-free and has the unique advantage of providing everything that a patient would need under one roof.

Highlights

Our Vision, Mission & Values

  • To develop the highest standard quality service delivery program for patients with spinal injuries and other spinal ailments.
  • To become a globally affiliated center for the development for the highest quality human resources, by setting up world-class education and research facilities, with a focus on graduate and post-graduate courses in Rehabilitation Sciences.
  • To apply the latest medical technology and explore all possibilities in uncharged areas so that answers to the unknown are discovered.

Our Mission

  • To become a center of excellence and an apex referral center in Asia.
  • To set up collaborations with other leading institutes from around the world and participate in global research in the field of spinal injuries.
  • To serve the needs of humanity as a whole and deliver the highest quality care to the poorest of the poor.

Our Core Values & Commitments

  • To strive to bring awareness in the field of disability and lobby with policy makers to introduce suitable legislation for prevention of disability and enforcement to the Equal Opportunity Bill.
  • We will continue to put strong emphasis on manpower development through education programs with a focus on graduate and post-graduate courses in Rehabilitation Sciences.
