Highlights

The First Magnet Hospital in Kansas.

On Wednesday, December 20, 2006, The University of Kansas Hospital became the first hospital in Kansas to attain the coveted Magnet™ designation. The Department of Nursing began working toward this goal in 2002.

Highest Ranking of Any Area Hospital In Any Specialty.

KANSAS CITY, Kan. – The University of Kansas Hospital has been ranked 30th in the nation for heart care and heart surgery in the new U.S. News and World Report’s “Best Hospitals” issue. It is the highest ranked heart program in the area and the highest ranking of any area hospital in any specialty areas ranked by the magazine.
