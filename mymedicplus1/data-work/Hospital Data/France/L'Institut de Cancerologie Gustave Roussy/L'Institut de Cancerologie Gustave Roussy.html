Description:
The IGR's vocation is to provide a very high level of expertise within the context of European cancerology and give patients access to the most appropriate treatment. With a single objective: to offer effective human and technical solutions to those suffering from or predisposed to cancer, using new preventive, predictive and curative drugs, developing support care and new support services (Espace de Rencontre et d'Information, - Meeting and Information Area -, thematic information meetings, development of networking with health professionals close to the patient's home).
