Description:

From its foundation, the Hospital Universitario San Vicente de Paul has been and open medical center whose philosophy is oriented to patients care, students’ education and research to improve the life standards of our society.

The Hospital and its staff give you and your family a heartfelt welcome. All our human, technologic and physical resources are at your disposal, so that with their help, you will recover your health. Energy and Faith!

The history of the Hospital Universitario San Vicente de Paul dates back to 1912 when a group of philanthropists from Antioquia came up with the idea of building a hospital. At that time, there was only one hospital in the city of Medellin, San Juan de Dios Hospital, but its conditions were pitiful: the premises were small, the furniture was completely deteriorated, the surgical and scientific instruments were incomplete and deficient, and there weren’t enough beds. The patients had to lie around on sidewalks for days, waiting for their turn to occupy a bed.

MISSION

Hospital Universitario San Vicente de Paul is a non-profit private institution that offers health services with emphasis on highly-complex patient care. It centers its economic effort on those patients who need service but cannot afford to pay for it. It performs its task with a comprehensive human focus, quality, and ethics with qualified and committed personnel.

VISION

In the year 2007, Hospital Universitario San Vicente de Paul will be a leader in health services with high specialization and optimum quality. It will always keep up with its vast social projection and will maintain its educational and research orientation.
