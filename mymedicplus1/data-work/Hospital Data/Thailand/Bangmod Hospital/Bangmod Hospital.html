Description:

Bangmod Hospital is the first hospital established in Rama 2 (Thonburi-Pak Thor) Rd. area. Since then, we have continually improved our services and operations systems in order to serve rapidly accelerating growth of public demands.

Bangmod hospital was first started as a clinic before expanded to a polyclinic. With the exploding growth of our medical knowledge and reputations, our executives committees have decided to develop our operation to a 100-bed hospital in 1987, registered in the name of Bangmod Hospital Co.,Ltd. managed by

Dr. Weerapong Ramangkool as Chairman
Surasit Assadamongkhol, M.D. as Chief Executive Officer
Associate Prof. Kessara Assadamongkhol, M.D. as Director

In 1995, Bangmod Hospital has evolved its capacity to 400 beds to accomplish our main objective which is to solely focus on providing the finest healthcare available for people in the area of Rama 2 and its vicinity. Furthermore, we have joined the promotion of Social Security and Public Affair project developed under the Governmental control due to our recognition of the importance of health and safety of public working for private sector.

We organize specialists in every field of medicine available on a 24 hours basis to provide immediate medical services with state-of-the-arts medical equipments such as Ultrasound machine, Computerized X-Ray machine, Hydrotripsy, etc. and we also set up specialties medical centers for example 24 Hrs. Emergency Center, Hemodialysis Center, Cosmetic Surgery Clinic, Child Malnutrition Clinic. For your convenience, we open up 2 more branches, Bangmod Hospital 2 located on the in-bound side of Rama 2 Rd. and Bangmod Hospital 3 located on Ekachai Rd. in Bang Bon Sub-district, set up a mobile medical unit to serve people in surrounding communities, factories as well as governmental offices. Furthermore, we also develop a Cleft Lip, Palate, and Craniofacial Deformities program to serve the communities with free of charge.

Today Bangmod Hospital is committed to offering the best patient care modern medicine has to offer with more than 100 of multi-national well-known physicians. Our operation is now achieved international standard with ISO 9001:2000 certified.
