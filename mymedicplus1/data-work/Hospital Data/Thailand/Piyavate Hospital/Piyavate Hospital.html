Description:

Piyavate International Hospital is a publicly-owned health care services facility which started its operations in 1993. The 27-story hospital in Bangkok has continually developed the expertise of it's health facility and has now over 300 beds, and range of specialized services.

Piyavate Hospital, a private hospital Bangkok is a modern 27-story building with over 500 indoor parking spaces, which can be accessed conveniently from the International Airport and all points in Bangkok.

In the midst of 2004, Piyavate Hospital repositioned itself from being just another general hospital to be the “Centers of Excellence” concentrating on providing high quality specialty services while maintaining other medical services.

The Piyavate Hospital medical staff is comprised both American Board and Thai Board Certified Physicians in every specialty field, supported by highly qualified registered nurses, technicians and administrative personnel. The hospital provides medical services using modern technology and state-of-the-art equipment, accredited by the International Standards Organization (ISO 9001-2000).

At every service point, the sophisticated Medical Information System is installed to provide prompt, accurate and reliable information, from registration through financial services.

Highlights

The facility has been ISO 9001:2000 certified by the International Standards Organization since 2001.

The doctors of our international hospital include both American Board and Thai Board certified physicians in every area of medical specialization. They are supported by highly-qualified and experienced registered nurses as well as technicians who maintain our high-tech medical equipment and sophisticated computerized Medical Information System. Our team is completed by warm, caring and diligent administrative personnel.
Mision & Vision

The hospital's mission is to maintain each of it's centers of excellence as a world leader in it's area of medical specialization, such as women's health and heart health. In that way, the hospital can provide it's patients from Thailand and around the globe with the most appropriate medical solutions to their problems and the most satisfactory results.
photo: American Board certified and Thai Board certified doctors at our international hospital in Bangkok

The hospital's vision includes providing its doctors with modern medical technologies and training in the latest, hi-tech medical procedures & medicine while continuing to provide it's services to patients at competitive prices to encourage the trend of medical tourism to Thailand.

We're merely six hours from Japan and the Middle East, 12 hours from Europe and 16 hours from New York City. We accept all forms of payment and can process insurance claims.

List of Surgeries/Treatments:
  • Advanced forms of stress testing including stress echocardiography and stress MRI
  • Standard and 3-dimensional echocardiography
  • Cardiovascular MRI which provides comprehensive details of cardiovascular systems without the usage of iodinated-contrast dye and radiation exposure
  • Multi-slice detector CT Scan which provides information on heart function and the best non-invasive methods to detect coronary artery luminal narrowing
  • High skills and latest technologies for diagnosing serious heart rhythm problems
  • State-of-the-art cardiac catheterization laboratories
  • Sophisticated methods and measurements for assessing congestive heart failure patients
  • Overall integration of technologies and methods to provide accurate and rapid determination of the causes of chest pains
  • Treating coronary artery blockages with balloon and intracoronary stents (bare stent or drug -eluting stent)
  • Treating blood vessel blockages other than coronary artery including lower and upper extremity blood vessels, aorta and carotid arteries.
  • Insertion of pacemaker or defibrillator to treat serious heart rhythms without open heart surgeries
  • Radiofrequency ablation of most heart rhythm abnormalities
  • Open heart surgeries to treat multiple blockages of coronary artery, valvular heart diseases and vascular diseases
  • Treating severe, intractable coronary artery disease and congestive heart failure in cases that do not respond to medicine, Enhanced External Counter-pulsation (EECP) or biventricular pacing
