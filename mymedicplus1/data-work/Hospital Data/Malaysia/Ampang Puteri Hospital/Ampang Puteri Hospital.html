Description:

Owned and managed by KPJ Healthcare Bhd which is the Healthcare Division of Johor Corporation and is the largest healthcare group in the country. KPJ Ampang Puteri Specialist Hospital (KPJAPSH) is one of the most modern of its kind in Malaysia and South East Asia.

KPJAPSH which is fully MS ISO 9001:2000 certified and accredited by Malaysian Society for Quality in Health, is designed to bring quality healthcare nearer to you. It is strategically located with easy access from Kuala Lumpur and Selangor.

The six-storey hospital, compact in design and furnished to international standard is situated near Ampang Point Shopping Complex, surrounded by the lush greenery of the Ulu Kelang hills from afar, providing patients an ideal. interactive and serene environment in which to recuperate.

With 226 beds, both private and semi private, patients can enjoy the privacy and comfort of a premier specialist hospital run by expert management and medical staff committed to the well-being and recovery of patients.

FACILITIES
Our multi-discilinary and holistic care includes a host of the facilities:

  • Day Theatre allows minor surgical procedures to be performed on a day - patient basis.
  • Opthalmology Unit contains the most up-to-date equipment including lasers for treatment of eye conditions.
  • Nuclear Medicine Centre for safe and accurate diagnosis.
  • Intensive Care & Coronory Unit with haemodynamic monitoring.
  • Mother and baby facilities with comfortable delivery suites and comprehensive nursery.
  • 24 hours accident and emergency unit and outpatient services.
  • Laboratory for full medical investigations 24 hours a day.
  • Pharmacy is well-stocked providing extensive range of pharmaceutical items.
  • Diagnostic Imaging Centre complete with MRI, Nuclear Medicine, CT Scan, Ultrasound,
  • Bone Densitometry, Mammogram and X-rays.
  • Physiotherapy with a wide range of electrotherapy and exercise therapy for rehabilitation.
  • Renal Dialysis Centre.
  • Executive Health Screening Centre and Wellness Clinic with Treadmill Stress Test
  • System, ECG and Lung Function Test. Packages are designed to suit your health needs.
  • Lithotripsy Centre, stone removal without surgery.
