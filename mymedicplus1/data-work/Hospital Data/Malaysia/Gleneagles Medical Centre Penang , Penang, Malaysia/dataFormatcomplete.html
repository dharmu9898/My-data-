Selamat Datang. Welcome to the Gleneagles Medical Centre, Penang Internet Homepage Service. This service is developed as an introduction to Gleneagles Medical Centre, Penang and to help answer some of the questions you may have.

Gleneagles Medical Centre, Penang is an acute care general hospital established in 1973 as the first private hospital in the island of Penang, commonly known as the Pearl of the Orient. It has a wide range of specialist services and facilities.

Owned by Parkway Holdings Limited of Singapore, Gleneagles Medical Centre, Penang is located in the heart of Georgetown and is easily accessible. Redevelopment of the hospital which started in October 1996 completed in December 2000. This has given the hospital a cutting edge in the delivery of care to the patients.

Working as a team with the Mission "To make a difference in people's lives through excellent patient care.", our staff are privileged to serve your needs. Furthermore, all of the hospital's services are delivered with your comfort, needs and recovery in mind.

We are here to serve you, and we are proud to be your "Hospital of Choice".

 MATERNITY SERVICES

Whenever you go on a journey, preparation is always necessary. The journey into motherhood is no exception. Attending ante-natal classes gives the mother-to-be the opportunity to learn something about the changes that are occuring in her body and enables her to actively prepare for the experience of childbirth and motherhood.

Gleneagles Medical Centre runs a comprehensive programme which consists of 6 weekly classes, each lasting 1 1/2 hours. Husbands are welcome to attend. A general outline of the ante-natal programme includes:

Changes In Pregnancy

Process Of Labour

Relaxation Exercises During Pregnancy

Pain Relief In Labour

Breathing Exercises In Labour

Feeding A Newborn

Care Of Newborn & Infant

Common Diseases In Babies

Tour Of Labour Room

Video: Changing & Bathing Of Newborn

A fee of RM100 per course is charged. Classes are conducted in the evening, on every consecutive Friday at 7:30pm. Refreshments will be served. Further details can be obtained from Executive Health Screening

Centre, Tel: +604-2202103.


Executive Health Screening Programme

Our Executive Health Screening Programme is designed to :

Give you an assessment of how your body is coping with stress

Investigate further any signs of disease of any particular organ / system, if necessary

Advice you on changes in lifestyle and / or diet should this be necessary

How often should I undergo this programme?

Good health is important for a busy executive like yourself. It is advisable that you come for screening once a year if you are above 40 years’ old and once in every two years or so if you are below 40 years’ old

Preparation for this programme

Fasting from 10.00pm onwards the night before the appointment (only PLAIN WATER is allowed).

On the day of your appointment, please register at the Executive Health Screening Centre at 8.45am.

Please wear short-sleeved loose-fitting blouse/shirt, slacks or bermuda shorts.

Should you be on any medication, please bring them along and inform the Executive Health Screening Co-ordinators before the screening.

Please bring along with you the results of any investigations done elsewhere

How long does it take to finish the programme ?

It usually takes at least 2 to 3 hours to finish the programme. It may take slightly longer if you are requested to undergo some extra tests. A complimentary snack will be provided.
