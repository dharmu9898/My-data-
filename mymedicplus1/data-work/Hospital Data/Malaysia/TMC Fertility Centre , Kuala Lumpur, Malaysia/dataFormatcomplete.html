TMC Fertility Centre

TMC Fertility Centre formerly known as Damansara Fertility Centre, was established in January 1994 and provides services in the area of women's health but with special dedication to the management of infertility and laparoscopic surgery. read more...

About TMC Fertility Centre

TMC Fertility Centre offers you a comprehensive range of fertility services and will tailor-make a plan of management that would best suit your needs.
They are committed to provide the most advanced medical treatment available, offered in an empathetic and caring environment.Their team includes doctors, embryologists and nurses, all experts in their areas of specialization. In addition to this, their centre maintains strong associations with experts in other related specialties, enabling them to provide individualized, multidisciplinary care to each patient.

They are proud to announce their latest pregnancy success rates. TMC Fertility Centre has consistently achieved high IVF success rates.
