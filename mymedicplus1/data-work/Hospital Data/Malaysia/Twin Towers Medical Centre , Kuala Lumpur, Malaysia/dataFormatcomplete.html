Description:

 

The Twin Towers Medical Centre (TTMC) is an outpatient polyclinic in the heart of Kuala Lumpur’s Golden Triangle. It is the in-house medical care facility in the Petronas Twin Tower complex providing multidisciplinary medical services for healthcare needs. The centre caters to patients from all age groups and accommodates both domestic and international patients too.


The TTMC provides special care to international patients. The management has developed a system to help a patient from immigration processes to treatment planning and booking right to the end of his or her medical journey. TTMC offers customized healthcare packages ensured to best suit your needs and included in the packages are accommodation, transfers, even a personal interpreter and financial assistance.
