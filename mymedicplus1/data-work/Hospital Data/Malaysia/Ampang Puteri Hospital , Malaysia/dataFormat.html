OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide affordable Health Retreats, Yoga Retreats and Yoga Holidays which create a sacred, transformational space for strengthening, healing and cosmic fun - linking  the inner self to the outer self.

 Shikamoo Health Retreats and Yoga Workshops

Shikamoo is a Swahili word meaning, "I touch your feet in gratitude".  Our goal and emphasis during the retreat is to invoke a permanent lifestyle shift of  healthy living, wellness, and kindness.

 About the Presenters
