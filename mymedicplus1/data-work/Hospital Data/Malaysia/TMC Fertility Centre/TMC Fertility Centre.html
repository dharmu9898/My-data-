Description:

Tropicana Medical Centre (TMC) is located in a prime alcove in Kota Damansara, Petaling Jaya, only a short distance from the city of Kuala Lumpur. TMC is the flagship tertiary care Medical Centre under the TMC Life Sciences Berhad Group of Companies. This resort-themed Medical Centre modeled in line with warm Balinese tones and settings, aims at providing personalised medical support in addition to educating society to create, nurture and maintain vitality in life.

Our centre of Medical Excellence in Fertility, TMC Fertility Centre (TMC) formerly known as Damansara Fertility Centre was established in January 1994 in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia and has since expanded further with branches in Sibu, Kepong, Kuantan, Penang & Johor Bahru, representative offices in Medan & Jakarta, Indonesia.

Armed with a highly trained and dedicated team of gynaecologists, urologists, embryologists and nursing staff and equipped with the latest expertise and facilities, TMC Fertility Centre is a comprehensive fertility treatment hub providing the most technologically advanced treatment options to couples with fertility problems.
