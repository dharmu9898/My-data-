Description:

The private Cancer Hospital SanaFontis is an international competence and service centre for holistic cancer therapy and clinical cancer research.

Our cancer hospital at Freiburg/Breisgau offers 85 beds for patients with acute oncological conditions. “Although our principle is to treat our cancer patients according to current standards, we also keep an open mind for innovative and complementary cancer therapy approaches. Just as important as providing cancer treatment based on state- of- the- art scientific knowledge are innovative medical equipment and compassionate care for our patients.

We actively involve them in all decisions concerning their cancer therapy and take their particular needs and wishes into account wherever possible,” says Dr. Joachim Drevs, medical director of the Cancer Hospital SanaFontis and private lecturer at Freiburg Medical University.
