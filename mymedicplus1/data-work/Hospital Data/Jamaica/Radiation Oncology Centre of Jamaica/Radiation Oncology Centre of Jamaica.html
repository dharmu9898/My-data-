Description:

The Radiation Oncology Centre of Jamaica is the first private cancer treatment (radiotherapy) centre of its kind in the English speaking Caribbean. Located in a medical complex offering diagnostic and other complimentary medical services, the Centre offers a comprehensive range of services and treatment of the highest standard in radiation therapy at competitive rates.

The Centre Utilizes state-of-the-art equipment for accurate planning and treatment of cancer patients. The sophisticated equipment enables the Centre's team of qualified professionals to deliver cancer treatments with a level of accuracy and precision not seen before in the practice of Radiation Oncology in Jamaica and the English speaking Caribbean.
