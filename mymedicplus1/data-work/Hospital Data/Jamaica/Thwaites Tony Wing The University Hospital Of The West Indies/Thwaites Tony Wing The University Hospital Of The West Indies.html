Description:

The Tony Thwaites Wing at the University Hospital of The West Indies, offering superior health care to Jamaica and the rest of the Caribbean since 1990.

Created out of the need for a healthcare facility that would foster a mutually beneficial relationship with the University Hospital of the West Indies, The Tony Thwaites Wing was officially opened in October 1990.

The strength of The Tony Thwaites Wing, is its broad range of specialty services designed to serve Jamaica and the rest of the Caribbean, and a medical staff that is recognized nationally for the quality of its patient care, teaching and research.
