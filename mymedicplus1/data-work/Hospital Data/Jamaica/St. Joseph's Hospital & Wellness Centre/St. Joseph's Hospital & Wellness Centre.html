Description:

Founded in December 18th, 1916 by the Dominican Sisters Religious Order, St. Joseph's Hospital is a fully accredited 44-bed General Hospital. Ideally situated on 12 acres...

...the Hospital is centrally located on 22 Deanery Road in Southeast Kingston. With its manicured lawns and lush shade trees, St. Joseph’s Hospital embodies holistic healthcare. The main Hospital provides a wide range of surgical, Medical, Ophthalmic, Psychiatric, Rehabilation and other specialized facilities including all diagnostic services.
