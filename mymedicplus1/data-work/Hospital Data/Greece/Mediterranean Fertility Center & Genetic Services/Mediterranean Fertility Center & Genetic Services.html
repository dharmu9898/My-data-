Description:

The Mediterranean Fertility Center & Genetic Services (MFCGS), locally owned and directed is totally committed to offering affordable and successful treatment to all patients. Our team is more than happy to welcome anyone with infertility problems who wishes to receive the suitable treatment in the MFCGC in Chania. We will provide the highest standard of care, treatment and advice to achieve the best prospects for a successful pregnancy. Having state of the art equipment and extensive experience in handling fertility problems, we ensure you that you will benefit from the highest level of treatment.

About Us

The Mediterranean Fertility Center & Genetic Services, Chania opened its doors in May 1994. Since its inception, MFCGS has become the leader in Reproductive Medicine and Assisted Reproductive Technologies in the Island of Crete. MFCGS has assisted thousands of couples from across the country and around the world, in their achievement of pregnancy and continues to pioneer new technologies and advancements for our patients.

Our mission is to provide the finest care in reproductive medicine. Dr Jacumakis and his teem have acquired special expertise in treating problems of Reproductive Endocrinology, menopause, and infertility. They are sensitive and considerate to the emotions that often accompany reproductive problems, and treat each patient with compassion and with every consideration for the patient’s privacy.

If you would like to pursue your dream of having a baby, while working with a team of professionals who are committed to your unique needs, please give us a call. Our accomplishments speak for themselves.
