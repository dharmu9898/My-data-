About Us

Patricai garland - Healing.iePatricia Garland (O'Reilly) is a fully qualified beauty therapist, reflexologist, aromatherapist and Reiki Master.

In 1992, Patricia established the Natural Health and Beauty Clinic in Coolmine Community Sports Complex, Dublin 15. This has been a successful venture over the years with a substantial and consistent client base.

In 2003 Patricia established The Healing Web Ltd. The Healing Web Ltd focuses on the provision of therapy services, alternative therapy training courses, and the sales of goods and services to those with an interest in alternative and complementary health and healing in Ireland.

In 2005 Patricia opened her second Centre with Healing.ie based in Glasnevin, Dublin.

Patricia has continued to develop her personal skills by completing many courses and workshops in a wide variety of related areas. These include NLP and stress management.

Patricia has also undertaken the teaching of various courses and workshops in the complementary and alternative health area.
