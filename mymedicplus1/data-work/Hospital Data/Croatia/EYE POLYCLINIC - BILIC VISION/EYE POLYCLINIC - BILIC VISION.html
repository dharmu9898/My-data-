Description:

"Ophthalmology and Orthopedics Polyclinic Bilic Vision " is located in the northern part of the city of Zagreb, at the address Ksaverska cesta 45 and is spread over the area of 120 square meters.

The Polyclinic was founded in 2002 on the grounds of a private ophthalmology practice owned by Ph.D. Nadežda Bilić MD. Having spent more than 20 years working as the resident ophthalmologist in the Zagreb "Sisters of Mercy" clinic, Doctor Bilić established her own practice in 1996.

By investing into specialization of her staff and by hiring new practicioners, Doctor Bilić managed to expand the range of services while maintaining top quality standards of ophthalmological services for which the Polyclinic has always been well-known.
