Description:

Eye Clinic 'Svjetlost' is a private ophthalmology clinic with a broad spectrum of ophthalmology procedures and services to offer to our patients including: glasses prescription and eye checkups, laser refractive procedures, laser photocoagulation treatments, cataract surgery, retinal detachment surgery, glaucoma surgery etc.

Our highly qualified experts of various fields in ophthalmology will guide you through diagnostic and surgical procedures. We are equipped with state of the art ophthalmology medical devices including: Legacy Phaco machine, Accurus 800 vitrectome, Eximer laser, YAG laser photo coagulator and disruptor etc.
