Description:

Offering a complete range of specialized programs for children and adolescents suffering from respiratory diseases, the Children's Hospital Srebrnjak is a unique health care institution in the Republic of Croatia.

Measured against European and international standards for delivery of specialized health care, it is a highly ranked institution particularly excelling in the following areas of biomedical research and practice: pulmonology, allergology, immunology and bronchology. We have otorhinolaryngology, cardiology and cardiosurgery expansion plans in the near future.
