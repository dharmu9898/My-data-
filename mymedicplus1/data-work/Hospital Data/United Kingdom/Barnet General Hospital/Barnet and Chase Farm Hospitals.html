Description:

We are a major hospital trust based in north London, across two main hospital sites, providing general and specialist care to patients. Every year we treat thousands of families offering high quality, specialist hospital treatments and therapies to over half a million patients from Barnet, Enfield, Haringey, East Harrow, South Hertfordshire, South Essex and Waltham Forest.

We are a large acute Trust, which came into being on 1st April 1999, following a merger of the former Chase Farm Hospitals and Wellhouse NHS Trusts. Most services are provided at our two hospitals, Barnet Hospital and Chase Farm Hospital, with others provided in four community hospitals managed by sister NHS organisations
