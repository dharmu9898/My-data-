Description:

Centro Clínico & Mejoramiento Profesional is an organization dedicated to the image diagnosis of diseases. During the past ten years it has consolidated an extraordinary medical team that works uninterruptedly every day of the year with the firm decision of rendering a service of an excellent quality. The majority of our specialists are active members of domestic and continental organizations such as: the Venezuelan Association of Medical Ultrasound (AVUM) and the Inter-American Association of Medical Ultrasound (AIUM).

It also counts with the collaboration of expert medic and paramedic staff highly trained to cover from the simplest to the most complex health issues.

Centro Clínico & Mejoramiento Profesional currently represents an important image diagnosis center of reference, hence, hospitals such as the José María Vargas, the Antonio Rísquez, the Jesús Yerena, the Miguel Pérez Carreño, the José Gregorio Hernández and others permanently submit many of their patients so that, through the use of latest generation technological equipment, they can get ultrasound tests that give results one hundred per cent reliable and accurate in order to adopt highly effective treatments.

Highlights

Mission

Our organization is one of the few private centers which parallelly to its usual activities, delivers education through courses of integral ultrasonography aimed at general medicine doctors and postgraduates in various fields.

Values

An extraordinary medical team working uninterruptedly every day of the year with the firm decision of rendering a service of an excellent quality. State-of-the-art equipment for the carrying out of Conventional and Doppler Ultrasound tests as well as 3D and 4D Echo sonography. Likewise, we count with a latest generation Mammary Research Unit, a Digestive Exploration Unit as well as a High-Tech Rx Unit which allows us for higher quality and precision digital diagnosis images.
