Description:

Since 1994, Centro Médico Teknon has been a leader in the Spanish private medical sector thanks to its renowned medical team, the high technology available and comfortable facilities.

The complete service offered by Teknon covers all medical and surgical specialties for both children and adults, including Cardiology, Aesthetic Surgery, Check-ups and a European referral center for cancer treatment, affiliated with the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in New York.
