Description:

The Grupo HOSPITEN (HOSPITEN Group) is an international network of private hospitals, with over 1,000 beds, committed to providing high-level healthcare services. We see over 500,000 patients every year. The result of our commitment can be seen in our twelve hospital centres located in major cities and tourist centres in countries such as Spain, the Dominican Republic and Mexico.

All of our centres are equipped with the latest technological innovations in the medical and management fields, and are staffed by a team of recognised professionals providing the top-level healthcare services that characterise the HOSPITEN Group
