Description:

The Clínica Universitaria de Navarra aims to offer excellent care to everyone. We have designed our organisation thinking about the patient and do our utmost to ensure his well-being and comfort.

Around two thousand people work at the Clínica Universitaria and they are totally dedicated to assure quality medical assistance, personalised care and deep respect for a person’s dignity.
