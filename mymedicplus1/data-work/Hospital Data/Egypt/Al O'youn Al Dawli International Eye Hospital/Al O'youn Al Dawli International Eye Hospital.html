Description:

Are the most updated specialized eye hospitals in the Middle East.

The first hospital was launched on 1999 at Dokki (in the heart of Cairo) and based on the great support we had from our patients that contributed to our success, we opened another 2 branches on 2006 at Heliopolis and Dar al-fouad hospital to continue providing superb quality service and during our journey serving medical field we were able to improve ourselves by getting the international quality certification system (ISO 9001: 2000) and with the help of the most talented and professional professors in the ocular field as well as our nursing staff and the most recent sophisticated equipments we were able to be pioneers in our field.
