Description:

Dar Al Fouad Hospital is planned and designed to provide high quality healthcare in the area of cardiac,thoracic, vascular and nervous diseases. Management and medical expertise are sourced from the most renowned national and international medical organizations . This enables us to provide our patients with the optimum quality and skills in surgical procedures, medical care and management. Our affiliation with world's recognized medical institutions, ensure that these services not only meet today's world class healthcare standards, but also a continuous and on- going update in all fields of hospital management, standard of equipment as well as professional excellence and skills. Our promise to our patients is to provide them with a level of care and service that matches the highest world standards in a professional, personalized, and friendly environment.

Getting such an international recognition as the only hospital in Egypt and Africa and the 59th hospital worldwide will enable us enhancing our medical service, with unwavering objective of continuous quality improvement along with maintaining the heritage of our patients love and appreciation.
