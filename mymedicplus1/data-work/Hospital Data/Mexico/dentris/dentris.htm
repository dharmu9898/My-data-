List of Surgeries/Treatments:

Prothesis
Totally ceramic crowns, conventional crowns, removable bridges, conventional dentures implant dentistry.

Endodontics
when the pulp (nerve) has failed bevause of a irreversible inflamation that can cause constant pain, leading to a infection an severe pain the dentist can remove this inflamed pulp in a painless and thanks to the tecnology of our instruments, a quick way so the patient can save his tooth will keep its function for many years.

Orthodontics
we help to discover your best smile, correcting dental positions through orthodontic treatments conventional or ceramic, according with personal diagnosis.

Pediatric dentistry
is the branch of dentistry that specializes in overall dental care of children, treatment and prevention of caries as well as instructions oral hygiene. Ensuring that our infant patients forget the myths and frustrations produce toward the dentist, offering a treatment with warmth and confidence

Implantology
when natural teeth are removed, many problems occur. The remaining teeth shift, rotate and become crooked. An improper bite will develop, making it very difficult to chew food properly. Dental implants, when properly placed, can restore missing teeth, thereby eliminating these potential problems.

Esthetic dentistry
today, there is tremendous advancements in technology and procedures that can dramatically enhance your smile. Nobody should have to mask or hide an unattractive smile anymore. Dentaris is the area's leading authority offering a wide range of services that will put the "wow in your smile".

Dental education
for us prevention is the best treatment, for it will always offer our patients information to lead a life without dental problems through brushing techniques, to different types of rinsing solutions, special brushes for patients with prosthetic appliances, tec.
