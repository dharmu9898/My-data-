Description:

Within its glistening walls, the Puerta de Hierro Medical Center houses more than humane treatment and excellent professional care: it also accommodates up-to-date technology and continuing education for its staff; all within an atmosphere of extravagant comfort that has yet to be adopted by any other hospital facility in this region, nor by moore than a very few in the entire country.
Puerta de Hierro Medical Center is a private, proud¬ly Mexican organization, specialized in the pro¬vision of high-quality health services.

Puerta de Hierro Specialty Hospital is unique in the country; it brings together 300 of Mexico’s leading specialists, plus world-class technology to offer diagnosis, treatment, and hospitalization services.

This is where you’ll find all of us, close to you, on hand for when you need us. We want to lend you a hand, understand you, and heal you. We have the most up-to-date hu¬man and technical resources in our different spe¬cializations, but the best we have to offer is our human warmth.
