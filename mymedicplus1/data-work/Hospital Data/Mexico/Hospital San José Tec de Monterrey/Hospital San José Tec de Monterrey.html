Description:

Hospital San Jose Tec de Monterrey, a world-class facility that is internationally recognized for its quality and safety, ensures clinical excellence by bringing together state-of-the-art technology and the very best talent, comprised of its medical staff as well as residents from the Tecnológico's de Monterrey Medical School.

Hospital San Jose Tec de Monterrey will soon see its capacities enhanced by the addition of the Zambrano Hellion Medical Center, which will house the Institute for Cardiology and Vascular Medicine and the Institute for Oncology. Hospital San Jose Tec de Monterrey is currently developing the Salvador Sada Gomez Center for Geriatrics and Alzheimer's Disease, a facility that will combine research, teaching and patient care.

Highlights

Hospital San José Tec de Monterrey is a world-class hospital accredited by the Joint Commission International for its high quality and best-in-class patient care. Since 1969, it has focused on the well-being of its patients by providing the right mix of cutting edge technology, warm & compassionate care, and the best medical professionals and procedures, in a cost effective way. It is sponsored by the internationally recognized Tecnologico de Monterrey and located in Mexico only 150 miles from the US border.

Hospital San José Tec de Monterrey’s quality and care services have obtained recognition in Mexico and internationally as a hospital of high standars. In addition, it has relationships with institutions in the United States that promote research, education and knowledge exchange.

  • In December 2007, Joint Commission International accredited the Hospital for complying with international norms regarding safe and high-quality patient care.
  • The Center of Multi-organ Transplants is the only one in Mexico that has been nationally certified by the Mexican Secretary of Health for carrying out all types of transplants in the Hospital and has been internationally certified by the National Marrow Donors Program® for bone marrow transplants.
  • SIEMENS has recognized Hospital San José Tec de Monterrey as a Center of Excellence in Mexico.
  • The Hospital has received certification from the Mexican Secretary of Health’s Board of General Health.
  • Since 2007, the Hospital has attained the Distinctive H, a certification granted by the Mexican Secretary of Health through the Department of Alimentary Services for complying and exceeding with the hygiene standards required by the Official Mexican Norms.
  • In 2004, during the Extraordinary Summit of the Americas, the White House selected the Hospital San José Tec de Monterrey as the hospital to be used in case of a medical emergency involving the President of the United States and/or members of his entourage.
