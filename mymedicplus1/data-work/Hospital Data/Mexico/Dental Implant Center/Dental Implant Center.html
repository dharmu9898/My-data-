Description:
The Valenzuela Dental Group was established in 1972 by Dr. Jose Valenzuela Fierro in Mexicali, Baja California, where he practiced for more than 25 years. In 2000 the Valenzuela Group opened its first cosmetic dentistry office in the city of Los Algodones, Baja California, which rapidly grew to become one of the most successful dental practices in the area thanks to our friendly staff, highly qualified dental specialists; advanced technology; and clean, modern facilities. As word spread about the quality care and outstanding results we provide, our office rapidly began attracting a high volume of patients from throughout the United States. Today, scores of people have discovered the convenience and quality of the many cosmetic and implant dentistry treatments offered at Dental Implant Center, near Yuma, Arizona and Imperial Valley.

Highlights

Dr. Jose Valenzuela, Jr. proudly carries on the tradition begun by his father over 35 years ago by maintaining a passion for helping people through dentistry. The entire implant dentistry team at our Los Algodones office pays respectful attention to our patients' concerns to ensure that we consistently provide a higher level of care and a uniquely different dental experience.
Our practice features many advanced modern procedures and the latest in dental technology, ranging from high-magnification intraoral cameras and digital X-rays to the latest cutting-edge dental implant and cosmetic dentistry treatments. Our patients come to Los Algodones from Yuma, Arizona, the Imperial Valley in Southern California, and even as far as Washington and Canada because they know we offer consistently top-notch dental treatment at reasonable prices, and our proven track record in planning and placing dental implants is unmatched.
