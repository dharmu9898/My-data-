Description:

Conscious of the responsibility in providing quality healthcare services, Health Services by the Angeles Group (Grupo Angeles) impulses the advancement of private medicine, contributing to the further development of quality healthcare in Baja California, through the establishment of a hospital center with technological strategies and high quality services of the Angeles Hospitals attracting the medical community from the city, region and state to our renown medical center.

Inaugurated in June 2004, the Hospital Angeles Tijuana offers its patients a warm and comforting medical and healthcare environment without having to leave the city. It is a specialties hospital in permanent search for medical advancement, with state-of-the art technology in operating rooms, as well as in diagnostic services in all medical fields
