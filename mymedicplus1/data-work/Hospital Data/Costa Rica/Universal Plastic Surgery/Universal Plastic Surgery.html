Description:

Universal Plastic Surgery, founded in 1981, is one of Costa Rica’s most prestigious plastic and reconstructive surgery clinics.Universal Plastic Surgery and cosmetic surgery in general have become worldwide one of the most important branches of medical practice in the last ten years.

Costa Rica in Central America is no exception, turning lately into a hot spot where people from every continent gather to relax and have plastic or reconstructive surgery after enjoying its almost 20.000 square miles of tropical forest, active volcanoes and splendid beaches.
