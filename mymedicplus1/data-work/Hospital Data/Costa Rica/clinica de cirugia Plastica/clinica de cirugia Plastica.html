Description:

Dr. Chavarría’s offices are located at Clínica Bíblica Hospital in San José. The Hospital was founded in 1921 and has a long tradition of excellent care and technological advances. It is one of the best private hospitals in the country. The operating rooms are equipped with modern monitoring devices to check every change during surgery and the staff is composed of highly trained professionals..

This team of qualified nurses is in charge of the recovery room, where you will rest right after the surgery. Each room offers a private bathroom, private phone, air conditioning, cable TV and internet access.
