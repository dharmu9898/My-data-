Description:

Confraternität-Privatklinik Josefstadt regards itself as a service centre for a wide range of medical examinations and therapies. In the long tradition of our hospital, the areas of internal medicine, cardiology, neurology, gastroenterology, urology, ophthalmology and nearly all surgical fields, such as general and vascular surgery, thoracic and plastic surgery as well as dental, oral and maxillofacial surgery have become the focus of our work.

Equipped with the latest high-tech medical equipment, Confraternität-Privatklinik Josefstadt furthermore plays a pioneering role in the field of imaging diagnostics. All our examination and treatment facilities are continually upgraded and correspond to the current state of medical technology.
