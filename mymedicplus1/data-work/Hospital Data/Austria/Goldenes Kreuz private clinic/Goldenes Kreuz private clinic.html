Description:

The aim of the Goldenes Kreuz private clinic is to combine tradition and progress for the benefit of our patients.In addition to the traditional high quality of our medical care, we are proud to offer our patients an environment that fulfills all of their needs and desires during their stay at our clinic.

A carefully selected team of doctors, certified nurses, midwives and physiotherapists with many years of experience are always there to ensure the wellbeing of our patients. Optimal care for our patients is always our prime concern.
