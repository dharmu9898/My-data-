Description:

In all its fields of activity, Wiener Privatklinik makes every effort to provide BETTER services than conventional hospitals, in order to ensure the best possible medical care for its patients.

Total Quality Hospital

Wiener Privatklinik is operated in accordance with the Total Quality Hospital Management (TQH) guidelines. The main principle of the TQH philosophy is not to content oneself with the achieved standards, but to continue to take new and innovative steps, particularly in the field of quality management. In line with its Total Quality Hospital philosophy, Wiener Privatklinik has had its whole nursing department certified according to the internationally acknowledged ISO 9001:2000 standard. After many years of hard work in the field of quality management, the ISO certificate was awarded in September 2003 by the TÜV CERT certification office of TÜV Austria.
