Description:

Oncology Institute in Gliwice was established in 1947 and in 1951 was transformed in the Comprehensive Cancer Centre Maria Skłodowska-Curie Memorial Institute Branch Gliwice.

From the beginning our main objective has been to comprehensively diagnose and treat in a connected way neoplasms diseases.

Highlights

Certificates, awards

Our care for the well-being of our patients would not be possible without the continuous improvement within the quality of our services. Bearing in mind our patient’s increasing expectations and the various legal requirements, we have introduced a System of Quality Management based on the norms outlined in ISO 9001:2000, 18001:2004, 14001:2004, HACCP.

In the Poland wide Hospital Ranking for 2002, organized by the „Rzeczpospolita” newspaper, we were awarded a creditable III place in the category for the Best Hospital in Poland.
