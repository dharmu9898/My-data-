Baskent University Alanya Hospital

In response to Turkey's need for high quality institutions of higher education, the Organ Transplant and Burns Treatment Foundation, which has been in operation since 1980, made the decision to turn its existing complexes into a university. In 1993, procedures were begun to establish Ba?kent University, with the aid of the Haberal Educational Foundation. In accordance with Article 3961 of the Legal Code, the University was officially founded on January 13, 1994.


The main objective is to build an academic institution that will ultimately be a full-fledged and internationally prominent university. In the 1995-1996 academic year, Baskent University moved to its new Campus at Baglica, located 20 km. west of the center of Ankara, on the main highway to Eskisehir.

Highlights

The Ankara Hospital has well-equipped laboratories that provide clinicians with accurate and rapid diagnostic assistance. All the laboratories serve research activities as well. Electrophoresis, nephelometry, and ELISA systems are available for specific tests. Immunofluoroassay, radioimmunoassay, and automatic absorpsiometry techniques are also used routinely.

Baskent University Adana Hospital is a fully staffed and well equipped 18,000 m2, 14-floor health center with a 420-bed capacity hospital was opened in June 1998 in Adana.

The center accommodates a hemodialysis unit that has 69 beds, 7 operating rooms, 4 intensive care units, and a burn unit. Radiology, nuclear medicine, and laboratory divisions provide all types of sophisticated techniques for advanced applications and investigations.

The center has outpatient clinics, performs all types of surgical procedures, and accepts emergency admissions.
