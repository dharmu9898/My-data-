Jinemed Hospital

Jinemed Hospital’s infertility treatment services provided by experienced English-speaking doctors and low cost IVF treatment options are reasons why it has been chosen as a health care provider by both local and international infertility patients.

The desire to have children and the feeling of family is one of the most basic of human instincts - but global statistics show that one out of every seven couples is unable to have children without assistance from a physician.

Jinemed Hospital was Istanbul’s first in vitro fertilisation (IVF) centre. Established over 16 years ago it has pioneered the introduction of every advance in fertility technology. Under its leadership, the total number of IVF clinics in Turkey has reached 50.

Qualifications:

The oral examination performed by a dentist, the evaluation of photos made by an intraoral camera and the orthopantomography, ensures that our patients will get a close picture of the the state of their masticatory apparatus and teeth. This is followed by preparing a treatment plan, which also gives detailed information to the patients regarding the timing and costs of each necessary procedure.

In the dental surgeries of Hungary Dental we use the most up-to-date instruments for examination. Our surgeries are equipped equally well as the best Western European surgeries.
