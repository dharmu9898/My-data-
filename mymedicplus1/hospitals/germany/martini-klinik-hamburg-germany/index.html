Martini Klinik

The Martini Clinic in the campus of the University Clinic Hamburg-Eppendorf is specialized in the diagnosis and treatment of prostate cancer. We put our hearts into the health and rapid recovery of our patients. To enable this, we offer all established therapy forms, based on the latest research discoveries. One focus of treatment is on nerve-preserving removal of the prostate gland, which has an extremely positive influence on the consequences of this radical operation. Our experienced surgeons are among the best-known experts world-wide in this field. In the pleasant surroundings of our private clinic, they make sure that the quality of our patients' lives is preserved.

Specialization on prostate cancer

The modern, up-to-date Martini-Klinik for prostate cancer was created on the grounds of the University Medical Center Hamburg-Eppendorf (UKE). This specialization, patterned after the American model, is unique in Germany. Patient health always represents the center of all our work. Patients receive treatment tailored to their individual needs. International experts, the doctors` and care team of Professor Dr. Hartwig Huland, Professor Dr. Markus Graefen and Professor Dr. Hans Heinzer take care of each individual patient (during his treatment for prostate cancer). Besides its excellent medical service, the particularly pleasant atmosphere at the Martini Clinic helps to accelerate the recovery process.

Comfortable ambience

As we know, only patients comfortable after an operation can be discharged and return home as soon as possible. Thus, we offer medical excellence and comfort which meet the highest expectations. This starts at the reception area, where dark leather armchairs and walls and pillars in warm shades of brown ensure an atmosphere which conveys trust and shelter from the very first moment. We have consciously chosen a hotel character to make the recovery phase as pleasant as possible. The Martini-Klinik provides single and double rooms with high-comfort beds and a built-in bathroom with shower.

 

Surgical methods

The Martini-Klinik offers ”classical” surgical removal of the prostate known as radical retropubic prostatectomy. In this procedure the surgeon removes the prostate manually.

The second option offered for radical prostatectomy at the Martini-Klinik is minimally invasive surgery with the da Vinci® surgical system. With this modern laparoscopic OP system the surgeon does the procedure using a surgical robot to which microsurgical instruments and a three-dimensional camera system are attached.

Medical care

Behind every successful course of treatment in a clinic there are always excellent doctors. Only specialists with many years of surgical experience and an outstanding reputation work in the Martini-Klinik. The Medical Directors Professor Hartwig Huland, Professor Dr. Markus Graefen and Professor Dr. Hans Heinzer and their doctors` team are among the best-known surgeons in the field of nerve-preserving radical prostatectomy in Europe. Together with the doctors` team and universitary partners, they have performed and improved this particularly difficult operation over 8,500 times.

Furthermore the assistant medical directors of the Martini-Klinik are additionally specialized deeper in one area of prostate cancer:

Dr. Georg Salomon: modern imaging techniques (Martini-Konsult Diagnostik)

Dr. Thorsten Schlomm: Martini-Konsult Zweitmeinung as offer of an independent second second expert opinion

PD Dr. Alexander Haese: prostate removal with da Vinci® surgical system

PD Dr. Thomas Steuber: advanced prostate carcinoma

The brachytherapy has already been made more than 1,600 times together with the specialists of the Ambulanzzentrum des UKE GmbH, section radio-oncology.

Beyond their clinical expertise, the doctors of the Martini-Klinik are active researchers and their close connection to patient care allows that innovations in therapy or diagnostics directly influence daily practice.

Guiding principle

In accordance with our guiding principle, each person is an individual entitled to the care that reflects his individuality. Our work is centered on both physical and psychological recovery. It is important for us to encourage our patients and their families to actively take part in this process.
The respect of rights of each individual is the principle that applies to the entire team of the Martini Clinic. In our professional life we respect and support each other, but we are also critical of ourselves. Our guiding principle commits us to offering each patient the best possible treatment based on the latest scientific developments and the results of our own research and the research of others. Latest achievements are critically assessed by our surgeons before being used in the routine treatment. Thus, we continuously invest in the quality of our service in order to deliver our patients the best possible treatment.
