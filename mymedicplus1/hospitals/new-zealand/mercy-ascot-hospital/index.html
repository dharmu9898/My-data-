Mercy ascot hospital

MercyAscot is New Zealand’s largest private hospital and provides comprehensive surgical and medical services from its two Auckland sites - Ascot Integrated Hospital on Greenlane Road East, Remuera and Mercy Integrated Hospital on Mountain Road, Epsom. MercyAscot aims to provide first world private health services in New Zealand through teamwork and innovation. This can be achieved through combining state-of-the-art medical facilities with the skills and attitude of our outstanding personnel.
