Nile Hospital

Nile hospitalin Hurghada has opened on the 18th of October. It was the result of two years of continuous work and planning. El Gouna hospital is serving thousands of tourists from all nationalities who are visiting red sea every year through a highly qualified medical staff in all the specialties.
Hurghada has been grown up, population wise and as touristic pole. This raises the necessity for a parallel medical service. The owners of the hospital, being working in Hurghada for many years discriminated that they have the following problem in their medical practice:


  • The need for advanced higher medical experiences, namely university staff to face the different medical problems of the population, whether residents or visitors, that mandates experienced medical consultants for precise decision making and adequate management.
  • The need for recent and up- to- date medical equipments in the diagnostic, emergency and therapeutic fields.
  • The need for an elegant building that fulfils the medical needs, easy circulation, and the luxurious look that detached the patient's anxiousness.
  • Scientific administration of the corporate that implement the quality control parameters in planning and running the hospital.
  • Cost containment and reasonable pricing of the service.

To sum up: Every patient will have better shape, rapid, precise and scientific medical service.


Hence, the seventeen owners have met and started to plan for a hospital that fulfills the whole five items in one building to serve with love and honesty their citizens and visitors.


This was the history of love and success that made the 18th of October 2005 a remarkable date in the history of Hurghada and neighboring areas.
