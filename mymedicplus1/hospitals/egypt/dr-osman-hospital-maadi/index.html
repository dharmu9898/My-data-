Dr. Osman Hospital-Maadi

Dr. Osman Hospital-Maadi is a 61 bed, acute care, general medical / surgical hospital. Our mission is to provide high quality, cost effective medical care which meet the needs and expetations of patients through comprehensive care services on an inpatient, out patient, & referral basis.


The hospital is a facility providing state of the art diagnostic & therapeutic equipment within our clinical areas, emergency room, operating rooms, and intensive care unite amongst other areas.


We have recruited highly qualified medical, nursing, & supporting staff. We are proud of our staff members who work hard to provide the highest quality medical care, together with a warm and caring attitude.
