Alexandria Clinic Centre

We are dedicated to providing preventive, interventional and supportive services in the most compassionate, thoughtful and efficient manner.
The Alexandria Clinic, P.A. was established in 1946. We are in the unique position of caring for second- and third-generation families. We are a multi-specialty clinic including the following specialties: Express Care, Family Practice, General Thoracic and Vascular Surgery, Hospitalist, Internal Medicine, Midwifery, Nurse Practitioner, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Pediatrics, Podiatry and Urology.
All of our physicians are board certified in their specialty and are members of the Minnesota Medical Association. We have on-site CT, digital mammography, general x-ray, and full-time laboratory services to facilitate our providers in diagnosing your health-care concerns.
In this website, you will find information on each of our specialties and services. We encourage you to select a primary provider to establish ongoing, routine care. In our Specialties section, you will find each provider's picture and short biography.
On behalf of the providers and staff, we look forward to caring for you and your family's health-care needs.

History:

The Alexandria Clinic was founded in 1941 when Dr. Harold Stemsrud and Dr. Clifford joined to practice in Alexandria, MN. Dr. Clifford had been practicing in Osakis and Dr. Stemsrud in Parkers Prairie, MN prior to that time. Their first office was located at 628 Broadway.

Dr. Stemsrud left to serve in the Navy during WWII and Dr. C. E. Carlson moved from Aitkin, MN to join Dr. Clifford in 1944. At the end of WWII, Dr. L. F. Wasson returned to Alexandria and joined Dr. Clifford and Dr. Carlson. When Dr. Stemsrud was released from military duty in 1946, the original four members of the Alexandria Clinic, P.A. worked together for the first time. At that time, they needed larger quarters, and the building at 613 Broadway was remodeled and served as the home of the Alexandria Clinic, P.A. for the next ten years. It was shortly after that, in 1946, that Mr. Clarence "Kelly" Martinson became the Clinic Manager and was synonymous with the Alexandria Clinic, P.A. for the next 30 years.

In the early 1950's, Dr. Peter Geiser became the next member of the Alexandria Clinic, P.A. In 1953 and 1954 came the additions of Dr. Bill Heegaard and Dr. Doug Perkins. At that time a larger facility was needed, and the original clinic building was built at 610 Fillmore and occupied in 1955. In 1961, Dr. Vern Kuhlmann became a member of the clinic. In 1969, Dr. Fred Green joined the clinic as a General Surgeon, which provided the first clinical specialist outside of Family Practice in Alexandria.

The 1970's and 1980's marked a period of rapid growth and expansion for the Alexandria Clinic, P.A. with the addition of specialists in the areas of Internal Medicine, Pediatrics and OB/GYN. It was also during this period that a satellite office was established in Osakis. In 1988 a major expansion of the clinic facilities at 610 Fillmore was undertaken, with a complete remodeling of the facility as well as doubling of the space available.

The Alexandria Clinic's growth continued. In 1995, the Osakis Clinic was doubled in size to accommodate four physicians. Also at that time, a long range plan was developed that resulted in the construction of a new 55,000 square foot building in Alexandria, built to accommodate 31 physicians and two mid-level providers. This building was occupied August 4, 1997.

Doctors Resume

Susan D. Paulson M.D.

Dr. Susan Paulson was born and raised in Thief River Falls, Minnesota. She earned her B.S. Degree in Biology at Bemidji State University and attended medical school at the University of Minnesota-Duluth. Dr. Paulson received her M.D. degree at the University of Minnesota-Minneapolis in 1982 and M.S. Degree in Family Practice and Community Health in 1988. Her three-year residency training was at the University of Minnesota Department of Family Practice and Community Health at Methodist Hospital in St. Louis Park, MN. She is board certified in family practice and adolescent medicine. Dr. Paulson completed her MBA in healthcare in 2009 at the University of St. Thomas.

Dr. Paulson joined Alexandria Clinic, P.A. in 1988. She is a member of the American Academy of Family Physicians, the Minnesota Academy of Family Physicians and the Minnesota Medical Association.


Dr. Paulson enjoys reading, piano, bicycling, scrapbooking and quilting.


Paul W. Bergstrand M.D.

Dr. Paul Bergstrand was born in Minneapolis, MN and raised in Jamestown, NY. He completed his undergraduate studies at Jamestown Community College, Jamestown, NY and Gustavus Adolphus College, St. Peter, MN, earning his B.A. degree in 1980. Dr. Bergstrand did his residency in family medicine and community health at the University of Minnesota Methodist Hospital in St. Louis Park, MN.
Dr. Bergstrand joined Alexandria Clinic, P.A. in 1987. In addition to Family Medicine, Dr. Bergstrand's expertise includes an emphasis on Allergy and Asthma Medicine.

Michael L. Bristow D.O.

Dr. Michael Bristow was born in Iowa City, IA. Following several early moves, the majority of his childhood was spent in Sioux City, IA. After graduating from high school, he went on to earn his B.S. degree in Zoology at the University of Iowa. Upon completion of his undergraduate education, Dr. Bristow attended the University of Osteopathic Medicine and Health Sciences in Des Moines, IA. The four-year course of study was completed with the granting of a D.O. degree or doctor of osteopathic medicine degree.
Dr. Bristow’s clinical training includes a one-year rotating internship, completed in Dayton, OH and a three-year residency in family medicine completed at the Mayo Clinic in Rochester, MN.
Dr. Bristow joined the Alexandria Clinic, P.A. as a member of the Family Medicine Department in August 1991. He is a member of the American Academy of Family Physicians and certified in bone densitometry.
His interests include the use of computers in medicine, outdoor activities and parenting. Dr. Bristow and his wife, Jean, have one son and one daughter.


Shawn M. Lanman D.O.

Dr. Shawn Lanman joined Alexandria Clinic, P.A. in June 2000. Dr. Lanman grew up in Fremont, NE. He received his B.S. degree in biology and ecology from Wichita State University in Wichita, KS. He served a one-year rotating internship at Riverside Hospital in Wichita, KS and completed his general surgery residency through Michigan State University in Garden City, MI. Dr. Lanman performs general, endoscopic, laparoscopic, thoracic and vascular surgery. He received his board certification in general surgery in the spring of 2001. Dr. Lanman is a member of the Minnesota Osteopathic Medical Society, the American College of Osteopathic Surgeons and the American Osteopathic Association.
Dr. Lanman and his wife, Nina, have three children, Sarah, Ellen and Cody. He enjoys camping with his family, hunting and ice fishing.

Dr. Isam Alakhras

His special interest is in the biology and management of non-Hodgkin’s lymphoma. He enjoys reading, traveling and listening to music. Dr. Kurt Hansberry was born in a military hospital in Panama. He traveled extensively throughout the southwestern United States while his father was serving in the United States Army. He received his M.D. degree from the University of Oklahoma College of Medicine in 1983. He completed his categorical surgical internship at William Beaumont Army Medical Center in 1984 while serving on active duty with the United States Army. Dr. Hansberry then directed a troop medical clinic in Korea for one year prior to doing his residency in urology from 1985 to 1990 at Brooke Army Medical Center. After completing his residency, he was on the teaching faculty and was assistant chief of urology at Madigan Army Medical Center in Tacoma, WA. He obtained his board certification from the American Board of Urology in 1992 and recertification in 2002. He is a member of the American Urological Association, American Association of Clinical Urologists and Society of Government Service Urologists.

Dr. Hansberry joined the Alexandria Clinic, P.A. in July 1996. He and his wife, Kathy, and family reside in Alexandria. He enjoys family activities, running and especially golf.
