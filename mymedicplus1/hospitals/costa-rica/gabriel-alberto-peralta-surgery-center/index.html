Gabriel Alberto Peralta surgery Center

Dr. Gabriel Peralta is a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon by the Costarican College of Physicians and Surgeons.

Dr. Gabriel Peralta has trained with some of the best plastic surgeons worldwide. His dedication, love and passion for plastic surgery made him to earn the title of Doctor Honoris Causa in Health Science in 2006.

He has been working with patients to provide exceptional plastic surgery care, safely, professionally and at a reasonable price.

Dr. Gabriel Peralta’s experience in plastic surgery since 1987 is couple with genuine concern for his patients. He specializes in aesthetic plastic surgery of the face and body.

His team of health professionals includes board certified doctor anesthesiologists only, who administer the latest in light, quick recovery anesthetics. The team is dedicated to giving the highest level of safety, comfort and care to his patients. Most procedures are performed in his Acreditated state of the art plastic surgery center, unique in Costa Rica. If the patient prefers Dr. Gabriel Peralta has hospital privileges in the CIMA San José Hospital and Hospital Clínica Bíblica.

Our Philosophy

Plastic Surgery Patients make an investment-physically, emotionally and economically. In order to respect that, we are committed to give the best care, during and after surgery to every one of our Patients.

For this reason, we have dedicated since 1987 receive the best possible training in plastic cosmetic surgery, state of the art surgical equipment and building the ultimate plastic surgery center, the kind which satisfies the highest standards worldwide; Offering Patients precision and safety. Without these requirements it is not possible to understand the practice of plastic surgery.

Success in cosmetic surgery requires attention to detail, careful planning and decision making, followed by a skillfull execution in surgery, and an intensive post-operative follow up of every Patient.

For our patients from abroad, we have developed an specialized program to make travel for plastic surgery as simple and safe as possible.


Our Facilities

The passion and love for our work is something you can feel in every aspect of our practice, staff and clinic. Our goal has been creating a state of the art surgical facility according to highest standards worldwide for safety, asepsis, functionality and privacy; all this in a warm, relaxing, contemporary atmosphere.

The Clinic includes a surgical theatre, modern anesthesia equipment, recovery room with the possibility to stay overnight, if needed and a complete sterilization area. This Surgical Center is the first facility specially designed for aesthetic plastic surgery in the Country.

For those patients who prefer a hospital for surgery, Dr Gabriel Peralta has hospital privileges at CIMA San Jose Hospital and Hospital Clínica Bíblica.

Doctors:

Dr. Gabriel A. Peralta M, M.D.

Dr. Gabriel A. Peralta’s interest in plastic surgery begins early in his medical career, while he was doing his third year.

He has assisted several of most renowned plastic surgeons of his natural Santa Fe de Bogotá, Colombia since 1987.

In 1991, presented the exams of the Educational Commission for Foreign Medical Graduates (ECFMG) - American Board of Medicine and the Federal Licensing Examination - Pennsylvania (FLEX) approving them successfully.

Between 1992 and 1993 he works as assistant instructor of Anatomy in the Medical and Dental Schools at the Universidad El Bosque (formerly, Escuela Colombiana de Medicina).

Moved to San Jose Costa Rica where he did five years training in Plastic and Reconstructive surgery at the University of Costa Rica between 1994 and 1998.

In 1999 he travels to Guadalajara, Mexico for a one-year fellowship in Aesthetic Surgery with Dr. Jose Guerrero-Santos and Dr. Rafael Vergara Calleros.

In 2001 completed the certification to join the Physicians and Surgeons College of Costa Rica.


Magda Liz Peralta Mantilla, DDS

The sense of aesthetics and taste for Surgical Sciences came together since an early age, under the influence of her parents, prestigious Colombian dentists focusing their professional practice in Integral Advanced Dentistry.

In 1992, received her Dentistry’s Diploma at the Pontificia Universidad Javeriana in Santa Fe de Bogotá, Colombia.

During 1993, she received training at the San Juan de Dios Hospital in Bogotá on Aesthetic Surgery procedures. At the same time, did her private practice in Cosmetic destistry.

In 1995, began specialization studies at the P.U.J in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, receiving her Diploma in 1998 after an intensive residency at the Medicine and Dentistry Faculties.

In 1999, she directed her efforts to treatment of dentofacial anomalies at the Police Central Hospital in Bogotá, Colombia, under the supervision of Dr. Maria Costanza Pineda (distinguished Maxilofacial surgeon).

Later on, in 2001 she moves to San José, Costa Rica where she completes her training in Oral Implantology at the U.L.A.C.I.T ( Universidad Latinoamericana de Ciencia y Tecnologia ).

Dr. Magda Peralta has fulfilled the requirements of law as a Dentist and Maxillofacial surgeon of the Costa Rican College of Dental Surgeons, of which she is member since 2003.
