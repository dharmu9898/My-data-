MEZA DENTAL CARE,COSTA RICA

Welcome to Meza Dental Care
Providing excellence in Cosmetic Dentistry
Meza Dental Care, a Center for Cosmetic Dentistry in Costa Rica, specializes in providing first class cosmetic dental services to our patients. In our practice, we combine experience and a gentle touch to provide our patients with exceptional restorative and cosmetic dental care.
Did you know that statistics reveal that having confidence in your smile can lead to personal and professional success? Nowadays, and thanks to advancements in Cosmetic Dentistry, it is easier than ever to have the smile of your dreams.
At Meza Dental Care, we are committed to excellence and superior customer care with an emphasis on smile design and restoration. We employ the latest Cosmetic Dentistry treatments, including porcelain veneers, dental implants, porcelain crowns, tooth-colored fillings, tooth bonding and teeth whitening.
Your care and satisfaction are of paramount importance to us. It is our mission to ensure that you receive the best attention and the highest quality dental treatment available while experiencing unsurpassed comfort in a state-of-the-art dental facility.
Meza Dental Care provides a full range of Restorative and Cosmetic Dentistry procedures and techniques such as:

• Porcelain Veneers
• Porcelain Crowns and Bridges
• Smile Design and Restoration
• Dental Implants
• ZOOM™ Teeth Whitening
• Composite White Fillings
• Gum Surgery
• Root Canal Treatment
• Oral Rehabilitation
Our practice provides high quality cosmetic dentistry procedures employing the ultimate in technology, the finest materials, and of course the professionalism and experience of our dentists and specialists.
We strongly believe dentistry should be a gentle and positive experience for our patients. Our dedicated staff is at your disposal before, during and after your dental work to ensure an exceptional experience.

We also believe in providing our patients with all the information they need in order to make educated dental treatment choices. We work to involve our patients in an active program to prevent future oral diseases. We stand behind our stated commitments to high technology in dentistry and high quality dental care at affordable cost.
Whether you are looking to make slight changes to your smile or are in need of a full mouth restoration, let us show you how beautiful your smile can be. At Meza Dental Care, we are not only changing smiles, we are changing lives!

Meza Dental Care

As a result, not only we have achieved quality in our practice, but our patients receive warm, personalized care, ensuring that your experience with us is a positive one.
