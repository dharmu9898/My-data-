PRISMA COSMATIC DETISTRY, COSTA RICA

We offer a personalized approach to planning for wellness... stressing care and concern for individual choices. Our practice is more than just dentistry; we pay attention to all aspects of a healthy lifestyle and a holistic approach to well-being.


Our patients get optimum quality attention aimed at balanced wellness and whole body dentistry. In many instances, preventative treatments are recommended to round-out a complete dental health program.


The shape, color, and alignment of your teeth can be improved so you can have the smile you've always wanted. We use the most modern and advanced dental techniques, together with versatile materials that are stronger and longer-lasting than ever before.


Prisma Dental is located in one of the best neighbourhoods of San Jose. We moved to our new state-of-the-art facility in 2004. We are pleased to offer panoramic x-rays specifically for implants, cephalometric x-rays, and radioviosgraphy.


Our waiting area offers free internet to our patients as well as cable television with CNN. There are nice views of the not so distant mountains from both the waiting area and the examination rooms.


Cleanliness is very important to us and is apparent to our patients from the moment they enter the waiting area at Prisma Dental. All of our work areas are maintained in immaculate conditions.
