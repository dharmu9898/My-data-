Colina Dental

Colina Dental is located in San Rafael de Escazú and equipped with the latest technology; Digital x-rays, Oral cameras, Audio Visual equipment, Rotatory Endodontics Instruments, Iin Office Teeth Whitening (Zoom) etc. The establishment follows a strict medical practice and sterilization process.


The facility has a very pleasant and inviting atmosphere that guarantees 1st class service to its clients.


Established over 29 years back Colina Dental has been constantly improving the dental service and has been adding dental specialists. Their exclusive pain free Dental method will make you feel comfortable and assured.
