Dentavac is devoted to providing our patients with the best San Jose, Costa Rica dentistry available today. Our entire Costa Rica dentist staff here at Dentavac is an integrated group of highly trained individuals with well known DDS degrees. We fulfill all denture care needs from simple bridge work to the most amazing mouth transformations that can be imagined and it is all done with the most modern technology available today.


Our Costa Rica dental clinic of serious professionals proves that dentistry in Costa Rica leads the industry around the world.
