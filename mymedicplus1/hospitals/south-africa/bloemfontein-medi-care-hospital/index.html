Bloemfontein Medi Care Hospital

Bloemfontein Medi-Clinic is a multidisciplinary private hospital with a maximum capacity of 377 beds, located in Bloemfontein, Free State, South Africa.It is one of the hospitals in the Medi-Clinic group of private hospitals. Medi-Clinic is one of the largest private hospitals groups in Africa and currently owns over 40 private hospitals throughout South Africa and 2 in Namibia.

Bloemfontein Medi-Clinic offers the comfort of a city with the warmth and hospitality of a town and is conveniently situated within easy reach of the N1, the airport and the business centre, as well as other large commercial centres.
