Arwyp Medical Centre Private Hospital

Arwyp Medical Centre Private Hospital provides its customers with state of the art facilities as well as a comprehensive range of services to address the needs of every patient. The hospital’s competitively priced facilities are available to medical aid users as well as to private patients.


The attractively decorated hospital is a haven within the hustle and bustle around it. At Arwyp the emphasis is on making the patient feel as comfortable and well cared for as possible. All wards are fitted with colour televisions and telephones. Private wards have adjoining bathrooms.


Established in 1986, the hospital has exceeded all growth expectations and now occupies all 12 floors of its own superbly located complex as well as many of the surrounding buildings. The executive management is assisted by more than 700 members of staff all committed to providing the customer with complete health care.
