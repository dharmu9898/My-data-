KREATIV DENTAL CLINIC HUNGARY

Professional Dentist in Hungary
Welcome to the Kreativ Dental Clinic. Kreativ lead the way in advanced, high quality, affordable dentistry in Europe.

If you require cosmetic dentistry or dental implant treatment, you may wish to consider visting our World Class dental facility in Hungary.

Our patients reguarly save 50% to 70% compared to the quotes they receive in the UK.

 

If you are in need of Dental Implants, Crowns, Bridge Work, Veneers or Teeth Whitening at a price you can afford contact Kreativ Dental on 0845 0945 331.

Speak to our UK team who will be only too pleased to arrange your trip to see us.

As a Member of the EU, dental care in Hungary conforms to EU regulations. The Dentists who work at Kreativ have the highest qualifications obtained from Hungary's prestigious Dental Universities and have all worked abroad to gain international experience.

Our new, purpose built, state-of-the-art Surgery has now opened! Consisting of a fully equipped, high-tech Dental Surgery and Laboratory combined with a Training Centre and specially designed facilities for client's relaxation, it is the most modern and well equipped Dental Clinic in Europe.

As well as the considerable financial savings our patients can enjoy the opportunity of exploring the vibrant city of Budapest perhaps with a companion or family member.

Travelling abroad to for dental implants or cosmetic dentistry is simple. The professionalism and cost effectiveness of dental surgery there means that Budapest has virtually become the dental capital of Europe. You will save money, and in many cases, time.

Hungary was the first country to establish a dental university and has since built an enviable reputation as the European capital of dentistry. It is the top European destination for dental tourism. In 2007, more than 25,000 of us Brits travelled to Budapest for Dental Treatment. As one of the first companies to specialize in dental tourism, last year Kreativ opened a new, purpose-built surgery complex.

England is the most expensive country in Europe for dental treatment. Many patients either cannot afford private care or cannot find an NHS dentist.

"According to research, the average price for a dental implant in the UK is between £2000 and £2500, but at Kreativ it's £605.00 (GBP)."

Healthy teeth and good dental hygiene are an essential part of maintaining a good quality of life. Knowing you have a beautiful, healthy and natural looking smile also helps to give you confidence. Neglected, decayed and unhealthy teeth will affect your overall health and missing teeth that are not replaced will probably cause further damage to your jaw line.

Doctors:

Dr. Louis Patonay

Chief Surgeon in charge of the dental team.
Dr. Patonay first qualified in 1989 as a General Doctor from Semmelweis Medical University in Budapest, Hungary. He went on to become a general Dentist in 1994 finally qualifying as a Dental Surgeon at the University for Dental Surgery Budapest in 1999. Since then he has been working exclusively as a Dental Surgeon and his specialist field is implantology.


Dr. Peter Magos
Peter qualified from the University of Dentistry in Budapest, Hungary in 1994. He was awarded a two year Fellowship in Dental Surgery at the University of Dentistry in Vienna, Austria. Following several hospital positions in restorative cosmetic dentistry and oral-maxillo facial Surgery where he gained valuable surgical training, he joined our dental team in 1998. His special interests are Cosmetic Dentistry and Periodontology.


Dr. Iván Solymosi
Iván qualified from Semmelweis University in Budapest, Hungary in 1994. In 1998 he joined Kreativ Dental after gaining three years experience in private practice. He has earned a distinction in a Master of Science degree in endodontics. Endodontic, or root treatment is required if a tooth becomes infected through decay or injury. It often avoids the need to remove a tooth. His special interests are Endodontics and Cosmetic Dentistry.


Dr. Viktor Ceglédi
From 1989 to 1994 Viktor attended the University of Dentistry in Budapest, Hungary. From 1994 to 1998 he practiced in a private dental surgery in Stuttgart, Germany where he carried out restorative dentistry and periodontology (treatment and cure of gum disease). In 1998 he was appointed dental coordinator of Kreativ Dental Surgery. His special interests are Advanced Restorative and Cosmetic Dentistry.
