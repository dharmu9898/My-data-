Doctor Credentials

Dr. Turner - DDS MAGD

Dr. Turner grew up in Albuquerque, New Mexico and received his BA in history from the University of New Mexico in 1969. After five years in diesel submarines, the US Navy sent him to dental school at University of Missouri-Kansas City School of Dentistry, graduating in 1978. He received his MBA at National University in 1984. The Navy transferred him to Oak Harbor in 1986.

He set up practice in 1990 after 21 years in the military. Dr Turner is a member of the Washington State Dental Society, American Dental Association, Academy of General Dentistry, Academy of Laser Dentistry, Mt. Baker Chapter of the Seattle Study Club.
He received his Mastership status in Academy of General Dentistry in 1999. He is past president of the Kiwanis Club of Oak Harbor and active with Oak Harbor's Chamber of Commerce, and Whidbey Whirles Square Dance Club.
