Lenox Hill Hospital


Lenox Hill Hospital, a 652-bed, acute care hospital located on Manhattan's Upper East Side, has earned a national reputation for outstanding patient care and innovative medical and surgical treatments. The hospital is particularly well known for excellence in internal medicine, cardiovascular disease, orthopedics, sports medicine, otolaryngology/head and neck surgery, and maternal/child health.

Mission:
The mission of Lenox Hill Hospital and Manhattan Eye, Ear & Throat Hospital is to deliver outstanding healthcare with compassion and respect, to promote wellness in our communities, and to advance the field of medicine through education and research.
Our Vision
Lenox Hill Hospital and Manhattan Eye, Ear & Throat Hospital seek excellence in all endeavors, aspiring to provide premier services to a diverse patient population and to nurture an environment in which health care professionals and staff can learn and thrive.
Our Values
The values that guide Lenox Hill Hospital and Manhattan Eye, Ear & Throat Hospital in pursuit of our mission are:

  • To provide exceptional care to patients and their families
  • To acknowledge the dignity and individuality of every patient and staff member
  • To respect patient privacy and confidentiality
  • To communicate openly and effectively with patients and staff
  • To maintain the highest standards of integrity and ethical conduct
  • To foster teamwork and accountability at all levels
