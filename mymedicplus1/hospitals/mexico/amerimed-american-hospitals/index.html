Amerimed Hospitals

AmeriMed is a network of medical facilities in Mexico that provide full medical services based upon U.S. standards of health care.

We offer a full range of emergency and routine services. Our facilities are staffed 24 hours a day with bilingual, in-house physicians who are specialists in emergency medicine. Our nurses provide total quality care, supported by the latest in technology.

AmeriMed is a recognized leader and preferred professional provider of optimal medical service and care for residents and travelers, both domestic and International.

Our first priority is to provide our patients the medical care they deserve and expect. Sunbirds and natives alike have discovered that AmeriMed Hospitals are an ideal place for walk-in continuing care.

We approach long term care as we do emergency care: with compassion, accurate diagnoses and the proper treatment.
Well baby visits, insulin titration, chemotherapy, dialysis, respiratory care, Coumadin titration, or any chronic condition needing multiple visits are all available at AmeriMed.

Appointments can be scheduled with the specialist of your choice by calling the AmeriMed Hospital and scheduling with our friendly, bilingual representative

Cardiac Catheterization in Cabo

Patient: Robert Moore
Diagnoses: Unstable angina
Treatment: Angioplasty plus stenting
May 18, 2006
To whom it may concern:
Words cannot adequately describe how traumatic it is to be in a foreign country and experience having severe chest pain where you know already that you have two stents and are taking 8 medications daily to ward off further heart problems. It was a blessing to have the taxi pull up to AmeriMed and to see the words “American Hospital”. From the moment we rolled in through the doors until now as I prepare to leave, my family and I were treated with extremely competent care and compassion. I was seen immediately and also very quickly given medicine that alleviate my pain and calm me. The doctors took the time to learn mi cardiac history and called my doctor in the USA to confer whit them. I really appreciate them taking the extra time to do this. While my initial choice would have been to have my own cardiologist care for me, the cost of air ambulance was prohibitive. However, I am glad that we chose to stay and am very grateful that Dr. Montes Marin was able locate the doctor who come to do my procedure.
All of the medical staff far exceeded my expectations. I can truly say that I feel my care here was equal to or better than the care I received in the US during my stay in the hospital there. We experienced very little problems with language barriers as most of the staff are competent in English even though we don’t speak or understand Spanish.
The only area of concern has been the payment of account. Because I am covered under my employers’ insurance which is an excellent policy (Duke University Medical Center) we never have to prepay any charges. This was new for us and caused some stress until we were able to get word from the insurance company that this was procedure here and that they would assist us with paperwork when we got back home. I suggest that the business office assist patients make those connections with insurance companies earlier in the process or that the hospital works towards being able to direct bill insurance electronically so that patients only pay a portion while in Mexico.
Overall, were had and excellent experience, and I am 100% satisfied with the care and service I received at AmeriMed.
