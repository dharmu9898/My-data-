Clinica Sao Vicente

Largest sector at Clínica São Vicente, roughly 90% of all patients go through the Admissions Unit. The unit has rooms, suites, Cellular Therapy and Burned Unit. The rooms are distributed through three stories and equipped with Oxygen net and emergency material – one crash cart and one defibrillator per story.

This unit stitches together doctors, nurses and administrative personnel attending clinical, surgical and oncological of medium complexity level patients. It is located on the first, second and third floors to integrate al the other services in the clinic.

As in the closed units, the Admission Unit at Clínica São Vicente counts on a team of doctors, nurses and technicians on the floors quickening the exchange of information and need of procedures.

Today, the Clínica São Vicente Surgical Centre conducts all types of interventions, from the most complex made by some of the greatest surgeons in Brazil. Professionals including nurses, technical and administrative staff and the hotel staff take rounds 24 hours per day in the Surgical Centre. There are five rooms with anaesthesia carts, complete monitoring systems (oximetry, electrocardiograms, non-invasive pressure, invasive pressure modules, telethermometer, nerve stimulator and BIS monitors), surgical tables, auxiliary tables, light focus and electrical scalpel. Approximately 300 surgeries are conducted by month, both elective and emergency.
