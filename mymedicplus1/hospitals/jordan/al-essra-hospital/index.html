Al -Essra Hospital

Al -Essra Hospital is one of the modern hospitals in Jordan which includes all the medical specialist with 160 beds, that has the most development equipments and medical machines and most qualified specialist doctors. The hospital continue advancements after operating all departments and started offering a complete set of medical services. We had to distinguished ourselves from others from quality of service, degree of proficiency, and caring for our patients, that is when we developed and raised the motto "Distinguished by caring for you" hoping to reach distinguished medical services. In the shadow of support and encouragement we receive from patients, during 2004 we developed IVF, Prematurely Baby's, Eyes surgery departments as well as increase the capacity of Kidneys dialysis units. Despite the development never stopped, we keep introducing new services and devices like Bone Density Scan (QDR), Breast Mammogram, device to break kidneys stones, and updating the medical lab devices.

2005 is considered a new reborn for the hospital regarding growth and development, due to the increased growth numbers and volume of work. The sales numbers are growing which means that we are gaining market share and our patients are happy with our services and confident in our doctors and their capabilities. The number of Arab patients who received treatment in our hospital has increased during the last months.

Medical Department


Surgery Dept

The surgery department is considered one of the most important departments in any hospital, since it is where all types of medical surgery conducted (small to large) to save patients, especially those resulting from emergency situations, maturnity, or others. Al-Essra Hospital Surgery department consists of anesthesia physicians, resident surgens, surgery nurses, anesthesia technicians, and recovery nurses. Also, the department contains all the necessary equipments to conduct operations including anesthesia and modern medical surveillance equipments. In addition the hospital keeps updating and modifing the equipments as the technology evolves. The department have:

Five Operation rooms.
One Operation room in the Maturnity Department.
One Operation room in the Emergency Department.
All these rooms are equiped with the necessary devices to conduct the necessary operations required and depending on the need and requirements of the situation.


Emergency

The emergency department is one of the vitally important sections in Al-Essra Hospital reflecting the true picture of the hospital. The department operates round the clock providing medical and therapeutic services to patients. It comprises of highly qualified doctors, nursing, and administrative staff working efficiently to provide high quality service to patients and exceed their expectation. This department which we cherishe at Al-Essra is consedered one of the most advanced, sophisticated and distinctive emergency departments in Jordan at both public and private level. It contains all the necessary services a patients might need regardless of the condition or injury such as heart and lungs recovery room, surgery room for small operations, maturnity room, orthepidic room, pharmacy, accounting, admission, two halls for men, women and childrens, and a waiting room at the entrance. In addition, we at Al-Essra cherish our distinguished medical staff and the emargency department mainly has highly qualified, certified, and specialty doctors as well as general general practitioners with long experience. Also, the department has access to resident specialists doctors in the hospital who follow-up on the patients. At Al-Essra Hospital we are always looking for new services and ways to best serve our patients at the country level. Maternity

The maturnity department was the first to be equiped and staffed while other departments was still under preparation. The department consists of three maturnity rooms, two observation rooms, and a recovery room equipped with the latest medical devices. In addition, it includes fourteen rest rooms after childbirth divided into four categories as follow:

Two Suites.
Seven rooms first class.
Three room second class.
Two rooms thired class.
The department is distinguished by introducing the concept of integrated birth in the same place so as performing the whole birth process in one room. Finaly, the department have a reception room equiped with a modern sonar for pregnant women and operations room for a Caesarean section.


Prematurely

We started the operation of the prematurity department since the begining of the hospital operation in 2001. We have chosen a suitable location on the second floor near the children's section and designed for the reception of newly born children prematurly with need for vigorous or moderate medical care.
The department is equiped with many modern devices specializing in caring for prematurly newborns and their critical cases, and weight. The most important device in this department is the incubators and the department is equiped with ten of these incubators, six breathing apparatus, optical processing devices and surveillance equipment to measure heart rate and oxygen. Also, the department is staffed around the clock with highly qualified specialty doctors and highly expreience nurses.
