Al Najah Hospital

We Concentrate on subspecialties through the main specialicities and we provide our Clients with highly specialized medical care. We believe in playing an effective role in the society serving and development. We held many symposiums for specialists and public to support the continuous medical education.


We look forward for serving Jordanian society and Jordanian women and try our best to develop our medical services to meet the needs and expectations of the clients.


Our Aims

Our message is to offer the best Medical care in Jordan ,we concentrate on the subspecialties and we are special in Gynaecology & Obstetrics and Fertility.
Our Aim is to meet the needs and expectations of our patients:

  • We work as a team.
  • We cooperate with foreign experts.
  • We use the new technology.
  • We develop a professional and flexible administration.
  • We employ a well trained highly qualified medical a nursing staff.

The Work Philosophy
  • Patients First.
  • With Help of the God we will care for you.
  • Creating a good atmosphere for work.
  • Care for values and morals of occupation.
  • Serving the Society through our work.
  • Continuous education and training of the medical and nursing staff.
