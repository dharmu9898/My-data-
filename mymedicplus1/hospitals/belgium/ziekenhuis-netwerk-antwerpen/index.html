ZNA (ZiekenhuisNetwerk Antwerpen) is Belgium’s leading health care provider offering affordable and high quality healthcare in the Antwerp metropolitan area and beyond. ZNA was established in 2004 as an independent management structure for the former public hospitals of the city of Antwerp. ZNA is comprised of 3 general hospitals, 8 day centers and 6 specialized care centers (children, psychiatry and geriatrics). ZNA employs 6,000 people of which are 600 medical doctors and 2,650 nurses, in all caring for 6,000 patients every day
