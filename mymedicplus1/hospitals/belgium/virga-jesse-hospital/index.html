Friendly, safe and professional care

The Virga Jesse Hospital Hasselt is a general public hospital in addition to all traditional medical specialties also has a wide range of top-level clinical services. This focus on a large part of the province of Limburg. The hospital has 1,800 employees, 165 doctors and 589 beds.


Patient Central

Within the internal functioning of the hospital the patient as much heart. We want a quality medical care and provide permanent and innovate. Patient friendliness and patient safety are key values in the hospital management. With numerous hospitals and care providers from the region are intense collaborations.


People-friendly company

The Virga Jesse Hospital attaches great importance to the development of a people-friendly and innovative staff. Received before the first non-profit price of "people-friendly company". Besides the quality is also highly integrated into the daily operation. It was awarded the first prize for "excellence in hospital management.. The hospital took in February 2008 as the first Belgian hospital NIAZ-quality.


Sub specialization

In 2006 our hospital has completed an intention to merge with neighboring Salvator St.-Ursula-Hospital. This should lead to a merger in 1001 of hospital beds where sufficient room for further sub specialization and the development of top-level clinical services. The scale must allow the hospital staff and resources as efficiently as possible way.
