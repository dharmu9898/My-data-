Belhoul European Hospital

Belhoul European Hospital was established in 1996 as the first day care hospital in the UAE providing comprehensive medical facilities through its 19 outpatient clinics. The operating theatre and diagnostic department is supported by an accomplished team of highly qualified, specialized doctors world wide.
Integration
Belhoul European Hospital is a collection of medical specialties, simultaneously offering advanced diagnostic technology and outstanding service.

Comprehensive
We provide over 19 clinics of specialist expertise under one roof to deliver the highest level of care to our patients.
