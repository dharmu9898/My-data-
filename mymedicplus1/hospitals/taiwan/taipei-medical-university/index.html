Taipei Medical University

Taipei Medical University Shuang-Ho Hospital is the first Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT) project (construction/operation/management) undertaken by Taipei Medical University as commissioned by Department of Health, Executive Yuan, R.O.C. (Taiwan). 1,580 sickbeds are available at Shuang-Ho Hospital, the largest hospital in Taipei County. Besides, Shuang-Ho Hospital, Taipei Medical University Hospital, and Taipei Medical University Wan-Fang Hospital become a Medical Health Care Triangle in Taipei area with a total capacity of 3,000 sickbeds. This Medical Health Care Triangle and Taipei Medical University are the strong supports to each other.
