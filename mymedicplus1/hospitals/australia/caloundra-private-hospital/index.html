Caloundra Private Hospital

Caloundra Private Hospital was known as Andrea Ahern Private Hospital Owned and operated by Ramsay Health Care, Australia's largest and most respected operator of private hospitals, Caloundra Private Hospital is located at the southern end of the Sunshine Coast in peaceful surroundings only minutes from the beach. The hospital has 64 beds and offers a range of services including surgical, medical, renal dialysis, day surgery and diagnostic services. The theatre suite includes 3 modern operating theatres plus a 5 bed recovery unit.
The Renal Dialysis Unit consists of 5 renal dialysis chairs; the Day Surgery Unit consists of a procedure room and 6 chairs; the Surgical and Medical Units consists of 32 beds each with 4 telemetry beds Surgical services include gynaecology, orthopaedics, gastro-enterology, plastic and reconstructive, general, laparoscopic and colo-rectal surgery. Medical services include general, respiratory medicine, palliative care and renal dialysis.
Caloundra Private has established an excellent reputation in the community for its commitment to customer service, staff satisfaction and clinical outcomes.

Highlights

MHistory:

For many years, Caloundra Private Hospital was known as Andrea Ahern Private Hospital – named after former Queensland Premier Mike Ahern’s wife. Built in 1984 by the Moran Health Group, the Hospital comprised 40 acute medical and surgical beds and one operating theatre.
Later purchased by MBF Australia in 1986, the Hospital underwent major extensions and renovations and a master plan was drafted for future possible development. In 1992 a two-stage redevelopment of the operating theatre was completed, wards were totally refurbished and the hospital grounds re-landscaped. Two years later, the Hospital’s Day Surgery Unit opened.
In 1996, an additional 24 beds were added – bringing the total to 64 beds evenly split between medical and surgical wards. A Renal Dialysis Unit opened in September 2003. The Hospital has been through several changes in ownership over the years – becoming part of the Ramsay Health Care network in 2005. Ramsay Health Care owns and operates around 70 private hospitals across Australia – making it the country’s largest and most respected provider of private hospital services plus an international presence in countries such as the United Kingdom and Indonesia.
Today, Caloundra Private Hospital is delivering on its solid healthcare foundations and evolving in response to the changing needs of the community it serves.


Mission & Vision

Caloundra Private Hospital strives to be the healthcare provider of choice for payers, health professionals and the community on the Sunshine Coast, by providing consistent excellence in proven and effective clinical outcomes.


Clinical Services:
Day Surgery

Caloundra Private Hospital’s purpose-built day surgery is fully equipped to conduct a broad range of diagnostic procedures – including endoscopy and minor surgical procedures. Day surgery facilities include procedure rooms, pre-operative and recovery beds and a dedicated patient recliner lounge area to facilitate recovery in comfort.


Renal Dialysis

Caloundra Private Hospital’s five-chair Renal Dialysis Unit is fully equipped with the latest in dialysis technology. Staffed by experienced, specialist renal nurses and consulting Nephrologists, the Renal Dialysis Unit provides Haemodialysis for patients with kidney disease and end stage renal failure. The Unit prides itself on providing holistic, patient-focused care for local patients and those holidaying on the Sunshine Coast from areas throughout Australia in a comfortable environment.


Orthopaedics

Caloundra Private Hospital operates a comprehensive general orthopaedic surgical service with a focus on joint replacement surgery of the hip and knee, and foot, hand and shoulder surgery. A wide range of orthopaedic surgeons attend the Hospital as Visiting Medical Officers.


Gastroenterology

A comprehensive range of gastroenterology services are available at Caloundra Private Hospital.


Urology

Caloundra Private Hospital offers patients a comprehensive urology service - including prostate surgery. A Urodynamic Service, incorporating operative and inpatient care is provided at the Hospital. This is a unique service on the Sunshine Coast.


General Surgery

Caloundra Private Hospital’s General Surgery Unit incorporates all aspects of general surgery including upper gastro-intestinal, pancreaticobiliary, colorectal and endocrine surgery. Our Hospital offers well equipped, modern operating theatres and prides itself on an outstanding record in the area of infection control.


Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery

Caloundra Private Hospital offers patients access to a comprehensive range of plastic and reconstructive services including liposuction, breast reduction and reconstruction and the removal of lesions.


Respiratory Medicine

Caloundra Private Hospital has a strong focus on respiratory medicine - providing services for patients with chronic airway disease, lung cancer and other respiratory conditions.
