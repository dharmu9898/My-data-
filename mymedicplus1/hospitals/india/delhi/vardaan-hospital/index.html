Vardaan Hospital

Vardaan Hospital was set up in 1995 with the aim of providing quality healthcare services. Our endeavour is to help people be healthy so that they can be productive members of society. We strive to provide the same quality of healthcare services to all strata of society.


The hospital was set up and is managed by Dr. R. K. Khandelwal and Dr. Rekha Khandelwal. They both have more than 20 years of experience in their respective fields of specialisation. Other consultants available at the hospital also have vast experience in their fields.

Highlights


Vardaan is a multi-specialty hospital in the heart of New Delhi. The facilities available at Vardaan are:
  • Comfortable spacious rooms including Deluxe A/C rooms, Single A/C rooms and General Ward.
  • Fully equipped operation theatre, with laparoscopy, eye surgery and ENT surgery facilities.
  • Separate fully equipped labour room.
  • 24 hour emergency services available, with a qualified doctor available round-the-clock.
  • In house Ultrasound, X-ray, ECG, pathology and microbiology facilities available
