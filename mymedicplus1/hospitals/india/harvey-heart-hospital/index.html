Harvey Heart Hospital

Harvey Heart Hospital is a clinical care facility housing 25-30 beds, including 12 critical care beds, solely dedicated to Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery and ancillary services with out-patient areas and clinics. It has the potential and option in increase the bed strength to 50-60 beds in the immediate future. The Hospital provides clinical care for patients with heart disorders and includes state of the art diagnostic, therapeutic, rehabilitative and preventive facilities. Harvey Heart Hospital is located at a modern custom built hospital facility in the heart of Chennai, the Vasanthi Medical Centre.
