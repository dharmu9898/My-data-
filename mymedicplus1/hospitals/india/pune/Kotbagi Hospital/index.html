Kotbagi Hospital

Kotbagi hospital was established in 1967 and subsequently migrated into a new place in 1991 located at the mid of Pune municipal corporation and Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal corporation in Aundh area in city of Pune which is 100 miles away from Mumbai.
Kotbagi hospital is multispeciality, superspeciality hospital in aundh pune having well equipped ot, intensive care unit, diagnostic dept, luxury rooms with all facilities, 24 hours drug stores that caters to the needs almost all speciality of the medical kingdom.

  • Recognized by CGHS, CSMA, ECHS (for ex servicemen)
  • Recognized by many insurance companies
  • Recognized by many industries
  • Authorized MTP center
  • Chirayu health card (new scheme for family members)

Highlights

Vision:
We aspire to become the comprehensive, accessible, integrated health care delivery system recognized for advanced clinical care, quality excellent service, cost effectiveness and commitment to improving the health status of the communities we serve.

Mission:
To be an innovative, publicly accountable and comprehensive health care organization To improve the health status of the diverse community we serve through excellence in patient care.

Structure:
With the help of a managed care, physician integration and corporate services infrastructure, the system supports each network's efforts to provide the highest quality health care to communities

Our helpful spirit also extends to our commitment to give back to the community. Kotbagi hospital has also made its contribution to charity care in the region, helping families who need financial assistance.
