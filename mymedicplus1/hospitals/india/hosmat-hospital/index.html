Hosmat Hospital

Hospital for Orthopaedics, Sports Medicine, Arthritis, Accident-Trauma, Hosmat Arthritis & Joint Replacement Center (HAJRC) ,Hosmat Institute of Neurosciences (HINS) and Hosmat Institute of Spine Problems (HISP) is a super major speciality hospital of 350 beds in Bangalore, India, ideally located in central Bangalore about 0.5 kilometers from Brigade Road or MG Road. It is the first comprehensive hospital in India specializing in Orthopaedics, Sports medicine, Arthritis, Accident-Trauma and Neurosciences, and is undergoing an expansion phase to 500 beds, making it the largest speciality hospital of its kind in Asia.


A modern and spacious building has been designed and constructed for Orthopaedic and Neuro patients, the latest state of the art equipment in all disciplines of Orthopaedics, Neuro surgery, Neurology, Accident - Trauma and Sports Medicine have been installed. Highly qualified and experienced medical consultants are the mainstay of the hospital.


In the past ten years, HOSMAT has gained a reputation of being one of the best hospitals in orthopaedics and its subspecialities,joint replacement, arthroscopy, children's ortho, hand, spine, arthritis, Ilizarov, sports medicine and reconstructive surgery, Neurosurgery and Neurology making it the first Orthopaedic and Joint Replacement Hospital to get ISO9002 - TUV Certified.


The various surgeries and facilities are: General Orthopaedics Surgeries,Medical and surgical treatment of arthritis, Joint replacements of the knee, hip, shoulder and elbow, Fibreoptic arthroscopy of various joints, treatment of fractures, non-union and malunion of fractures, ligament injuries, reconstructive surgeries, specialized Ilizarov surgery (limb lengthening procedure), Correction of deformities, Spinal surgeries (disc problems, spondylitis, pinched nerves, spine fractures, deformities, scholiosis and tumours), Paediatric Orthopaedics and Industrial injuries.
