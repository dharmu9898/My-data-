Cumballa Hill Hospital and Heart Institute

Located in South Mumbai and having an impeccable record of over 30 years of dedicated services to the community, Cumballa Hill Hospital has always strived to keep abreast with the latest developments in the world of medicine.

Dr.K.R.Shetty, the founder of Cumballa Hill Hospital, is a cardiac surgeon of international repute. In recognition of his work as a pioneer of numerous cardiac surgery procedures the Indian Association of Cardiovascular and Thoracic Surgeons has bestowed upon him the "Life Time Achievement Award."

At the onset of our venture to make Cumballa Hill Hospital one of the best hospital in Mumbai we chose cardiology as our area of specialization. Having achieved considerable success in this area we have started catering to other specialities as well.

With our promise to keep abreast with the latest advances in medicine we have acquired state-of-the-art technology in Cardiology, Cardiac Surgeries and Joint Replacement Surgeries.

Our goal is to establish a superspeciality center for excellent quality health care with a human touch.


Future Plans

Cumballa Hill Hospital has acquired the adjacent property and now is in the process of building approx. 220 bedded multispeciality hospital with a helipad facility for Air transport. Our aim is to build one of its kind superspeciality hospital in south Mumbai extending excellent services with human touch to its patients. With Gods grace and blessing of all the loyal patients Dr. K. R. Shetty, Chairman and Medical Director, Cumballa Hill Hospital will be able to soon realize his dreams.

Doctors:

Dr.K.R.Shetty spent 15 years in USA where he acquired both his Undergraduate and Postgraduate medical education, and then attained the position of Assistant Professor of Cardiovascular and Thoracic Surgery at the prestigious University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia. On his return to India in 1969, he was appointed as Cardiovascular and Thoracic Surgeon to the J.J.Group of Hospitals and Grant Medical College, Mumbai from where he retired as Director Professor of Cardiovascular surgery. He has presided over the first World Conference on Open Heart Surgery in 1985 and also the second World Conference on Open Heart Surgery in 1991, both held in Mumbai. He is one of the pioneers in coronary bypass surgery and was the first one to perform triple coronary bypass surgery in India in 1977. He was elected as the president of the Asian Congress of Thoracic and Cordiovascular Surgery held at Bangkok in 2001.


With his vast experience that he has in medical education, Dr.Shetty has been nominated to the Medical Council of India as a member and also to the postgraduate committee of the Medical Council of India, by the Government of India.
