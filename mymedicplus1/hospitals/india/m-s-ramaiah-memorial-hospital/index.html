M S Ramaiah Memorial Hospital

The most fundamental Principle in Medicine is Love. This hospital was built to ensure Love and care for people with pain and suffering. The basic philosophy of the hospital is care, concern and commitment to the patient. This institution believes that Doctors, Hospital and Medical Technology exist for the sake of the patient and for healing of the sick.


It was in September 1985, the M.S.Ramaiah Hospital was conceptualized and it became a reality exactly seven months later. The hospital was the dream of the late Founder Chairman M.S.Ramaiah who believed that if the hospital cannot help the many who are poor in our society, there is no purpose in helping the few who are rich. The hospital with a built-in area of 2,00,000 sq.ft. stands today as a true testimony of what one man can do, if he dares to dream. The hospital incorporates a complete range of state-of-the-art clinical equipment, diagnostic facilities and amenities comparable to the best in the world.

Highlights

Services Offered

The hospital caters to a large cross section of people, irrespective of their economic status. It has competent and experienced doctors who are ably supported by skilled and dedicated teams of nursing and other Paramedical staff. The range of services offered include medical and surgical specialties such as: General Medicine, General Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Pediatrics, Dermatology, Psychiatry, ENT, Ophthalmology, Orthopedics and Radiology.

Specialty Services

The basic services are supplemented by super-specialties like Cardiology, Cardiothoracic Surgery, Pulmonology, Gastroenterology, Clinical Immunology, Neonatology, Endocrinology, Nephrology, Urology, Neurology, Neuro-surgery, Vascular and Endovascular Surgery, Pediatric Surgery and Plastic surgery.

In-Patient Facilities

The hospital has options of semi private and private wards, which have 2 to 3 and single bed accommodation respectively. All the rooms are well ventilated, spacious, well lit and extra care is taken to maintain cleanliness and hygiene. There are 14 major operating theatres ,which are fully equipped for all surgeries including specialized Neurosurgery and Cardiothoracic surgery.
Multi-disciplinary intensive care units managed by highly qualified doctors round the clock offer complimentary support. All major specialty services are available for 24 hours. Two fully equipped ambulances are available with trained Para-medical staff to deal with emergencies.

Laboratories

The hospital has a centralized Diagnostic Laboratory for Biochemistry, Microbiology, Pathology, RIA Laboratory, Clinical immunology etc. The licensed Blood Bank has facilities for blood component separation and it works round the clock on all days.
