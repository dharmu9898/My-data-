Fortis Escorts Hospital, Jaipur

Fortis Escorts Hospital, Jaipur (FEHJ) is the first amongst the proposed multi super speciality hospitals to be set up in Rajasthan, with the mission to bring quality medical care at doorstep. FEHJ is a “Multi Super-Speciality” Hospital with major focus on super specialties of Cardiac Sciences, Neurosciences, Renal Sciences and GI Diseases backed up by a wide range of specialties. Our services are the most comprehensive available in Jaipur, all under one roof, with treatment supported by state-of-the-art facilities and systems.


FEHJ will establish itself as an institution that provides world - class healthcare with a high focus on medical excellence, compassionate patient care and health education on preventive measures.


At FEHJ one would experience the most comprehensive medical treatment across a wide range of specialties. A team of highly experienced physicians and surgeons, nurses, technicians & healthcare professions would provide treatment in all the specialties.


Ensuring quality medical treatment at FEHJ are the most comprehensive facilities available which include high end diagnostic equipments like 64 slice CT Scan machine, Touch Screen Monitors, world-class Dialysis facilities and a Flat Panel Cath Lab, to name a few.


There are specialized ICUs like the CCU Surgical ICU [SICU], Medical ICU [MICU] Neonatal ICU [NICU]. An expert intensivist is available around-the-clock in the ICUs to ensure that help is always at hand. The ICUs are equipped with the most advanced facilities:


Modular monitors, which measure the blood oxygen, ECG, Temperature, Respiration, Bispectral Index, EEG, Cardiac Output, Blood Pressure, Invasive Blood Pressure constantly and display them at a Central Station.


Adequate number of ventilators to handle simultaneous emergencies.


The attention of the highest grade is ensured by having a ratio of one nurse for every ICU patient. While the state-of-the-art monitoring allows the doctors to monitor all the vital parameters from anywhere in the hospital.


Operation Theatre

The Fortis Escorts Hospital, Jaipur has 5 of the most advanced Operation Theatres in India. The state-of-the-art Operation Theatres are edgeless and with Laminar air flow to eliminate the chances of any infection. The operation theatres are equipped with high-tech facilities like:


ALM Lights with camera which can record and broadcast the operation for archival purposes or remote monitoring


High-end endoscopic, arthroscopic and spinal discectomy equipment for advanced and complicated procedures


Imaging and Diagnostics

We firmly believe that the more accurate the diagnostics, the better the treatment. To achieve this we have the most advanced equipment to ensure the best possible accuracy:


  • Flat Panel Cath lab
  • 64 slice CT Scan
  • 500mA Image Intensifier TV supplemented by digital CR reader X-Ray
  • Mobile X-Rays
  • C-Arm with Digital Subtraction Angiography
  • E Ultrasound machine with cardiac probe
  • Mammography
  • Non Invasive Cardiac Investigation with TMT, ECHO, Holter Recorder and Monitor and ECG
  • One of the most Advanced Pathology Labs in the country

India’s largest Pathology laboratory SRL Ranbaxy (NABL & CAP accredited lab, maintaining best & advanced standards) manages our pathology services including Biochemistry, microbiology and histopathology. A range of over 3000 tests is offered, from routine to highly specialized ones. The result is cutting-edge accuracy and speed in investigation, enabled by the high- tech procedures and state-of-the-art equipment.
