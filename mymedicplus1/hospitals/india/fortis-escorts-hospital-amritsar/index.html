Fortis Escorts Hospital, Amritsar

The Fortis Escorts Hospital, Amritsar (FEHA) is brought to you by India's fastest growing Healthcare Group - FORTIS. In delivering healthcare, the organization is inspired by its founder's vision to "create a world-class integrated healthcare delivery system in India, entailing the finest medical skills combined with compassionate patient care".


With Fortis Escorts Hospital, the arrival of world-class medical care in Amritsar is a reality. Medical excellence redefined not just for patients in the Holy City, but all over northern India especially Punjab region.


Fortis Escorts Hospital is a Multi-Super Specialty Hospital with Cardiac Sciences, Neuro Sciences, Ortho & Joint Replacement, Renal Sciences, Gastro sciences as super specialties, backed by a range of Multi-specialties. We have a faculty of highly experienced physicians & surgeons who provide treatment across the wide range of specialties.


We bring a special emphasis to critical care that's so crucial to a patient's recovery, through advanced dedicated ICUs for Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery supported by a General ICU catering to trauma, and various other specialties. And, most significantly, you will experience a patient sensitive approach to medical care that is part of our ethos.
