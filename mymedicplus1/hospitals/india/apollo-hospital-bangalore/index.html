Highlights


Surgical Infrastructure

Attention to surgical infrastructure goes far beyond the obvious. A completely sterile environment during and after surgery is ensured with the help of the latest technologies. From airflow systems to surgical equipment, world standards in surgical protocol are followed to eliminate even minimal chances of infection. Navigation systems with complete orthopaedic and neuro modules and on-time cardiac output monitoring system are also available.

Cath Lab

Cath Lab with 3D CA technology that minimizes the quantity of contrast dye used in a single injection, is available for the first time in India.


Dialysis Unit

10 State-of-the-art stations with both hi-flux & low-flux transfusion compatibility, advanced round-the-clock renal service for all age groups and one of the best Reverse Osmosis water treatment plants for ultra pure water are some of the unique features in the Dialysis unit.
