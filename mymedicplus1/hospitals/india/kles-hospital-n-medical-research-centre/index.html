KLES Hospital & Medical Research Centre

A qualitative change in the medical scenario of Northern Karnataka was ushered in 1996 with commissioning of the new 1500 - bedded K.L.E.S. Hospital & Medical Research Centre at Belgaum. Meticulously planned for the 21 "century, the hospital has state - of - the - art facilities catering to all disciplines of medicine. The hospital has in a very short time introduced several new services that were hitherto unheard of in the region, including Cardiology, Cardiac Surgery, Lithotripsy, Gamma Camera etc.


K.L.E.S. Hospital & Medical Research Centrehas exploded the myth that large. multispeciality hospital cannot be set up in small town like Belgaum, thus breaking the monopoly of the metropolitan cities in this respect. The hospital is the most comprehensive medical centre in the entire region with a gamut of facilities incorporating the latest developments in the field of medical technology, bringing Belgaum on par, or even better than most hospitals in the country. In fact, K.L.E.S. Hospital claims that its facilities and services are comparable with the best hospital around the world.
